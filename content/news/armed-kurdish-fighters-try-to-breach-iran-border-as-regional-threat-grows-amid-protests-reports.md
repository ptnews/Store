<p>Armed Kurdish separatist groups tried to cross into Iran <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/world-regions/iraq" target="_blank" rel="noopener">from Iraq</a> in recent days, stoking fears that the country’s spiraling unrest has attracted dangerous foreign militants who could destabilize the wider region, according to reports.</p><p>Iranian officials said the attempted breach came amid a sweeping crackdown on nationwide protests against the country’s regime, with Iran’s Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps (IRGC) leading the response, <a href="https://www.reuters.com/world/middle-east/armed-kurdish-groups-sought-cross-into-iran-iraq-sources-say-2026-01-14/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">Reuters reported</a>.</p><p>The Tasnim News Agency also reported armed militia groups operating in Iraq crossed the border in western and northwestern Iran, according to <a href="https://www.middleeastmonitor.com/20260109-iran-accuses-kurdish-militants-from-iraq-of-crossing-border-to-fuel-protests/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">Middle East Monitor.</a></p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/top-iranian-official-downplays-death-toll-blames-israeli-plot-us-considers-strikes" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>TOP IRANIAN OFFICIAL DOWNPLAYS DEATH TOLL, BLAMES 'ISRAELI PLOT' AS US CONSIDERS STRIKES</strong></a></p><p>Reuters had reported that three sources, including a senior Iranian official, said <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/world-regions/middle-east" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Turkey’s intelligence agency</a>, known as MIT, warned the IRGC that Kurdish fighters were trying to cross the Iran-Iraq border.</p><p>The Iranian official said clashes also broke out after the attempt to cross and accused the fighters of trying to exploit the unrest and create further instability.</p><p>According to the <a href="https://www.cfr.org/news-releases/new-cfr-infoguide-explores-emergence-kurds" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">Council on Foreign Relations,</a> around 30 million Kurds live in the Middle East, mainly in Iran, Iraq, Syria and Turkey.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/trump-says-iran-starting-to-cross-us-red-lines-protesters-die-government-crackdown" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>TRUMP SAYS IRAN 'STARTING TO' CROSS US RED LINES AS PROTESTERS DIE IN GOVERNMENT CRACKDOWN</strong></a></p><p>Turkey has designated Kurdish militant groups in northern Iraq as terrorist organizations and has carried out cross-border military operations against them. The Turkish military has also targeted PKK bases in Iraq.</p><p>In 2025, the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/world-regions/turkey" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Kurdistan Workers’ Party (PKK)</a> said it would disarm and end its decades-long battle against Turkey.</p><p>Reuters said MIT and President Recep Tayyip Erdoğan's office did not comment on the Iran crossing, though it warned that any interference in Iran would inflame regional crises.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/travel/leave-iran-now-us-embassy-posts-warning-americans-still-country" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>'LEAVE IRAN NOW': US EMBASSY POSTS WARNING TO AMERICANS STILL IN THE COUNTRY</strong></a></p><p>Iranian authorities alleged the fighters were dispatched from Iraq and Turkey and said the Iranian regime has asked both governments to stop any transfer of fighters or weapons into Iran.</p><p>The number of deaths during the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/world-protests" target="_blank" rel="noopener">crackdown on protesters</a> rose to at least 2,571 on Wednesday, accordin g to the Human Rights Activists News Agency.</p><p>President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/donald-trump" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Donald Trump</a> said Wednesday he had been told the killings had halted, and he believes there is no plan for large-scale executions. </p><p>Asked who told him, Trump said they were "very important sources on the other side."</p><p>Iran closed its airspace to most flights Wednesday, according to flight-tracking website <a href="https://www.flightradar24.com/40.76,-73.98/6" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">Flightradar24,</a> with the closure lasting a little more than two hours.</p>
