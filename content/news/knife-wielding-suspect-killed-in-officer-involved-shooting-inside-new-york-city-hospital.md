<p>Authorities are investigating an officer-involved shooting Thursday night inside NewYork-Presbyterian Brooklyn Methodist Hospital in <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/new-york-city" target="_blank" rel="noopener">New York City</a> that left a knife-wielding suspect dead.</p><p>NYPD Brooklyn South Commanding Officer Assistant Chief Charles Minch said police responded to multiple 911 calls around 5:27 p.m. for a violent man armed with a knife inside the hospital. </p><p>The man barricaded himself inside a room on the eighth floor with an elderly patient and a hospital security guard. He allegedly threatened to kill hospital staff, and had cut himself and attempted to cut others, Minch said during a news conference after the incident.</p><p>"This incident demonstrates the incredibly dangerous situation that our officers encounter every single day," Minch said, noting that the information about the incident remained preliminary.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/knife-wielding-man-st-louis-airport-shot-killed-police" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>KNIFE-WIELDING MAN IN ST. LOUIS AIRPORT SHOT AND KILLED BY POLICE</strong></a></p><p>Minch said <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/police-and-law-enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener">officers encountered</a> blood throughout the room and door area and issued repeated verbal commands for the man to drop the weapon. Police attempted to use Tasers multiple times, but they were ineffective.</p><p>When the man "advanced toward officers with the weapon in his hand," officers discharged <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/personal-freedoms/second-amendment" target="_blank" rel="noopener">their firearms</a>, striking him, Minch said. The man was pronounced dead a short time later.</p><p>The suspect's identity has not yet been released.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/north-carolina-police-officer-killed-line-duty-emergency-room-shooting-incident" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>NORTH CAROLINA POLICE OFFICER KILLED IN LINE OF DUTY DURING EMERGENCY ROOM SHOOTING INCIDENT</strong></a></p><p>The knife was recovered at the scene, and the entire incident was captured on officers’ body cameras, police said.</p><p>A law enforcement source told Fox News Digital the suspect was armed with a knife inside the hospital and security called for help.</p><p>The NYPD issued a statement on X asking the public to avoid the vicinity of 6th Street and 7th Avenue in Brooklyn <strong>at the time</strong> "due to a police investigation."</p><p>"Expect delays and heavy traffic in the surrounding area," NYPD officials wrote in the post.</p><p>The hospital directed all inquiries to the NYPD.</p>
