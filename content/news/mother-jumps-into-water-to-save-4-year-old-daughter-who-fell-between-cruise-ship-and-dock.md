<p>A mother jumped into the water without hesitation after her young daughter fell between a dock and a Carnival cruise ship <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/travel/vacation-destinations/bahamas" target="_blank" rel="noopener">in the Bahamas</a>, prompting a frantic rescue captured on video.</p><p>The incident unfolded near Celebration Key on Grand Bahama Island and was captured from above aboard a nearby cruise ship, with video showing workers and passengers reacting as the child and her mother were in the water alongside the Carnival Sunrise.</p><p>The video was shared on TikTok by user Chelspiers, who said she was aboard the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/travel/general/cruises" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Carnival Conquest</a> when she heard screaming outside her room and realized something was wrong along the dock.</p><p>"I’m sitting in my room on the Carnival Conquest and I hear screaming outside," she wrote. "Apparently, people fell between the dock and the Carnival Sunrise. They were pulled to safety! A mama jumped in after her baby, brave woman! Very scary situation but they were rescued quickly!"</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/nypd-officer-leaps-freezing-river-save-teenage-girl-from-drowning" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>NYPD OFFICER LEAPS INTO FREEZING RIVER TO SAVE TEENAGE GIRL FROM DROWNING</strong></a></p><p>Video shows several workers lining the edge of the dock just feet from the cruise ship, looking down and scrambling to locate a life ring as others rushed to assist.</p><p>Once a life ring was tossed into the water, additional people ran over to help, some carrying a ladder toward the edge of the dock as the rescue continued.</p><p>Moments later, the 4-year-old child was pulled up to safety using the life ring, drawing cheers from onlookers watching the dramatic rescue unfold.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/fort-worth-police-good-samaritans-rescue-baby-trapped-under-vehicle-interstate" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>FORT WORTH POLICE AND GOOD SAMARITANS RESCUE BABY TRAPPED UNDER VEHICLE ON INTERSTATE</strong></a></p><p>A short time later, the mother was pulled back onto the dock.
