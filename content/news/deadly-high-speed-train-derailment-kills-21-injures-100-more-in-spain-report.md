<p>At least 21 people were killed and dozens more were injured after a high-speed train derailed and struck another train in <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/world-regions/spain" target="_blank" rel="noopener">southern Spain</a> on Sunday evening, according to reports.</p><p>The crash happened at around 6:40 p.m. local time near the town of Adamuz in Córdoba province, when the initial derailment forced a second train off the tracks. Roughly 100 people were injured, including 25 with serious injuries, Reuters reported, citing police sources and local media.</p><p>One of the train drivers was among those killed, according to Reuters.</p><p>Spain’s rail infrastructure operator, Adif, said the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/video/topics/world/disasters/transportation" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Iryo 6189</a> Malaga-to-Madrid train derailed and crashed onto an adjacent track, causing a second train traveling from Madrid to Huelva to also derail. </p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/multiple-people-killed-after-construction-crane-falls-derails-train-thailand" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>MULTIPLE PEOPLE KILLED AFTER CONSTRUCTION CRANE FALLS, DERAILS TRAIN IN THAILAND</strong></a></p><p>The <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/disasters" target="_blank" rel="noopener">crash happened</a> about 10 minutes after the Iryo train departed Córdoba. </p><p>The Iryo train was identified as a Freccia 1000 train carrying more than 300 passengers, while the other train was operated by Renfe and had approximately 100 people on board, Reuters reported.</p><p>One woman, who said she was a passenger on the Iryo train, described chaotic scenes during the derailment.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/travel/us-citizens-injured-peru-train-collision-near-world-famous-machu-picchu-tourist-destination" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>US CITIZENS INJURED IN PERU TRAIN COLLISION NEAR WORLD-FAMOUS MACHU PICCHU TOURIST DESTINATION</strong></a></p><p>"Ten minutes after departing, the train started to shake a lot, and it derailed from coach 6 behind us. The lights went out," she <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/tech/companies/twitter" target="_blank" rel="noopener">wrote on X</a>.</p><p>Videos posted on social media showed <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/disaster-response" target="_blank" rel="noopener">emergency responders</a> assisting people as they climbed out through train windows.</p><p>Rail services between Madrid and Andalusia were subsequently suspended, according to Reuters.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/travel/train-crash-800-passengers-risk-likely-caused-human-error" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>TRAIN CRASH WITH 800 PASSENGERS AT RISK LIKELY CAUSED BY HUMAN ERROR</strong></a></p><p>The deadly derailment comes just days after a separate rail disaster in Thailand, where at least 22 people were killed when a construction crane collapsed onto a moving passenger train, causing it to derail and catch fire.</p><p><i>Fox News Digital's Michael Sinkewicz contributed to this report.</i></p>
