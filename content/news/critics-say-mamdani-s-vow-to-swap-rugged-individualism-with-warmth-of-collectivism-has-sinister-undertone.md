How many dead over the past 100 years due to collectivist ideologies?" DeSantis wrote in a <a href="https://x.com/RonDeSantis/status/2006851592847544431" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">post on X.</a></p><p>House Republican Conference Chair Rep. <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/person/m/rep-lisa-mcclain" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Lisa McClain</a> of Michigan called Mamdani a "communist," a label which he has previously rejected.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/media/desantis-warns-gop-faces-issue-keeping-trump-voters-engaged-future-elections" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>DESANTIS WARNS GOP FACES ‘ISSUE’ KEEPING TRUMP VOTERS ENGAGED IN FUTURE ELECTIONS</strong></a></p><p>"Zohran Mamdani is a dangerous communist who is likely to DESTROY NYC through his dedication to communist ideology.
