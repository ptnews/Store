<p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/alex-bregman" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Alex Bregman</a> bet on himself, and it appeared to have paid off.</p><p>The three-time All-Star agreed to a five-year contract with the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/sports/mlb/chicago-cubs" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Chicago Cubs</a> on Saturday, according to multiple reports. The deal is reportedly worth $175 million and includes a no-trade clause.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/sports" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>CLICK HERE FOR MORE SPORTS COVERAGE ON FOXNEWS.COM</strong></a></p><p>Bregman joined the Cubs after he signed a three-year deal with the Boston Red Sox before the 2025 season. The deal included opt-outs after each year. Bregman tested free agency and got a lengthy deal with Chicago.</p><p>In his lone season with the Red Sox, he hit .273 with a .821 OPS and 18 home runs. He missed all of June with a quadricep injury. Boston was 89-73 and finished in third place in the American League East. The Red Sox will still owe him $5 million in January 2028 and $2 million each June from 2035-44.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/sports/mlb-free-agent-outfielder-suspended-80-games-testing-positive-banned-performance-enhancing-substance" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>MLB FREE AGENT OUTFIELDER SUSPENDED 80 GAMES AFTER TESTING POSITIVE FOR BANNED PERFORMANCE-ENHANCING SUBSTANCE</strong></a></p><p>Bregman, 31, played the first nine seasons of his major league career with the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/sports/mlb/houston-astros" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Houston Astros</a>. He hit .272 with a .848 OPS and 191 home runs in 1,111 games with the Astros.</p><p>Now, he’ll join a Cubs team poised to make a run in the playoffs following a successful 2025 season.
