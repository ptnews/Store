<p>Ole Miss appeared to finally feel the impact of <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/sports/ole-miss-legend-describes-hurt-created-lane-kiffins-decision-join-lsu-theres-anger" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Lane Kiffin’s controversial pivot</a> to LSU. The Rebels’ Cinderella-like run ended in the Fiesta Bowl, triggering a lucrative payment for the former Ole Miss head coach.</p><p>Within the next 30 days, Kiffin will receive a bonus of $500,000. </p><p>According to <a href="https://frontofficesports.com/lane-kiffin-earns-500k-bonus-from-ole-miss-win-over-georgia/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener"><u>Front Office Sports</u></a>, part of LSU’s deal to lure Kiffin away from Ole Miss was to pay his bonuses promised by his previous employer. The payment is expected to fall under the "ancillary benefits" category listed in Kiffin’s deal with the Tigers.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/sports" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>CLICK HERE FOR MORE SPORTS COVERAGE ON FOXNEWS.COM</strong></a></p><p>Kiffin’s bonus was tiered. A $150,000 payment was earmarked once Ole Miss secured a first-round playoff game. The Rebels’ advance to the quarterfinals increased the compensation to $250,000.</p><p>The Rebels’ New Year’s Day win over Georgia upped that <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/sports/lane-kiffin-cashes-ole-miss-advances-college-football-playoff" target="_blank" rel="noopener">bonus to $500,000.</a> Had Ole Miss hoisted the College Football Playoff national championship trophy, Kiffin would have earned a total of $1 million.</p><p>"Coach will be entitled to receive a payment in an amount equal to the amount Coach would have been entitled to receive had he remained Head Coach at Coach’s immediate prior employer and coached the prior employer’s football team through the 2025-26 CFP," Kiffin's LSU contract, <a href="https://www.on3.com/news/lane-kiffin-due-500000-payout-from-lsu-after-ole-miss-college-football-playoff-run/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">obtained by On3</a>, states. </p><p>"If applicable, the payment under this section may be paid from affiliated foundation funds and shall be paid within 30 days following the prior employer’s team being eliminated from the 2025-26 CFP."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/sports/miami-beats-ole-miss-behind-carson-becks-game-winning-touchdown-reach-cfp-national-championship-game" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>MIAMI BEATS OLE MISS BEHIND CARSON BECK'S GAME-WINNING TOUCHDOWN TO REACH CFP NATIONAL CHAMPIONSHIP GAME</strong></a></p><p>After dominating Tulane in the first round of the College Football Playoff, the Rebels <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/sports/ole-miss-upsets-georgia-sugar-bowl-unusual-ending" target="_blank" rel="noopener">upset the Georgia Bulldogs</a> in the quarterfinals.</p><p>Kiffin celebrated the team’s win in a post on social media. </p><p>"What a great finish!!! So much fun!! Only two more to go," he wrote in a post on X, tagging the Ole Miss football team. </p><p>Despite being underdogs in Thursday’s semifinal, Ole Miss pushed the favored Miami Hurricanes to the brink. Miami quarterback Carson Beck rushed for a go-ahead touchdown in the game’s final seconds to punch the Hurricanes’ ticket to the national championship game.</p><p>Miami now awaits the winner of Friday’s other semifinal matchup, the Peach Bowl, between <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/sports/indiana-hoosiers-head-coach-curt-cignetti-happy-cfp" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Indiana and</a> Oregon.</p><p><i>Fox News' Paulina Dedaj contributed to this report.</i></p><p><i><strong>Follow Fox News Digital’s </strong></i><a class="c-link" href="https://twitter.com/FoxNewsSports_" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><i><strong>sports coverage on X</strong></i></a><i><strong>, and subscribe to </strong></i><a class="c-link" href="https://www.foxnews.com/newsletters" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><i><strong>the Fox News Sports Huddle newsletter</strong></i></a><i><strong>.</strong></i></p>
