<p>The surgeon accused of gunning down his ex-wife and her dentist husband inside their <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/us-regions/midwest/ohio" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Ohio home</a> has retained a local defense attorney who previously worked on another high-profile murder case.</p><p>Michael McKee is now being represented by attorney Diane Menashe as he faces four counts of aggravated murder, each with firearm specifications, according to court documents obtained by Fox News Digital. Menashe filed a notice of appearance on Jan. 22. </p><p>Menashe has worked as an attorney for 27 years and currently manages her own firm, <a href="https://menashelawgroup.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow"><u>according to</u></a> the Menashe Law Group website. </p><p>She previously represented Dr. William Husel, an Ohio doctor who was acquitted in 2022 on all 14 counts of murder after prosecutors accused him of prescribing excessive pain medication to his patients in the Columbus-based Mount Carmel Health System.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/ex-husband-charged-ohio-dentist-double-murder-appears-illinois-court-possible-extradition" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>EX-HUSBAND CHARGED IN OHIO DENTIST DOUBLE MURDER TO APPEAR IN ILLINOIS COURT FOR POSSIBLE EXTRADITION</strong></a></p><p>During <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/trials" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Husel’s trial</a>, his defense team called one witness to the stand – a Georgia anesthesiologist who testified that Husel’s patients died from their medical conditions and not Husel’s actions.</p><p>He was acquitted on all charges in April 2022 after a jury deliberated for more than a week. </p><p>McKee is accused of shooting and killing Spencer Tepe, 37, and Monique Tepe, 39, inside their Weinland Park home in Columbus between 2 a.m. and 5 a.m. on Dec. 30. McKee is Monique’s ex-husband.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/ex-husband-charged-ohio-dentist-murders-allegedly-tried-disguise-himself-fake-information-expert" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>EX-HUSBAND CHARGED IN OHIO DENTIST MURDERS ALLEGEDLY TRIED TO 'DISGUISE' HIMSELF WITH FAKE INFORMATION: EXPERT</strong></a></p><p>Following a <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/manhunt" target="_blank" rel="noopener">weekslong manhunt</a>, McKee was arrested in Illinois on Jan. 10 and subsequently <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/surgeon-ex-husband-extradited-ohio-slaying-dentist-wife" target="_blank" rel="noopener">extradited to Ohio</a> after awaiting his hearing two days later, where he remains in custody at the Franklin County Jail. </p><p>A sheriff’s spokesperson previously said that McKee faces <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/ohio-dentist-slayings-suspect-indicted-more-aggravated-murder-burglary-charges" target="_blank" rel="noopener">upgraded premeditated aggravated murder charges</a><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/homicide" target="_blank" rel="noopener">,</a> with three of the counts including specifications alleging the use or display of an automatic firearm or silencer.
