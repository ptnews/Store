<p>North Korean leader <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/kim-jong-un" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Kim Jong Un</a> appeared publicly with his daughter, Ju Ae, during a New Year’s Day visit to the Kumsusan Palace of the Sun, a symbolic site honoring the country’s founding leaders.</p><p>Images from the state news agency KCNA show Ju Ae standing between her parents in the main hall of the mausoleum, a moment that analysts say has fueled speculation about her future role in the regime.</p><p>Ju Ae has been featured more often in <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/conflicts/north-korea" target="_blank" rel="noopener">state media</a> over the past three years, fueling speculation by analysts and South Korea’s intelligence agency that she may be positioned as a potential successor to Kim Jong Un.</p><p>The appearance comes amid a longstanding tradition in North Korea, where Kim visits the mausoleum on key dates and anniversaries to reinforce the legitimacy of the nuclear-armed country's dynastic heritage.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/trump-kim-jong-un-should-make-bold-decision-meet-during-his-asia-trip-south-korean-official-says" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>TRUMP AND KIM JONG UN SHOULD MAKE 'BOLD DECISION' TO MEET DURING HIS ASIA TRIP, SOUTH KOREAN OFFICIAL SAYS</strong></a></p><p>North Korea has never confirmed Ju Ae’s age, and her exact age remains unknown, but she is believed to be around 12 or 13.</p><p>After being publicly introduced in North Korea in 2022, Ju Ae made her first public appearance outside of her home country while accompanying her father on a <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/world-regions/china" target="_blank" rel="noopener">trip to Beijing</a> in September, his first visit to China in years, a move analysts said may signal her role as North Korea’s heir-in-waiting.</p><p>Ju Ae's existence was inadvertently revealed by retired NBA star Dennis Rodman over a decade ago in an interview with The Guardian.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/north-korea-fires-ballistic-missile-days-after-hegseth-wraps-south-korea-visit" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>NORTH KOREA FIRES BALLISTIC MISSILE DAYS AFTER HEGSETH WRAPS SOUTH KOREA VISIT</strong></a></p><p>Rodman told reporters during a 2013 trip to Pyongyang he had "held the baby Ju Ae," while calling Kim Jong Un a "good dad."</p><p>Parenting expert Kirsty Ketley told Fox News Digital in September that placing Ju Ae in the global spotlight at a young age could have lasting consequences.</p><p>"She’s being shaped into a role rather than being supported to figure out who she is as an individual, and that pressure could have a <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/health/mental-health" target="_blank" rel="noopener">huge impact on her mental health</a>," she said. </p><p><i>Fox News Digital's Emma Bussey and Reuters</i><strong> </strong><i>contributed to this report.</i></p>
