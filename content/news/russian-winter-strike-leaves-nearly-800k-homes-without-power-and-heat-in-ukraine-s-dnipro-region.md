<p>Nearly 800,000 <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/conflicts/ukraine" target="_blank" rel="noopener">homes in Ukraine</a> were left without electricity and heating Thursday morning after a Russian overnight attack on energy infrastructure, the country’s acting energy minister said, as power crews raced to restore service.</p><p>Artem Nekrasov said in comments published on the Ukrainian Energy Ministry’s official Telegram channel that the Dnipro and Zaporizhzhia regions were almost completely without power following the Russian strike.</p><p>"Repair work is ongoing to return <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/energy" target="_blank" rel="noopener">light and heat</a> to consumers as soon as possible," Nekrasov said.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/russian-drone-strikes-tanker-ukraines-odesa-zelenskyy-closes-us-gas-deal" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>RUSSIAN DRONE STRIKES TANKER IN UKRAINE’S ODESA AFTER ZELENSKYY CLOSES US GAS DEAL</strong></a></p><p>Power has since been restored in the Zaporizhzhia region, while hundreds of thousands of homes in Dnipro remain without electricity and heat.</p><p>Ukrainian officials urged residents to limit the use of high-consumption electrical appliances to prevent renewed outages as repairs continue.</p><p>Nekrasov said <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/weather" target="_blank" rel="noopener">adverse weather conditions</a> also left parts of several other regions without power, including Chernihiv, Kyiv, Ivano-Frankivsk and Zakarpattia.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/uk-france-strike-new-ukraine-security-pact-us-takes-lead-ceasefire-enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>UK, FRANCE STRIKE NEW UKRAINE SECURITY PACT AS US TAKES LEAD IN CEASEFIRE ENFORCEMENT</strong></a></p><p>The power outages are unfolding amid freezing temperatures in Kyiv, where forecasts show overnight lows under 20 degrees.</p><p>"There is absolutely no military rationale in such strikes on the energy sector and infrastructure that leave people without electricity and heating in wintertime," <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/volodymyr-zelenskyy" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy</a> said Thursday on X.</p><p>"This is Russia’s war specifically against our people, against life in Ukraine – an attempt to break Ukraine."</p><p>The strikes come just days after <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/zelenskyy-says-peace-deal-close-trump-meeting-territory-remains-sticking-point" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Zelenskyy met in Paris</a> with U.S. special envoy Steve Witkoff and President Donald Trump’s son-in-law Jared Kushner to discuss continued diplomatic efforts to end Russia’s nearly four-year war.</p><p>Zelenskyy said the talks focused on security guarantees, monitoring a potential ceasefire, and rebuilding Ukraine as part of a broader framework for peace.</p>
