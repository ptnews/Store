<p><a href="https://www.foxsports.com/college-basketball/morez-johnson-player" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Morez Johnson Jr.</a> had 24 points and 11 rebounds to lead No. 2 <a class="entity" href="https://www.foxsports.com/college-basketball/michigan-wolverines-team">Michigan</a> to a 112-71 victory over <a class="entity" href="https://www.foxsports.com/college-basketball/mcneese-state-cowboys-team">McNeese</a> State on Monday night.</p><p>The Wolverines (12-0) are off to their best start since they opened the 2018-19 season 17-0, a school record. It’s just the fourth time Michigan has won its first 12 games.</p><p>The Cowboys (10-3) had won four straight overall and two consecutive road games before being overmatched by one of college basketball’s top teams.</p><p>The 24 points matched a career high for Johnson. He also scored 24 this season against Oakland.</p><p><a class="entity" href="https://www.foxsports.com/college-basketball/yaxel-lendeborg-player">Yaxel Lendeborg</a> added 16 points for the Wolverines, and <a class="entity" href="https://www.foxsports.com/college-basketball/trey-mckenney-player">Trey McKenney</a> and <a class="entity" href="https://www.foxsports.com/college-basketball/aday-mara-player">Aday Mara</a> each had 13. <a class="entity" href="https://www.foxsports.com/college-basketball/nimari-burnett-player">Nimari Burnett</a> scored 12 and Roddy Gayle Jr. finished with 10.</p><p><a class="entity" href="https://www.foxsports.com/college-basketball/garwey-dual-player">Garwey Dual</a> led McNeese State with 15 points. <a class="entity" href="https://www.foxsports.com/college-basketball/larry-johnson-6-player">Larry Johnson</a> scored 14, <a class="entity" href="https://www.foxsports.com/college-basketball/tyshawn-archie-player">Tyshawn Archie</a> had 12 and DJ Richards Jr. added 11 for the Cowboys.</p><p>The Wolverines scored the first seven points and led 22-7 before the seven-minute mark of the first half. McNeese State was never able to close the gap to less than 10 the rest of the game. Michigan led 60-29 at halftime.</p><p>Michigan scored 100 points for the third straight game and sixth time in seven games. The Wolverines have reached the 100-point plateau seven times already this season. That’s three games shy of the school record set in 1988-89.</p><p>The Wolverines have won nine games this season by 25-plus points and six by 40-plus points. Six of Michigan’s last eight wins were by margins of 30 or more.</p><p>McNeese State: The Cowboys are home against Lamar on Friday in Southland Conference play.</p><p>Michigan: The Wolverines host No. 24 <a class="entity" href="https://www.foxsports.com/college-basketball/usc-trojans-team">USC</a> in a <a class="entity" href="https://www.foxsports.com/college-basketball/big-ten">Big Ten</a> game Friday.</p><p><i>Reporting by The Associated Press.</i></p><p><i>Want great stories delivered right to your inbox? </i><a href="https://www.foxsports.com/newsletter"><i><u>Create or log in to your FOX Sports account</u></i></a><i>, and follow leagues, teams and players to receive a personalized newsletter daily!</i></p>
