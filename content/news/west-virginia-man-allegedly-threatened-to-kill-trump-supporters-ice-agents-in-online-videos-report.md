rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow"><u>according to</u></a> a criminal complaint obtained by WBOY. </p><p>While investigating the claim, troopers learned that 20-year-old Cody Smith "posted videos of himself" in which he told viewers "he was going to attack and kill ICE agents" while contacting the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/executive/homeland-security" target="_blank" rel="noopener">U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS)</a>, the complaint reportedly said. </p><p>He also allegedly threatened the DHS employee who answered his call, <a href="https://www.wdtv.com/2026/01/23/harrison-county-man-arrested-threatening-ice-agents-trump-supporters-videos/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow"><u>according to</u></a> WDTV.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/new-jersey-twins-charged-threats-kill-dhs-official-shoot-ice-sight" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>NEW JERSEY TWINS CHARGED IN THREATS TO KILL DHS OFFICIAL, ‘SHOOT ICE ON SIGHT’</strong></a></p><p>Authorities began looking into Smith’s online activity, and later discovered previous social media posts in which Smith allegedly made claims
