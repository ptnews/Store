Kennedy Jr. said in part of a January 22, 2026, joint statement.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/un-chief-claims-us-puts-power-over-international-law-trump-questions-uns-relevance" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>UN CHIEF ACCUSES US OF DITCHING INTERNATIONAL LAW AS TRUMP BLASTS GLOBAL BODIES</strong></a></p><p>"Going forward, U.S. engagement with the WHO will be limited strictly to effectuate our withdrawal and to safeguard the health and safety of the American people. All U.S. funding for, and staffing of, WHO initiatives has ceased," their <a href="https://www.hhs.gov/press-room/joint-statement-by-secretary-of-state-rubio-and-secretary-of-health-and-human-services-kennedy-on-the-termination-of-us-membership-in-the-world-health-organization-who.html" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">statement</a> said.</p><p>They claimed the WHO "pursued a politicized, bureaucratic agenda driven by nations hostile to American interests."
