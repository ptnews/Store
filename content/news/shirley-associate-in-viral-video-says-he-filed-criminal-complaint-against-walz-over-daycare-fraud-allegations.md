Tim Walz</a> related to the alleged widespread fraud.</p><p>In a post shared on X, Shirley and David are also shown during the visit standing outside the facility in matching sweatshirts.</p><p>Shirley captioned the post, "WE OUT HERE LEARIN AND STOPPING FRAUD."</p><p><a OVER ALLEGED DAYCARE FRAUD SCHEME</strong></a></p><p>Both men's sweatshirts also read "<a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/minneapolis-st-paul" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Quality ‘Learing’ Center</a>," a <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/misspelled-learning-center-no-children-inside-emmer-presses-walz-over-minnesota-daycare-tied-4m" target="_blank" rel="noopener">misspelling that mirrored</a> the signage associated with the Minneapolis site central to <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/corruption" target="_blank" rel="noopener">fraud allegations.</a></p><p>Shirley’s return visit came after the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/controversies" target="_blank" rel="noopener">facility’s manager claimed</a> his original video
