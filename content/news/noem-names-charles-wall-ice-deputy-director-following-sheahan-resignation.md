<p>Department of Homeland Security (DHS) Secretary <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/kristi-noem" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Kristi Noem</a> announced Thursday via X that longtime U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) attorney Charles Wall will serve as the agency’s new deputy director as enforcement operations intensify nationwide.</p><p>"Effective immediately, Charles Wall will serve as the Deputy Director of @ICEGov," <a href="https://www.dhs.gov/news/2026/01/15/secretary-noem-announces-new-deputy-director-ice" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">wrote Noem</a>. "For the last year, Mr. Wall served as ICE’s Principal Legal Advisor, playing a key role in helping us deliver historic results in arresting and removing the worst of the worst criminal illegal aliens from American neighborhoods."</p><p>Wall replaces Madison Sheahan, who <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/ice-deputy-director-resigns-agency-run-congress" target="_blank" rel="noopener">stepped down earlier</a> Thursday to pursue a congressional run in Ohio. Her departure left ICE leadership in transition at a moment when the agency has faced increasing resistance to enforcement efforts and heightened threats against officers in the field.</p><p>The move comes as the Trump administration intensifies <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/immigration/enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener">immigration enforcement</a> against murderers, rapists, gang members and suspected terrorists living illegally in the U.S., even as sanctuary jurisdictions and activist groups seek to block or disrupt ICE actions.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/dhs-demands-mn-leaders-honor-ice-detainers-alleges-hundreds-criminal-aliens-have-been-released-under-walz" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>DHS DEMANDS MN LEADERS HONOR ICE DETAINERS, ALLEGES HUNDREDS OF CRIMINAL ALIENS HAVE BEEN RELEASED UNDER WALZ</strong></a></p><p>ICE officials said Wall brings more than a decade of experience inside the agency.</p><p>"Mr. Wall has served as an ICE attorney for 14 years and is a forward-leaning, strategic thinker who understands the importance of <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/immigration/deportation" target="_blank" rel="noopener">prioritizing the removal</a> of murderers, rapists, pedophiles, gang members, and terrorists from our country," Noem added.</p><p>Wall most recently served as ICE’s principal legal advisor, overseeing more than 3,500 attorneys and support staff who represent the DHS in removal proceedings and provide legal counsel to senior agency leadership. </p><p>He has served at ICE since 2012, previously holding senior counsel roles in New Orleans, according to DHS.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/worst-worst-ice-arrests-child-predator-violent-criminals-amid-surge-anti-agent-attacks" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>‘WORST OF THE WORST’: ICE ARRESTS CHILD PREDATOR, VIOLENT CRIMINALS AMID SURGE IN ANTI-AGENT ATTACKS</strong></a></p><p>DHS has described the appointment as part of a broader effort to ensure ICE leadership is aligned with the Trump administration’s public safety priorities.</p><p>The leadership change comes as ICE operations have drawn national attention following protests in Minneapolis after the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/omar-dems-demand-noem-impeachment-paint-minnesota-woman-shot-ice-poet-chose-love" target="_blank" rel="noopener">ICE-involved fatal shooting</a> of 37-year-old Renee Good on Jan. 7.</p><p>Administration officials have repeatedly emphasized that ICE’s focus remains on what they describe as the "worst of the worst" criminal illegal aliens, warning that local resistance and political opposition increase risks for officers carrying out enforcement duties.</p><p>ICE has recently created a <a href="https://www.dhs.gov/wow" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow">specific landing page</a> where these 'worst of the worst' offenders can be viewed with names and nationalities attached.</p><p>"I look forward to working with him in his new role to <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/executive/homeland-security" target="_blank" rel="noopener">make America safe again</a>," Noem concluded.</p><p>ICE did not immediately provide additional comment to Fox News Digital.</p>
