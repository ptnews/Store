<p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/conflicts/iran" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Iran’s government</a> has said its citizens will be given a monthly payment equivalent to about $7 to ease economic pressures as protests spread across the country, according to reports.</p><p>The announcement was reported to have been made on Monday by the government spokesperson, Fatemeh Mohajerani, on Iranian State TV.</p><p>She said the measure was aimed at "preserving households" purchasing power, controlling inflation and ensuring food security," per <a href="https://www.nytimes.com/2026/01/05/world/europe/iran-protests-payments.html" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">The New York Times</a>.</p><p>The outlet also said the plan represents a <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/executive/economic-policy" target="_blank" rel="noopener">shift away from long-standing import subsidies</a> toward direct assistance for citizens.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/iran-crackdown-rattles-middle-east-analysts-weigh-us-options-short-military-intervention" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>IRAN CRACKDOWN RATTLES MIDDLE EAST AS ANALYSTS WEIGH US OPTIONS SHORT OF MILITARY INTERVENTION</strong></a></p><p>Under the proposal, roughly $10 billion that had been spent each year to subsidize certain imports, will now be given directly to the public.</p><p>Eligible Iranians will get one million Iranian tomans, which is around $7, and in the form of credit that can be used to buy goods.</p><p>The labor minister said the payments would be handed out to about 80 million people, which is the majority of Iran’s population.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/protests-spread-across-iran-regime-threatens-us-forces-legitimate-targets-after-trump-warning" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>PROTESTS SPREAD ACROSS IRAN AS REGIME THREATENS US FORCES AS 'LEGITIMATE TARGETS' AFTER TRUMP WARNING</u></strong></a></p><p>Iran’s economy has been hit by <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/finance/sanctions" target="_blank" rel="noopener">sanctions and declining oil revenues</a> which have led to protests.</p><p><a>T</a>he currency has lost more than half its value against the U.S. dollar.</p><p>The Statistical Center of Iran, a state-run body under Iran’s regime, reported in December that the average annual inflation rate also reached 42.2%, according <a href="https://iranfocus.com/economy/56505-irans-point-to-point-inflation-surpassed-52-in-december/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">to reports</a>.</p><p>The payments were announced amid <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/iranian-protesters-clash-security-forces-tear-gas-fills-tehran-streets-amid-nationwide-unrest" target="_blank" rel="noopener">widespread protests</a> that included merchants, traders and university students, according to the Times. Marketplaces have been shut down and rallies have been held on campuses.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/irans-khamenei-lashes-out-protesters-nationwide-anti-regime-unrest-grows" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>IRAN’S KHAMENEI LASHES OUT AT PROTESTERS AS NATIONWIDE ANTI-REGIME UNREST GROWS</strong></a></p><p>As previously reported by <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/iran-protests-prompt-new-trump-warning-over-deadly-government-crackdowns" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Fox News Digital</a>, according to the U.S.-based Human Rights Activists in Iran (HRAI), <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/iran-protests-prompt-new-trump-warning-over-deadly-government-crackdowns" target="_blank" rel="noopener">the intensity of the protests </a><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/irans-khamenei-lashes-out-protesters-nationwide-anti-regime-unrest-grows" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><u>has reached at least</u></a> 78 cities and 222 locations.</p><p>Protesters have been demanding the end of the regime controlled by the 86-year-old Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei.</p><p>The group said the regime has <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/foreign-policy/human-rights" target="_blank" rel="noopener">killed at least 20 people</a>, including three children, and arrested 990 people. Khamenei’s security forces have detained more than 40 children, HRAI noted.</p><p><i>Fox News Digital's Benjamin Weinthal contributed to this report.</i></p>
