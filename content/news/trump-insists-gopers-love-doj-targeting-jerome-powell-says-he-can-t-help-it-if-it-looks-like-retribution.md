<p>President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/donald-trump" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Donald Trump</a> insisted there's conservative support behind the DOJ targeting Federal Reserve Chair Jerome Powell and that he "can't help" the perception that it looks like political retribution.</p><p>Speaking with CBS News' Tony Dokoupil Tuesday, Trump was asked about <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/trump-criminal-probe-fed-chair-powell-marks-unprecedented-escalation-warning-his-successor" target="_blank" rel="noopener">the probe into Powell</a>, who is facing scrutiny over congressional testimony he gave last summer about the pricey overhaul of the Fed's two main buildings on the National Mall.</p><p>Trump, an outspoken critic of Powell, previously said he had nothing to do with the launch of the investigation.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/media/trump-says-u-s-take-very-strong-action-against-iran-regime-starts-hanging-protesters" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>TRUMP SAYS THE U.S. WILL TAKE ‘VERY STRONG ACTION’ AGAINST IRAN IF THE REGIME STARTS HANGING PROTESTERS</strong></a></p><p>"Even some prominent conservatives say it looks like political retribution," Dokoupil said.</p><p>"And some prominent conservatives also love it — you have to mention that, too," Trump fired back. "As you grow older in this business, you have to mention the other side. More people like it than not."</p><p>Trump called Powell a "lousy" <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/finance/federal-reserve" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Fed chair</a> who's been "too high on interest rates." He also insisted he could've fixed the Fed buildings for "$25 million."</p><p>"He's into billions and billions of dollars, and it looks like they won't open for a long time. And he's either corrupt or incompetent," Trump said.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/thune-warns-dojs-probe-fed-chair-powell-better-real-serious-amid-political-backlash" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>THUNE WARNS DOJ'S PROBE OF FED CHAIR POWELL 'BETTER BE REAL' AND 'SERIOUS' AMID POLITICAL BACKLASH</strong></a></p><p>"On the question of corrupt or incompetent, you're right, some prominent conservatives are criticizing this. I'll take your word that others are not," Dokoupil said.</p><p>"You don't have to take my word," Trump interjected. "What you should do is go out and interview some people, and you'll find out."</p><p>"But on this direct question about political retribution, what it looks like, sir, what's your response?" the anchor asked.</p><p>"I can't help what it looks like," Trump responded before touting his accomplishments on the economy.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/media" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>CLICK HERE FOR THE LATEST MEDIA AND CULTURE NEWS</strong></a></p><p>Several GOP lawmakers have expressed concern about the looming Powell probe, including Trump's allies like Sen.
