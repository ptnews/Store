<p>Five individuals were killed during a Dec. 31 U.S. military kinetic strike against two vessels operated by designated terrorist organizations involved in <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/terrorism/narco-terror" target="_blank" rel="noopener">narcotics trafficking</a>, U.S. Southern Command said Wednesday.</p><p>Intelligence confirmed the vessels were transiting along known narcotics trafficking routes and were engaged in <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/drugs" target="_blank" rel="noopener">drug trafficking activities</a> at the time of the operation, officials said.</p><p>A total of <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/us-military-strikes-narco-terrorist-vessels-latest-eastern-pacific-drug-trafficking-operation" target="_blank" rel="noopener">five individuals described as narco-terrorists</a> were killed during the actions, officials said, with three killed aboard the first vessel and two killed aboard the second.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/us-military-destroys-narco-terror-convoy-three-vessels-sea-kinetic-strikes" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>US MILITARY DESTROYS NARCO-TERROR CONVOY OF THREE VESSELS AT SEA IN KINETIC STRIKES</strong></a></p><p>The Department of War identified the operation as part of <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/military" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Operation Southern Spear</a>.</p><p>Video released of the operation shows a strike on a vessel at sea. Military officials did not clarify whether additional engagements occurred beyond those shown in the unclassified video.</p><p>Another strike was announced earlier in the day, but it is unclear if the two are linked at this time.</p><p>U.S. Southern Command did not provide any further comment to Fox News Digital at this time.</p>
