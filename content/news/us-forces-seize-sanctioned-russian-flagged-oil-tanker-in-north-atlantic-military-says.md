<p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/military/navy" target="_blank" rel="noopener">U.S. forces</a> on Wednesday seized the Russian-flagged Marinera oil tanker in the North Atlantic Sea, U.S. European Command (EUCOM) said.</p><p>"The @TheJusticeDept &amp; @DHSgov, in coordination with the @DeptofWar today announced the seizure of the M/V Bella 1 for <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/finance/sanctions" target="_blank" rel="noopener">violations of U.S. sanctions</a>," read a post from EUCOM on X. "The vessel was seized in the North Atlantic pursuant to a warrant issued by a U.S. federal court after being tracked by USCGC Munro."</p><p>"This seizure supports @POTUS Proclamation targeting sanctioned vessels that threaten the security and stability of the Western Hemisphere," EUCOM added. "The <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/immigration/enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener">operation was executed by DHS components</a> with support from  @DeptofWar, showcasing a whole-of-government approach to protect the homeland."</p><p>Fox News previously reported that U.S. forces were <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/us-forces-attempting-board-sanctioned-russian-flagged-oil-tanker-north-atlantic-sources-say" target="_blank" rel="noopener">attempting to board the Marinera</a> tanker, which previously operated under the name Bella 1, in the North Atlantic earlier Wednesday morning.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/treasury-targets-oil-traders-tankers-accused-helping-maduro-evade-u-s-sanctions" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>TREASURY TARGETS OIL TRADERS, TANKERS ACCUSED OF HELPING MADURO EVADE U.S. SANCTIONS</strong></a></p><p>The development comes after the Wall Street Journal reported on Tuesday that Russia had sent a submarine and other naval assets to escort the tanker.</p><p>The vessel had spent more than two weeks <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/venezuela-oil-tanker-marinera-evades-us-interception-adopting-russian-identity-fleeing-atlantic" target="_blank" rel="noopener">attempting to slip past</a> U.S. enforcement efforts targeting sanctioned oil shipments near Venezuela, the outlet reported.</p><p>"The blockade of sanctioned and illicit Venezuelan oil remains in FULL EFFECT — anywhere in the world," said Secretary of War <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/pete-hegseth" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Pete Hegseth</a> after the tanker was seized.</p><p>President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/donald-trump" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Donald Trump</a> announced a blockade of all sanctioned oil tankers going in and out of Venezuela in mid-December.</p>
