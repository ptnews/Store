<p>The South Carolina <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/health/measles" target="_blank" rel="noopener">measles outbreak</a> has spread to Clemson University.</p><p>The state's Department of Public Health informed Clemson staff of a "confirmed case of measles" in an individual associated with the university, according to an update from the school published on Saturday.</p><p>"The individual has isolated, per DPH requirements, and DPH is conducting contact tracing with individuals who may have been exposed and outlining isolation and quarantine protocols," the school stated.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/travel/measles-cases-confirmed-four-major-us-airports-across-country-amid-peak-holiday-travel" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>MEASLES CASES CONFIRMED AT FOUR MAJOR US AIRPORTS ACROSS COUNTRY AMID PEAK HOLIDAY TRAVEL</strong></a></p><p>Individuals thought to be exposed to the virus will be contacted via email about quarantining.</p><p>Officials are reporting 558 cases of measles centered around Spartanburg County in the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/health/infectious-disease/outbreaks" target="_blank" rel="noopener">current outbreak</a>.</p><p>Some cases are travel-related exposures or close contacts with known cases, according to the DPH.</p><p>Other cases have no identified source, suggesting that measles is circulating in the community and could <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/health/infectious-disease" target="_blank" rel="noopener">spread further</a>.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/health/largest-measles-outbreak-us-officially-over-health-officials-say" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>LARGEST MEASLES OUTBREAK IN US IS OFFICIALLY OVER, HEALTH OFFICIALS SAY</strong></a></p><p>"Over the last seven to nine days, we've had upwards of <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/health/measles-outbreak-explodes-south-carolina-multiple-hospitalized-cases-surpass-200" target="_blank" rel="noopener">over 200 new cases</a>. That's doubled just in the last week," Dr. Johnathon Elkes, an emergency medicine physician at Prisma Health in Greenville, South Carolina, said during a media briefing on Friday.</p><p>"We feel like we're really kind of staring over the edge, knowing that this is about to get a lot worse."</p><p>Nearly 98% of main campus Clemson students have provided <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/health/infectious-disease/vaccines" target="_blank" rel="noopener">proof of immunity</a>, according to the most recent data from Student Health Services.</p><p>"The health, safety and well-being of Clemson’s campus community remains our highest priority," the school noted.</p><p><a href="http://www.foxnews.com/health" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>CLICK HERE FOR MORE HEALTH STORIES</u></strong></a></p><p>Measles is highly contagious, according to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention.
