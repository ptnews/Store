<p>The <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/travel/general/airports" target="_blank" rel="noopener">world’s most beautiful airport</a> has been crowned, and it’s located in the United States.</p><p>Prix Versailles, an international architecture and design award, recently announced its selections recognizing airport terminals it considers among the world’s most beautiful.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/san-francisco" target="_blank" rel="noopener">San Francisco</a> International Airport’s Harvey Milk Terminal 1 received the top honor.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/travel/airports-allow-non-traveling-public-past-security-entrances-first-time-decades" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>AIRPORTS ALLOW NON-TRAVELING PUBLIC PAST SECURITY ENTRANCES FOR FIRST TIME IN DECADES</strong></a></p><p>The terminal opened in June 2024, according to San Francisco International Airport’s website.</p><p>The terminal features an exhibition honoring gay rights leader Harvey Milk, highlighting his political career and impact on San Francisco through photographs and historical materials.</p><p>On Reddit, some users have praised the terminal for its engaging art and clean environment.</p><p>"Harvey Milk Terminal meets and exceeds my hopes for what an airport terminal should be in the 2020s," one user wrote.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/newsletters?cmpid=fnfirstnl"><strong><u>CLICK HERE TO SIGN UP FOR OUR LIFESTYLE NEWSLETTER</u></strong></a></p><p>"It’s the best airport I’ve ever been to," another commenter said.</p><p>Airport officials addressed the recognition in a statement shared on the airport’s website.</p><p>"We are truly honored to be named the top airport in the world by Prix Versailles," Airport Director Mike Nakornkhet said in the press release.</p><p>"Harvey Milk Terminal 1 was designed to establish a new benchmark for an extraordinary airport experience, bringing to life our mission to put people and planet first. Being the first airport terminal in the world named for an LGBTQ+ leader only enhances the significance of this recognition, and my thanks go out to the entire project team for this milestone achievement," Nakornkhet said.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/lifestyle"><strong><u>CLICK HERE FOR MORE LIFESTYLE STORIES</u></strong></a></p><p>Other airport terminals were also recognized by Prix Versailles as part of its annual selections.</p><p>Those included Terminal 2 at Yantai Penglai International Airport in China, the arrivals terminal at Roland Garros Airport on Réunion Island in France, and Terminal 1 at Kansai International Airport in <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/world-regions/japan" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Osaka, Japan</a>.</p><p>In addition to the main awards, special prizes were given to Terminal 1 at Marseille Provence Airport in Marignane, France, for interior design, and to Portland International Airport in Oregon for its main terminal.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/lifestyle/quizzes"><strong><u>TEST YOURSELF WITH OUR LATEST LIFESTYLE QUIZ</u></strong></a></p><p>In addition to airports, Prix Versailles also recognized projects across several other categories, including campuses, passenger stations, sports venues, <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/travel/general/museums-exhibits" target="_blank" rel="noopener">museums, emporiums</a>, hotels and restaurants.</p><p>"Each year, the Prix Versailles challenges us to look beyond aesthetics alone and to evaluate architecture and design as a force capable of shaping the world we inhabit," the Prix Versailles' jury chairperson said in a press release.</p><p>Fox News Digital reached out to the Prix Versailles for further comment.</p>
