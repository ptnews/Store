<p><a class="entity" href="https://www.foxsports.com/college-basketball/bruce-thornton-player">Bruce Thornton</a> had 20 points to move into eighth place on <a class="entity" href="https://www.foxsports.com/college-basketball/ohio-state-buckeyes-team">Ohio State's</a> all-time scoring list and the Buckeyes defeated <a class="entity" href="https://www.foxsports.com/college-basketball/rutgers-scarlet-knights-team">Rutgers</a> 80-73 on Friday night.</p><p>Thornton made 8 of 13 shots with two 3-pointers for the Buckeyes (10-3, 2-1 Big Ten Conference). Thornton moved three points ahead Jay Burson, who scored 1,756 from 1985-89. <a class="entity" href="https://www.foxsports.com/personalities/jim-jackson">Jim Jackson</a> (1989-92) is next with 1,785. Thornton also grabbed 10 rebounds for his third double-double this season.</p><p><a class="entity" href="https://www.foxsports.com/college-basketball/devin-royal-player">Devin Royal</a> finished with 16 points and four assists, while John Mobley Jr. scored 12 with four assists for Ohio State.</p><p>Reserve Tariq Francis had 17 points to lead the Scarlet Knights (7-7, 0-3). <a class="entity" href="https://www.foxsports.com/college-basketball/dylan-grant-player">Dylan Grant</a> scored 14.</p><p>Francis hit two 3-pointers and scored 15 to help Rutgers take a 41-37 lead into halftime. Reserve Brandon Noel scored all 14 of his points before the break for Ohio State — on 5-for-5 shooting from the floor and a 4-for-4 effort at the free-throw line.</p><p>Francis hit a jumper to begin the second half, but Royal dunked, Thornton hit a jumper and Mobley sank a 3-pointer to cap a 7-0 run as Ohio State took a 44-43 lead with 17:26 remaining.</p><p>Grant answered with a 3-pointer, but Mobley hit another 3 to spark an 8-1 run and Ohio State led 55-47 with 14 minutes left.</p><p><a class="entity" href="https://www.foxsports.com/college-basketball/kaden-powers-player">Kaden Powers</a>' fastbreak dunk gave the Scarlet Knights a 61-60 lead with nine minutes to go. There were five lead changes and two ties until Royal's three-point play put Ohio State up 70-67 and the Buckeyes led over the final 5:23.</p><p>Ohio State: Hosts No. 13 Nebraska on Monday.</p><p>Rutgers: Hosts Oregon on Monday.</p><p><i>Reporting by The Associated Press.</i></p><p><i>Want great stories delivered right to your inbox? </i><a href="https://www.foxsports.com/newsletter"><i><u>Create or log in to your FOX Sports account</u></i></a><i>, and follow leagues, teams and players to receive a personalized newsletter daily!</i></p>
