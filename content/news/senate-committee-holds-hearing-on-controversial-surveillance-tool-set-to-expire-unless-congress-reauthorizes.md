<p>The <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/senate" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Senate</a> Judiciary Committee held a hearing Wednesday about a controversial surveillance authority set to expire later this year unless Congress moves to reauthorize it.</p><p>Committee Chair <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/chuck-grassley" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Chuck Grassley</a>, R-Iowa, indicated the Foreign Intelligence Surveillance Act Section 702 is a "vital" tool for national security.</p><p>"There is no doubt that section 702 is valuable," ranking member <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/person/d/sen-dick-durbin" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Dick Durbin</a>, D-Ill., said.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/grassley-presses-fbi-over-trump-arctic-frost-probe-name-calls-change-anything-random" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>GRASSLEY PRESSES FBI OVER TRUMP ARCTIC FROST PROBE NAME, CALLS CHANGE ‘ANYTHING BUT RANDOM’</strong></a></p><p>But Durbin added that "for years, the government has" utilized "it as a domestic spying tool to collect millions, maybe billions of Americans' private … communications.</p><p>"Section 702 has been abused to spy on business and religious leaders, political parties, members of Congress, campaign donors, journalists and political protesters of all stripes," he added.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/trump-takes-aim-senate-blue-slip-tradition-gop-resists-change" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>TRUMP TAKES AIM AT SENATE ‘BLUE SLIP’ TRADITION AS GOP RESISTS CHANGE</strong></a></p><p>Durbin argued FISA 702 lacks adequate "safeguards."</p><p>"It is my view, when 702 was authorized in 2008, and it remains my view now, that this section does not contain sufficient safeguards to protect the privacy and civil liberties of Americans," he said.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/dick-durbin-no-2-senate-democrat-wont-seek-re-election" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>DICK DURBIN, NO. 2 SENATE DEMOCRAT, WON'T SEEK RE-ELECTION</strong></a></p><p>According to <a href="https://www.intel.gov/foreign-intelligence-surveillance-act/fisa-section-702" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">intel.gov</a>, "Section 702 only permits the targeting of non-United States persons who are reasonably believed to be located outside the United States. United States persons and anyone in the United States may not be targeted under Section 702." </p>
