<p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/entertainment/genres/country" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Country singer</a> Chase Rice is hitting the brakes after more than a decade of touring.</p><p>In an emotional <a href="https://www.instagram.com/p/DTOon_Kjmjn/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">Instagram</a> post, the 40-year-old country star announced he will step away from music after completing a limited run of shows slated for 2026, admitting he has reached a point of exhaustion. </p><p>"Alright everybody, I wasn't sure whether I was gonna talk about this or not but here we are. I've been touring for 13 years and I've lived a dream far greater than I could've ever expected," Rice began.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/entertainment/chase-rice-reflects-career-journey-country-music-nascar-pit-crew" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>COUNTRY SINGER CHASE RICE REFLECTS ON CAREER JOURNEY WORKING FOR A NASCAR PIT CREW</strong></a></p><p>Rice was quick to shut down speculation that he was <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/entertainment/grammy-winner-nelly-furtado-quits-performing-foreseeable-future-body-shaming-criticism" target="_blank" rel="noopener">walking away</a> for good.</p><p>"This isn't a goodbye thing or anything like that to be clear upfront, although it may feel like that, but I'm exhausted," he said.</p><p>He explained that the nonstop pace has dulled his connection to the stage and to the music that built his career.</p><p>"I haven't been able to be myself on stage in quite a while and really enjoy music and why I got into it in the first place," he wrote, adding, "I love songs, I love living them, hearing stories from other people, and figuring out how to put that life into music."</p><p>After more than a decade of life on tour buses and backstage hallways, Rice said being on the road has taken an emotional and physical toll.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/entertainment/country-music-duo-maddie-tae-shock-fans-announce-heartbreaking-split-after-15-years-together" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>COUNTRY MUSIC DUO MADDIE &amp; TAE SHOCK FANS AS THEY ANNOUNCE 'HEARTBREAKING' SPLIT AFTER 15 YEARS TOGETHER</strong></a></p><p>"After 13 years it's finally beat me up to the point where I need to step away for a while," he admitted.
