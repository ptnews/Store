<p>President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/donald-trump" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Donald Trump</a>'s granddaughter, Kai Trump, revealed on social media Wednesday she underwent surgery to repair a wrist injury. </p><p>Kai is an active golfer and <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/sports/kai-trump-makes-lpga-debut-what-know-about-presidents-granddaughters-big-day-golf-course" target="_blank" rel="noopener">made her LPGA debut</a> in November at The Annika in Belleair, Florida. </p><p>"Today, I underwent left wrist surgery to address injuries to the stabilizing tissues of my ECU tendon and damage to my fibrocartilage complex. The surgery was successfully performed at the Lehigh Valley Orthopedic Institute by Dr. Thomas Graham, Chief of Jefferson Orthopedics. I look forward to my rehab in the coming months, I am excited for the ability to play golf pain free," she wrote. </p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/sports" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>CLICK HERE FOR MORE SPORTS COVERAGE ON FOXNEWS.COM</u></strong></a><i><strong> </strong></i></p><p>The teen wore a bandage on her wrist during a recent interview on Logan Paul's podcast, "Impaulsive." </p><p>Trump finished in last place at The Annika but made a <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/sports/kai-trump-reveals-mental-change-led-improvement-second-day-lpga-debut" target="_blank" rel="noopener">notable improvement in her second day</a> of competition compared to her first day, improving by eight strokes.</p><p>She finished with a two-round score of 18-over 158, putting her 27 shots behind leader Grace Kim (65-66) and 17 away from the projected cut line at one over par.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/sports/kai-trump-granddaughter-donald-trump-reveals-she-100-wants-pro-golfer" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>KAI TRUMP, GRANDDAUGHTER OF DONALD TRUMP, REVEALS SHE '100%' WANTS TO BE A PRO GOLFER</strong></a></p><p>"For the first day, I was definitely really nervous.
