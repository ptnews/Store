<p>Hawaii’s "Green Fee" bill was set to raise tourist taxes quite substantially to help fund "climate change" mitigation this year — but now a judge has halted the provision. </p><p>The Cruise Lines International Association (CLIA), along with a cruise ship supplier, filed a <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/travel/high-tourist-tax-hawaii-designed-raise-funds-climate-change-challenged-lawsuit" target="_blank" rel="noopener">lawsuit in early September</a> challenging state tax and county finance officials in the Aloha State, as Fox News Digital previously reported.</p><p>On Dec. 31, the Ninth U.S. Circuit Court of Appeals issued an injunction pending appeal. </p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/travel/historic-landmark-begin-charging-foreign-visitors-overcrowding-battles" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>HISTORIC LANDMARK TO BEGIN CHARGING FOREIGN VISITORS AMID OVERCROWDING BATTLES </strong></a></p><p>It temporarily blocks enforcement of the cruise-ship tax while the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/judiciary/appeals" target="_blank" rel="noopener">appeals proceed</a>, The Associated Press (AP) reported.</p><p>The Transient Accommodations Tax (TAT) planned to raise the tax on tourists who <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/travel/general/hotels" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">stay at Hawaii hotels</a> while imposing a new 11% tax on cruises, according to the text of the bill.</p><p>Toni Schwartz, spokesperson for the Hawaii attorney general’s office, told AP the office "remain[s] confident that Act 96 is lawful and will be vindicated when the appeal is heard on the merits."</p><p>Fox News Digital reached out to the CLIA for comment.</p><p>The association previously told Fox News Digital it "believe[s] the extension violates both the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/constitution" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">U.S. Constitution</a> and federal law, while imposing an additional financial burden on passengers already subject to substantial fees and taxes."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/newsletters?cmpid=fnfirstnl" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>CLICK HERE TO SIGN UP FOR OUR LIFESTYLE NEWSLETTER</strong></a></p><p>The association also pointed out the benefits that <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/travel/general/cruises" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">cruise tourism</a> brings as a major economic driver of the state.</p><p>"Extending the TAT to cruise passengers threatens to <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/travel/general/travel-tips" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">deter visitors</a> whose spending fuels this economic engine, risking job losses and eroding the financial stability of businesses dependent on tourism," CLIA added.</p><p>The lawsuit points out that the state's counties each add their own 3% surcharge on top of the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/finance/taxes" target="_blank" rel="noopener">state's tax</a> — bringing the total tax to 14%.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/lifestyle" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>CLICK HERE FOR MORE LIFESTYLE STORIES</strong></a></p><p>Portions of the revenue raised would go into the "<a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/science/planet-earth/climate" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">Climate Mitigation</a> and Resiliency Special Fund" and the "Economic Development and Revitalization Special Fund," according to the bill.</p><p>The bill addresses invasive species, wildlife conservation, and <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/travel/general/beach" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">beach management</a> and restoration. </p><p>It will also help fund a "green jobs youth corps," as well as areas of environmental concern.</p><p>In 2024, 168,123 visitors traveled to Hawaii by cruise ship, according to the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/travel" target="_blank"
