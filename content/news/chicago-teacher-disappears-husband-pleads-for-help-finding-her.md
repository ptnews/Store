<p>A Chicago <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/education/teachers" target="_blank" rel="noopener">special education teacher</a> has disappeared, leaving her family fearful and desperate for answers.</p><p>Linda Brown, 53, was last seen on Saturday in Chicago's Bronzeville neighborhood, police said.</p><p>Brown was heading to the Wicker Park area for an acupuncture appointment but never arrived, according to a missing person flyer the Chicago Teachers Union posted online.</p><p>Brown’s husband, Antwon Brown, told <a href="https://www.fox32chicago.com/news/husband-pleads-for-help-to-find-missing-cps-teacher" target="_blank" rel="noopener">FOX32 Chicago</a> that he and his wife watched a movie before going to bed early Friday night. When he woke up the next day, Brown was already gone.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/slender-man-stabbers-escape-proves-she-should-never-have-been-released-expert-says" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>‘SLENDER MAN’ STABBER’S ESCAPE PROVES SHE ‘SHOULD NEVER HAVE BEEN RELEASED,’ EXPERT SAYS</strong></a></p><p>"I'm broke down, I don't know what to do," he told the station. "I've done everything. I'm talking to people; we got people searching for her, I'm out of options. I don't know what to do." </p><p>Antwon Brown said that it was out of character for his wife not to show up to the scheduled acupuncture appointment.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/missing-south-carolina-teen-mackenzie-dalton-found-safe-after-monthlong-search-leads-arrest" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>MISSING SOUTH CAROLINA TEEN MACKENZIE DALTON FOUND SAFE AFTER MONTHLONG SEARCH LEADS TO ARREST</strong></a></p><p>"I woke up at 8:45 a.m. and she was gone," he said. "She took her purse and credit cards, but I'm thinking she was at acupuncture." </p><p>The family <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/police-and-law-enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener">called police</a> on Sunday after multiple calls and messages to Brown went unanswered.</p><p>Antwon Brown said Linda, a special education teacher at Robert Healy Elementary School in Bridgeport, has a history of <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/health/mental-health" target="_blank" rel="noopener">mental health issues</a>. He said this was the first time she has gone missing.</p><p>Police said Brown may be driving a blue Honda Civic with <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/us-regions/midwest/illinois" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Illinois plates</a> and that she may need medical attention.</p>
