<p>Tommy Lee Jones' daughter, Victoria Jones, was <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/entertainment/events/arrest" target="_blank" rel="noopener">facing multiple charges</a> prior to her death. </p><p>According to court documents obtained by Fox News Digital, Victoria — who was found dead on Jan. 1 from a suspected overdose — was arrested in Santa Cruz, Calif., on May 14, 2025, and charged with public intoxication and resisting a police officer. The documents show that Victoria had a pretrial conference scheduled for Jan. 27. </p><p>The 34-year-old was found dead inside the Fairmont San Francisco on Jan. 1, Fox News Digital confirmed at the time. She may have suffered a <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/drugs" target="_blank" rel="noopener">drug overdose</a>, according to 911 audio.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/entertainment/tommy-lee-joness-daughter-victoria-found-dead-suspected-overdose-san-francisco-hotel" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>TOMMY LEE JONES' DAUGHTER VICTORIA FOUND DEAD IN SUSPECTED OVERDOSE AT SAN FRANCISCO HOTEL</strong></a></p><p>The <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/police-and-law-enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener">San Francisco Fire Department</a> confirmed to Fox News Digital that SFFD units responded to a call originating from the hotel around 2:52 a.m. on Jan. 1 for a medical emergency.</p><p>When paramedics arrived, they performed an assessment and declared an unnamed person dead at the scene.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/newsletters?cmpid=fnfirstnl" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>CLICK HERE TO SIGN UP FOR THE ENTERTAINMENT NEWSLETTER</u></strong></a></p><p>The call for the incident allegedly involving Victoria was classified as a "code 3 <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/health/mental-health/addiction" target="_blank" rel="noopener">for the overdose</a>, color change," according to 911 audio obtained by <a href="https://people.com/tommy-lee-jones-daughter-victoria-died-of-suspected-overdose-11878415" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener"><u>People</u></a>.</p><p>The <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/san-francisco" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><u>San Francisco</u></a> Police Department also responded to the scene, Fox News Digital confirmed.
