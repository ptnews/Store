<p>An anti-U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) agitator in <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/us-regions/southeast/florida" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Florida</a> was arrested after allegedly punching a trooper in the face during an immigration enforcement operation. </p><p>"This is Jennifer Cruz of Jacksonville," Florida Attorney General James Uthmeier wrote in an X post, sharing an image of the woman, who appeared to smile as law enforcement restrained her. </p><p>Moments before she cracked a grin, video shows Cruz kicking toward a female officer while other law enforcement members had already placed her hands behind her back.</p><p>"Jennifer disagrees with immigration enforcement and decided to commit a few felonies by getting out of her car and punching a Trooper in the face. But unlike Minnesota, we don’t put up with this nonsense. Not today, Jennifer," <a href="https://x.com/AGJamesUthmeier/status/2011800589882061213" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">Uthmeier wrote.</a></p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/sen-john-fetterman-calls-democrats-resist-advocacy-extreme-stances-like-abolishing-ice" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>SEN JOHN FETTERMAN CALLS FOR DEMOCRATS TO ‘RESIST’ ADVOCACY OF ‘EXTREME’ STANCES LIKE ABOLISHING ICE</strong></a></p><p>Footage shows a chaotic scene as <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/police-and-law-enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener">authorities</a> attempted to detain Cruz. As several officers placed her hands behind her back and walked her toward one of their vehicles, Cruz is seen attempting to kick another female officer nearby. Cruz then smirks as the officers detaining her place her on the hood of a vehicle. Once placed in the back of the vehicle, </p><p>A News4JAX reporter spoke to Juan Alvarez, the owner of the Mi Pueblo grocery store which was near where the incident occurred. He said he witnessed a traffic stop involving a state trooper. </p><p>"ICE agents showed up with the state trooper. They detained the driver," <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XbEEABSLVXA" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">he said.</a> </p><p>"And so yeah, they had an operation going on. And after that, they had detained another person. But it seems they got into an altercation with that person. It turned violent," he said, noting that more law enforcement showed up.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/video/6387774692112" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>RON DESANTIS BACKS ADMINISTRATIVE LEAVE FOR JACKSONVILLE OFFICIAL WHO WARNED PUBLIC OF ICE PRESENCE</strong></a></p><p>Florida Gov. <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/ron-desantis" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Ron DeSantis</a> shared Uthmeier's post and wrote, "Really bad decision to attack one of our troopers. This isn’t Minneapolis…" </p><p>DeSantis also mentioned the incident at an unrelated press conference on Thursday. </p><p>"I do know that there was a woman who came out and assaulted one of our troopers," DeSantis said. "Let me just tell you guys this is not Minneapolis. That is not going to end well for you in Florida."</p><p>Rep. Anna Paulina Luna, R-Fla., <a href="https://x.com/RepLuna/status/2011839566416589029" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">quipped in a post,</a> "Never go full Jennifer."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/watch-ice-takes-down-illegal-alien-who-rammed-law-enforcement-vehicles-nearly-running-over-officer" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>WATCH: ICE TAKES DOWN ILLEGAL ALIEN WHO ALLEGEDLY RAMMED LAW ENFORCEMENT VEHICLES, NEARLY RUNNING OVER OFFICER</strong></a></p><p>"This is how it’s done.
