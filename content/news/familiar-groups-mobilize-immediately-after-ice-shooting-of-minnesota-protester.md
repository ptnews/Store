<p>Within hours of a Minnesota protester being fatally shot after allegedly attempting to run over <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/immigration" target="_blank" rel="noopener">ICE agents</a>, a familiar network of far-left protesters for causes ranging from communism to climate change mobilized across the country.</p><p>The unidentified woman, who Minneapolis Mayor Jacob Frey said was 37 years old, was killed at the wheel of her car just after 11 a.m. ET. Federal authorities said she had <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/ice-involved-minnesota-shooting-vehicle-ramming-incident-dhs" target="_blank" rel="noopener">tried to run over ICE agents</a> who were part of a 2,000-strong team sent to the Twin Cities to round up and deport illegal immigrant criminals.</p><p>"I have just viewed the clip of the event which took place in <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/minneapolis-st-paul" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Minneapolis, Minnesota</a>.
