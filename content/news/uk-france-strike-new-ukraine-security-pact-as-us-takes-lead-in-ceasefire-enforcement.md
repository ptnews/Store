<p>The U.K. and France signed a declaration Tuesday pledging troops for Ukraine under a future peace deal and with security guarantees supported by the U.S. and allied partners.</p><p>The declaration was adopted in Paris by the Coalition of the Willing and sets out what leaders said was a <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/us-officials-tout-progress-talks-reach-lasting-durable-peace-between-ukraine-russia" target="_blank" rel="noopener">framework for lasting peace</a> between Ukraine and Russia, set in international law and the principles of the United Nations Charter.</p><p>Russia’s full-scale invasion of Ukraine in 2022 triggered Europe’s largest conflict since World War II.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/zelenskyy-says-fresh-russian-attack-ukraine-shows-putins-true-attitude-ahead-trump-meeting" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>ZELENSKYY SAYS FRESH RUSSIAN ATTACK ON UKRAINE SHOWS PUTIN'S 'TRUE ATTITUDE' AHEAD OF TRUMP MEETING</strong></a></p><p>The new agreement says that Ukraine’s sovereignty and its ability to defend itself are non-negotiable elements of any peace deal and warned that its self-defense is essential to its own security and wider Euro-Atlantic stability.</p><p>Under the plan, a <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/uk-ready-send-troops-jets-ships-trump-clinches-ukraine-ceasefire-defense-chief-says" target="_blank" rel="noopener">multinational force for Ukraine</a> would be deployed once a ceasefire is in place, aimed at deterring any Russian aggression and supporting the rebuilding of Ukraine’s military.</p><p>The force would be European-led with proposed support from the U.S.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/russia-warns-may-reject-us-ukraine-peace-plan-fails-uphold-alaska-summit-understandings" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>RUSSIA WARNS IT MAY REJECT US-UKRAINE PEACE PLAN IF IT FAILS TO UPHOLD ALASKA SUMMIT 'UNDERSTANDINGS'</strong></a></p><p>The declaration also commits the Coalition to <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/media/nato-ambassador-says-ukraine-peace-deal-could-on-cusp-nations-near-finalization-security-pact" target="_blank" rel="noopener">security guarantees that would</a> be activated once a ceasefire begins.</p><p>These include commitments to support Ukraine militarily, diplomatically and economically in the event of a future armed attack by Russia.</p><p>A key U.S. role is outlined in plans for a continuous, <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/defense" target="_blank" rel="noopener">U.S.-led ceasefire monitoring</a><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/ukraine-us-near-20-point-peace-deal-putin-spurns-zelenskyy-christmas-ceasefire-offer" target="_blank" rel="noopener"> and verification</a> mechanism, with contributions from partners.</p><p>The U.S. would participate in a special commission to manage ceasefire breaches, attribute responsibility and determine solutions.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/ukraine-russia-crossroads-how-war-evolved-2025-what-comes-next" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>UKRAINE–RUSSIA AT A CROSSROADS: HOW THE WAR EVOLVED IN 2025 AND WHAT COMES NEXT</u></strong></a></p><p>Coalition members also agreed to carry on with <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/defense/conflicts" target="_blank" rel="noopener">long-term military support</a> for Ukraine and pledged defense cooperation, including training, defense production and intelligence sharing.</p><p>Leaders also announced the creation of a permanent <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/trumps-team-reports-concrete-progress-ukraine-peace-negotiations-european-partners" target="_blank" rel="noopener">U.S.-Ukraine-Coalition coordination cell</a> based at the Coalition’s headquarters in Paris.</p><p>The declaration was unveiled at a joint news conference by French President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/emmanuel-macron" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Emmanuel Macron</a>, Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy and U.K. Prime Minister Keir Starmer.</p><p>This followed talks in Paris which were attended by Jared Kushner and the U.S. special envoy, Steve Witkoff.</p>
