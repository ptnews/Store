father turned him in to police, authorities said.</p><p>Lequan Stephens, 16, faces charges of aggravated assault, according to the Clayton County Sheriff’s Office.</p><p>The <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/police-and-law-enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Lovejoy Police Department</a> said officers responded Jan. 17 to a shooting in the 2500 block seat of a vehicle suffering from gunshot wounds to the face and neck.</p><p>Investigators said the suspect fired into the vehicle, striking the victim, before fleeing the scene.</p><p>Authorities later identified Stephens as the alleged shooter and obtained warrants for his arrest.</p><p><a
