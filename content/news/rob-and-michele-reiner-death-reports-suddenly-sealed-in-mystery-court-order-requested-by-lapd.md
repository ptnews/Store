<p>Rob and Michele Reiner's death reports have been sealed by <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/finance/investigations" target="_blank" rel="noopener">court order,</a> which was "initiated by" the Los Angeles Police Department.</p><p>On Monday, the Los Angeles County Medical Examiner posted <a href="https://me.lacounty.gov/2025/press-releases/statement-on-medical-examiner-case-numbers-2025-19480-and-2025-19481/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">a press release</a> stating that although Michele and Rob's cause of death reports were already made public, they have removed them.</p><p>"While the cause and manner of death were previously released on these cases, due to the court order, the information is no longer available. No other case information or records, including the Medical Examiner report, can be released or posted on the website until further notice," the press release stated, noting that a "security hold" has been placed for the time being.</p><p>The press release stated that the Medical Examiner's department received the court order on December 24 at 10:30 am.</p><p> The Medical Examiner's office noted that more information will be shared once the court order has been lifted.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/entertainment/rob-reiner-wife-micheles-death-certificates-released" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>ROB REINER AND WIFE MICHELE'S DEATH CERTIFICATES RELEASED</strong></a></p><p>Fox News Digital has reached out to <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/police-and-law-enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener">the LAPD</a> for comment.</p><p>On Dec. 23, <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/entertainment/rob-reiner-wife-micheles-death-certificates-released" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Rob and Michele's death certificates </a>were released.</p><p>According to the death certificates obtained by Fox News Digital from the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/los-angeles" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><u>Los Angeles County Public Health Department</u></a>, the couple were cremated at Mount Sinai Mortuary. </p><p>The certificates also confirmed what was revealed last week by the Los Angeles County <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/entertainment/rob-michele-reiners-bodies-released-family-after-autopsies" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><u>Medical Examiner's office</u></a> that the Reiners died due to multiple sharp force injuries.
