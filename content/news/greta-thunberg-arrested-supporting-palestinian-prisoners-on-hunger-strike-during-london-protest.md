<p>Climate activist <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/greta-thunberg" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Greta Thunberg</a> was arrested Tuesday in London while supporting pro-Palestinian activists in British prisons staging a hunger strike.</p><p>Prisoners for Palestine, the protest group, released a video showing the 22-year-old Swede holding a sign saying: "I support Palestine Action prisoners. I oppose genocide," referring to the war in Gaza between Israel and Hamas. </p><p>She was later released on bail, the group said on X. </p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/greta-thunbergs-flotilla-bombarded-abba-music-radios-hijacked-report" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>GRETA THUNBERG’S FLOTILLA BOMBARDED WITH ABBA MUSIC AFTER RADIOS HIJACKED: REPORT</u></strong></a></p><p>The protest was in support of the group on hunger strike, Palestine Action, which has been banned in Britain as a terrorist organization. </p><p>The protests were part of a larger demonstration in which two other activists sprayed red paint in front of an insurance company <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/world-regions/united-kingdom" target="_blank" rel="noopener">in London</a>.</p><p>Prisoners for Palestine says they targeted the insurer because it supports the Israel-linked defense firm Elbit Systems.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/greta-thunbergs-alleged-jail-complaints-called-brazen-lies-israeli-government" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>GRETA THUNBERG'S ALLEGED JAIL COMPLAINTS CALLED 'BRAZEN LIES' BY ISRAELI GOVERNMENT</u></strong></a></p><p>A man and a woman have been arrested on suspicion of criminal damage. A third woman was later arrested on suspicion of supporting a banned organization. </p><p>Eight Palestine Action members have staged a hunger strike to protest their detention <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/crime" target="_blank" rel="noopener">without bail</a>. They are awaiting trial on charges related to earlier protests. </p><p>Two of the members have been on hunger strike for 52 days and are at a "critical stage, where death is a real possibility," Prisoners for Palestine said in a statement.</p><p>Last year, Thunberg was <a href="https://apnews.com/article/greta-thunberg-acquitted-blocking-oil-conference-583472826d0181e2562045864e16c8af">acquitted</a> by a London court of refusing to follow a police order to leave a protest that was blocking the entrance to an oil and gas industry conference.</p><p><i>The Associated Press contributed to this report. </i></p>
