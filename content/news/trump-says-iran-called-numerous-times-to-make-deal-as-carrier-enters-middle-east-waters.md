<p>President Donald Trump said Iran appears to be looking to negotiate with the U.S. amid a growing military buildup in the Middle East.</p><p>In a Monday interview with Axios, Trump suggested that Tehran had reached out on "numerous occasions" and "want[s] to <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/state-and-local/federal-relations" target="_blank" rel="noopener">make a deal."</a></p><p>"They want to make a deal. I know so. They called on numerous occasions. They want to talk," the president told the outlet.</p><p>According to U.S. officials, also cited <a href="https://www.axios.com/2026/01/26/trump-iran-deal-strike-protests" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">by Axios</a>, any potential agreement would need Tehran to remove all enriched uranium, cap its long-range missile stockpile, a change in support for regional proxy forces, and cease independent uranium enrichment, terms Iranian leaders have not agreed to.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/israeli-un-ambassador-sends-stark-warning-iran-amid-growing-unrest" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>ISRAELI UN AMBASSADOR SENDS STARK WARNING TO IRAN AMID GROWING UNREST</strong></a></p><p>Trump also described the situation with Iran as "in flux," and pointed to the arrival of what he called "<a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/military/navy" target="_blank" rel="noopener">a big armada next to Iran</a>. Bigger than Venezuela," referencing the recent deployment of U.S. naval assets.</p><p>As previously reported by Fox News Digital, the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/uss-abraham-lincoln-aircraft-carrier-strike-group-makes-move-threat-iran" target="_blank" rel="noopener">USS Abraham Lincoln aircraft carrier</a> entered CENTCOM waters in the Indian Ocean on Monday amid increasing threats from Iran, a senior U.S. official said.</p><p>Trump had told reporters Jan. 21, "We have a big flotilla going in that direction, and we’ll see what happens. We have a <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/us-sending-military-assets-middle-east-trump-weighs-iran-strike-sources-say" target="_blank" rel="noopener">big force going towards Iran</a>. I’d rather not see anything happen, but we’re watching them very closely."</p><p>The U.S. military buildup comes amid <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/world-protests" target="_blank" rel="noopener">widespread unrest inside Iran following protests</a> that began Dec. 28.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/secret-service-aware-after-iranian-state-tv-airs-trump-threat-featuring-photo-butler-assassination-attempt" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>SECRET SERVICE AWARE AFTER IRANIAN STATE TV AIRS TRUMP THREAT FEATURING PHOTO OF BUTLER ASSASSINATION ATTEMPT</strong></a></p><p>According to a recent report from <a href="https://www.en-hrana.org/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">Human Rights Activists News Agency</a> (HRANA), the confirmed death toll from <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/foreign-policy/human-rights" target="_blank" rel="noopener">the protests</a> has reached 5,848, with an additional 17,091 deaths under investigation.</p><p>Supreme Leader Ayatollah <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/ali-khamenei" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Ali Khamenei</a> has been sheltering in a fortified underground facility, according to Iran International.</p><p>Trump is expected to hold further consultations this week, Axios said, before adding that White House officials said an attack is still on the table.</p><p>Fox News Digital has reached out to the White House for comment.</p>
