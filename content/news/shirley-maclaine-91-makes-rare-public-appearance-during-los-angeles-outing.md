<p>Legendary actress, <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/entertainment/shirley-maclaine-says-hollywood-100-different-today-glamours-gone-out" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Shirley MacLaine</a>, was spotted out during a rare public outing in Los Angeles.</p><p>The 91-year-old "Postcards from the Edge" star was photographed leaving Nobu in Malibu, Calif. on Sunday, Jan. 18.</p><p>In the photograph, MacLaine can be seen walking arm in arm with a young man, who was wearing a red T-shirt and jeans. MacLaine was sporting a green T-shirt and khaki pants.</p><p>Over the years, sightings of the iconic actress have become rarer. However, she was also spotted getting a bite to eat with some friends earlier this month.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/entertainment/sissy-spacek-credits-stage-name-her-brothers-southern-roots" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>SISSY SPACEK CREDITS STAGE NAME TO HER BROTHERS, SOUTHERN ROOTS</strong></a></p><p>MacLaine got her start as an actress in 1955, making her film debut in Alfred Hitchcock's "The Trouble with Harry."
