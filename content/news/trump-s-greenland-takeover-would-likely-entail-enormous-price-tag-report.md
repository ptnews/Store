<p>President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/donald-trump" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Donald Trump</a>’s push for the U.S. to acquire Greenland could carry an estimated price tag of $700 billion, according to reports.</p><p>The estimate, reported by <a href="https://www.nbcnews.com/politics/white-house/buying-greenland-cost-much-700-billion-rcna253921" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">NBC News</a>, was said to have been calculated by scholars and former U.S. officials involved in early planning discussions surrounding Trump’s interest in acquiring the 800,000-square-mile island as a strategic buffer in the Arctic against adversaries such as Russia and China.</p><p>The figure would exceed half of the War Department’s annual budget and reflects what officials described as a significant <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/executive/national-security" target="_blank" rel="noopener">national security priority</a> for the Trump administration.</p><p>The reported cost comes as new <a href="https://www.reuters.com/world/europe/just-one-five-americans-support-trumps-efforts-acquire-greenland-reutersipsos-2026-01-14/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">Reuters</a> polling shows the Trump proposal is unpopular with Americans, as it also continues to strain relations with U.S. allies.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/european-allies-working-plan-us-acts-acquiring-greenland-report" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>EUROPEAN ALLIES WORKING ON PLAN IF US ACTS ON ACQUIRING GREENLAND: REPORT</strong></a></p><p>"The United States needs Greenland for the purpose of national security," Trump wrote Wednesday in a Truth Social post, warning that if the U.S. does not act, "Russia or China will."</p><p>The president added that "anything less" than U.S. control of Greenland is "unacceptable" and argued that the Arctic territory is vital to American defense interests, including the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/golden-dome-missile-shield-tested-trump-key-american-territory" target="_blank" rel="noopener">"Golden Dome" missile defense system</a> in development.</p><p>Trump's renewed push has increased tensions with Denmark and other NATO allies.</p><p>As previously reported by Fox News Digital, <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/troops-from-europe-deploy-greenland-rapid-2-day-mission-trump-eyes-us-takeover" target="_blank" rel="noopener">troops from several European countries</a> deployed to Greenland Thursday for a two-day mission aimed at boosting the territory’s defenses.</p><p>France, Germany, Sweden and Norway are participating in the exercise, Fox News said, with leaders saying the mission is intended to demonstrate NATO’s ability to deploy military assets quickly in the Arctic.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/trump-issues-stern-warning-nato-ahead-vances-high-stakes-greenland-meeting" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>TRUMP ISSUES STERN WARNING TO NATO AHEAD OF VANCE'S HIGH-STAKES GREENLAND MEETING</strong></a></p><p>NBC News also reported that Secretary of State <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/marco-rubio" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Marco Rubio</a> has been tasked with developing a proposal to purchase Greenland that will be presented to Trump in the coming weeks.</p><p>Rubio and Vice President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/jd-vance" target="_blank" rel="noopener">JD Vance</a> are also expected to meet with officials from Denmark and Greenland in Washington, D.C., on Wednesday.</p><p>Trump has vowed to acquire Greenland "one way or the other," a remark Danish and Greenlandic officials have sharply rejected.</p><p>Danish leaders have warned that any <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/bipartisan-lawmakers-propose-bill-block-military-action-against-nato-members-amid-threats-take-greenland" target="_blank" rel="noopener">military action against another NATO member</a> could jeopardize the alliance itself.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/greenland-leaders-push-back-trumps-calls-us-control-island-we-dont-want-americans" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>GREENLAND LEADERS PUSH BACK ON TRUMP'S CALLS FOR US CONTROL OF THE ISLAND: 'WE DON’T WANT TO BE AMERICANS'</strong></a></p><p>A <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/only-1-5-americans-support-us-effort-acquire-greenland-poll-finds" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Reuters/Ipsos poll also found</a> just 17% of Americans approve of Trump’s efforts to acquire Greenland, with 47% opposed, and 35% unsure.</p><p>Greenland is the world’s largest island and has a population of roughly 56,000 people, with most living along its ice-free coastline. About 80% of the territory is covered by ice caps and glaciers.</p><p>Fishing, hunting, whaling, sealing and tourism are the backbone of its local economy.</p><p>Fox News Digital has reached out to the White House for comment.</p>
