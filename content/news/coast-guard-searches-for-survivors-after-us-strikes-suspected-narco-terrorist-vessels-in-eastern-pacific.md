<p>The <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/military/coast-guard" target="_blank" rel="noopener">U.S. Coast Guard</a> is continuing to search for survivors after a strike Tuesday targeting three suspected narco-trafficking vessels traveling as a convoy in the Eastern Pacific.</p><p>The ships, allegedly operated by <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/terrorism/narco-terror" target="_blank" rel="noopener">designated terrorist organizations</a>, were traveling in international waters and allegedly transferred narcotics between the three vessels prior to the strikes.</p><p>Three narco-terrorists were reportedly killed aboard the first vessel, while those on the remaining two abandoned ship before follow-on engagements sank the vessels.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/us-military-destroys-narco-terror-convoy-three-vessels-sea-kinetic-strikes" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>US MILITARY DESTROYS NARCO-TERROR CONVOY OF THREE VESSELS AT SEA IN KINETIC STRIKES</u></strong></a></p><p>It is unclear how many <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/topic/missing-persons" target="_blank" rel="noopener">survivors escaped the vessels</a>.</p><p>A U.S. Coast Guard spokesperson told Fox News officials began coordinating search efforts at about 3 p.m. Tuesday after receiving notification from the Department of War of people in the water approximately 400 nautical miles southwest of the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/immigration/mexico" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Mexico and Guatemala border</a>.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/maduro-says-venezuela-ready-make-deal-us-drugs-oil-after-military-strikes" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>MADURO SAYS VENEZUELA IS ‘READY’ TO MAKE DEAL WITH US ON DRUGS AND OIL AFTER MILITARY STRIKES</strong></a></p><p>A HC-130J aircraft was deployed from Sacramento, <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/us-regions/west/california" target="_blank" rel="noopener">California</a>, to search an area covering more than 1,000 nautical miles and issued an urgent marine information broadcast to mariners in the area, according to the spokesperson.</p><p>As of Friday, the Coast Guard has coordinated more than 65 hours of search efforts, including working with <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/united-nations/partners" target="_blank" rel="noopener">partner nations</a> and commercial fishing and Automated Mutual-Assistance Vessel Rescue (AMVER) system vessels.</p><p>On Wednesday, five suspected narco-terrorists were killed in a U.S. military strike against two vessels allegedly operated by designated terrorist organizations involved in <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/terrorism/narco-terror" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><u>narcotics trafficking</u></a>.</p><p><i>Fox News Digital's Jasmine Baehr contributed to this story.</i></p>
