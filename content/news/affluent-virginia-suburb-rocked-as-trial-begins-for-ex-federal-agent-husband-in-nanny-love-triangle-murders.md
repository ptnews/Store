<p>Opening statements are expected to begin Tuesday in the trial of Brendan Banfield, a former IRS special agent who is accused of orchestrating a grisly double murder to conceal an affair with his family's au pair.</p><p>Twelve jurors and four alternates were seated Monday as the trial begins, <a href="https://www.fox5dc.com/news/jury-seated-brendan-banfield-murder-trial-gets-underway" target="_blank" rel="noopener">FOX 5 reported.</a></p><p>Banfield is charged with aggravated murder in the February 2023 killings of Christine Banfield and Joseph Ryan at the Banfields’ home in the idyllic <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/washington-dc" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Washington, D.C.</a>, suburb of Herndon, Virginia.</p><p>According to <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/trials" target="_blank" rel="noopener">court records</a>, Banfield and then-22-year-old Brazilian au pair Juliana Peres Magalhães were present when his wife and Ryan were killed in the home's primary bedroom. At the time, the au pair told investigators they had discovered Ryan — a stranger — stabbing Banfield's wife and had both opened fire to stop the intruder.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/affluent-virginia-husband-nanny-charged-murders-mansion-love-triangle" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>AFFLUENT VIRGINIA HUSBAND, NANNY CHARGED WITH MURDERS IN MANSION LOVE TRIANGLE</u></strong></a></p><p>Prosecutors, however, allege a more <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/crime" target="_blank" rel="noopener">calculated plot</a>. They say that Banfield and Magalhães lured Ryan to the house and staged the scene to make the homicides appear like an act of self-defense.</p><p>Prosecutors have said Banfield was impersonating his wife, Christine, in an online account on a fetish website. Prosecutors said the husband lured Ryan to the couple’s Herndon-area home as part of his plot to kill his wife.</p><p>"It is a theory in search of facts rather than a series of facts supporting a theory," John Carroll, Banfield’s attorney, previously said.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/virginia-au-pair-murder-fetish-plot-affair-gun-range-tied-double-homicide-home-prosecutors-say" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>VIRGINIA AU PAIR MURDER: FETISH PLOT, AFFAIR, GUN RANGE TIED TO DOUBLE HOMICIDE AT HOME, PROSECUTORS REVEAL</u></strong></a></p><p>When police initially responded to a 911 call from the residence in February 2023, they found Christine with <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/homicide" target="_blank" rel="noopener">stab wounds to the neck</a> and Ryan with fatal gunshot wounds in the upstairs bedroom of the home.</p><p>Authorities took Christine to the hospital, where she was later pronounced dead, <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/police-and-law-enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Fairfax County Police Department</a> Chief Kevin Davis said.
