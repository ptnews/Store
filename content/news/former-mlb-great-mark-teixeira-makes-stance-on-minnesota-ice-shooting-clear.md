<p>The <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/live-news/minneapolis-ice-shooting-1-8-2026" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Minnesota ICE shooting</a> that resulted in the death of Renee Good has drawn strong reactions everywhere.</p><p>According to Department of Homeland Security Secretary <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/kristi-noem" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Kristi Noem</a>, Good was operating a vehicle that agents ordered her to exit. Good, according to Noem, refused and "attempted to run them over and ram them with her vehicle."</p><p>After Vice President JD Vance delivered a strong statement defending ICE agents for their work, the White House posted a graphic with a portion of Vance's plea and the caption, "STAND WITH ICE."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/sports" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>CLICK HERE FOR MORE SPORTS COVERAGE ON FOXNEWS.COM</strong></a><i><strong> </strong></i></p><p>Former MLB All-Star Mark Teixeira, who launched his campaign for Texas’ 21st Congressional District in August as a Republican to "help defend President Trump’s America First agenda," voiced his support.</p><p><a href="https://x.com/teixeiramark25/status/2009364216604655769?s=46" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">"I stand with ICE,"</a> the former Texas Ranger, Atlanta Brave, Los Angeles Angel and New York Yankee wrote on X.</p><p>Teixeira’s announcement followed Rep. Chip Roy’s decision not to seek re-election and run for Texas attorney general. Roy, who represents the district, made the announcement in a campaign video shared on X last year. His video centered on preserving the Lone Star State's legacy of "liberty, freedom and self-determination."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/sports/us-patent-office-denies-as-trademark-applications-las-vegas-athletics" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>US PATENT OFFICE DENIES A’S TRADEMARK APPLICATIONS FOR ‘LAS VEGAS ATHLETICS’</strong></a></p><p>Noem called the incident "domestic terrorism."</p><p>"An officer of ours acted quickly and defensively, shot to protect himself and the people around him," she said.</p><p>Federal authorities said Good <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/ice-involved-minnesota-shooting-vehicle-ramming-incident-dhs" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><u>tried to run over ICE agents</u></a> who were part of a 2,000-member team sent to the Twin Cities to round up and deport undocumented immigrant criminals.</p><p>Teixeira, 45, played 14 seasons in the majors. He debuted with the Rangers in 2003 but is perhaps best known for being an MVP candidate and World Series champion with the Yankees, the final stop of his career.</p><p>One of the best hitters in the game, Teixeira was a three-time Silver Slugger Award winner and finished his career with a .269 batting average and 409 home runs. He was a five-time Gold Glove winner and was a member of the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/sports/mlb/new-york-yankees" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Yankees’ 2009 World Series</a> championship team. </p><p>The congressional race is set for November 2026, with a primary scheduled for March.</p><p><i>Fox News' Jackson Thompson contributed to this report.</i></p><p><i><strong>Follow Fox News Digital’s </strong></i><a href="https://twitter.com/FoxNewsSports_" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><i><strong>sports coverage on X</strong></i></a><i><strong>, and subscribe to </strong></i><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/newsletters" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><i><strong>the Fox News Sports Huddle newsletter</strong></i></a><i><strong>.</strong></i></p>
