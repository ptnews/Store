<p>The wives of the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/sports/nfl/denver-broncos" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Denver Broncos quarterbacks</a> are certainly going through it.</p><p>Both are undoubtedly counting down the seconds until Sunday’s AFC title game against the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/sports/nfl/new-england-patriots" target="_blank" rel="noopener">New England Patriots</a>, but they have found themselves in very different situations.</p><p>Bo Nix suffered a fractured ankle on the team's final drive of their 33-30 victory over the Buffalo Bills on Saturday night, prompting Jarrett Stidham to get the nod for a potential trip to the Super Bowl.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/sports" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>CLICK HERE FOR MORE SPORTS COVERAGE ON FOXNEWS.COM</strong></a><i><strong> </strong></i></p><p>Nix’s wife, Izzy, "snuck" a photo of Nix meeting with teammates and coaches following the Broncos’ celebration for a personal memento.</p><p>"I snuck this picture because I wanted to remember this moment forever," Izzy wrote on her Instagram Stories.
