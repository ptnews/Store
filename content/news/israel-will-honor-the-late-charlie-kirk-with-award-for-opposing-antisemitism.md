<p>Israel will reportedly honor slain conservative <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/media/charlie-kirk" target="_blank" rel="noopener">activist Charlie Kirk</a> with an award for his efforts battling antisemitism.</p><p>Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu's office indicated that this recognition will take place at the International Conference on Combating Antisemitism, The Associated Press reported.</p><p>Kirk, who founded the conservative organization Turning Point USA, was assassinated while holding an event at Utah Valley University in September.</p><p>"A lion-hearted friend of Israel, he fought the lies and stood tall for Judeo-Christian civilization," <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/benjamin-netanyahu" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Netanyahu said</a> in a post on X on the day Kirk was fatally shot.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/media/grand-theft-auto-blacklists-charlie-kirk-players-try-recreate-his-killing-game" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>GRAND THEFT AUTO BLACKLISTS ‘CHARLIE KIRK’ AS PLAYERS TRY TO RECREATE HIS KILLING IN THE GAME</strong></a></p><p><a href="https://x.com/netanyahu/status/1965888327938158764?lang=en" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">In the post,</a> the Israeli leader called Kirk "an incredible human being" whose "boundless pride in America and his valiant belief in free speech will leave a lasting impact."</p><p>Kirk asserted in a <a href="https://x.com/charliekirk11/status/1960056637185831228" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">post on X</a> less than a month before he was killed, "Jew hate has no place in civil society. It rots the brain, reject it." </p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/netanyahu-rubio-discuss-us-military-intervention-iran-amid-ongoing-nationwide-protests-report" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>NETANYAHU AND RUBIO DISCUSS US MILITARY INTERVENTION IN IRAN AMID ONGOING NATIONWIDE PROTESTS: REPORT</strong></a></p><p>Kirk, who was a <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/world-regions/israel" target="_blank" rel="noopener">supporter of Israel,</a> indicated last year on "The Megyn Kelly Show" that some in the pro-Israel camp had unfairly criticized him.</p><p>"The behavior by a lot, both privately and publicly, are pushing people like you and me away. Not like we're gonna be pro-Hamas," <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=n2qn0mvSCig" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">he said.</a> "But we're like, honestly, the way you are treating me is so repulsive."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/tyler-robinson-prosecutors-say-charlie-kirk-shooting-texts-show-confusion-not-bias-rebut-conflict-claim" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>TYLER ROBINSON PROSECUTORS SAY CHARLIE KIRK SHOOTING TEXTS SHOW CONFUSION, NOT BIAS, TO REBUT CONFLICT CLAIM</strong></a></p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/download" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>CLICK HERE TO GET THE FOX NEWS APP</u></strong></a></p><p>"I have text messages, Megyn, calling me an antisemite. I am learning biblical Hebrew and writing a book on the Shabbat. I honor the Shabbat, literally the Jewish sabbath. I visit Israel and fight for it," he noted at the time.</p><p><i>The Associated Press contributed to this report.</i></p>
