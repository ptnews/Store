<p>A <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/travel/general" target="_blank" rel="noopener">recent report</a> is shining a light on popular destinations considered the "worst" for tourists to visit because they fall short of the hype they've received over the years.  </p><p>Analysts looked at more than 100 tourist attractions around the world and came up with a list of the "worst" destinations that "underwhelm visitors despite their global fame," according to the report.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/travel/holiday-dreams-fall-short-5-americas-most-complained-about-christmas-attractions" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Stasher, a travel company</a> specializing in this <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/travel/vacation-destinations" target="_blank" rel="noopener">sidewalk of celebrity stars</a> had the lowest Google rating and safety score," analysts noted.</p><p>There are over 2,800 stars on the Hollywood Walk of Fame, honoring achievements in <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/entertainment" target="_blank" rel="noopener">film, TV, music</a>, radio and live performance, according to its website.</p><p>The stars on the Hollywood Walk of Fame are located on the sidewalks of Hollywood Boulevard and Vine Street.</p><p><a "Prices in <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/food-drink/food/restaurants" target="_blank" rel="noopener">restaurants and shops</a> are completely different.
