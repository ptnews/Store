<p>Thieves attempted to steal an ATM on Christmas Eve by dragging it through the front of a <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/us-regions/southwest/texas" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Texas</a> convenience store with a stolen SUV and a metal cable, but the machine broke free as they fled, police said.</p><p>The White Settlement Police Department posted footage of two suspects towing an ATM through the front glass doors of a 7-Eleven in Fort Worth before driving away.</p><p>The failed heist happened just before 4 a.m. Wednesday and may be connected to a string of similar convenience store crimes reported across North Texas in recent weeks, police said.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/police-and-law-enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Investigators are asking</a> for the public’s help identifying the suspects as they attempt to determine whether the incident is part of a broader crime pattern.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/brazen-blue-state-jewelry-store-heist-caught-camera-armed-mob-1m-merchandise-police" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>BRAZEN BLUE STATE JEWELRY STORE HEIST CAUGHT ON CAMERA AS ARMED MOB MAKES OFF WITH $1M IN MERCHANDISE: POLICE</strong></a></p><p>White Settlement police said in a statement that two men traveled to the store parking lot in the stolen SUV wearing dark-colored clothing with their faces covered.</p><p>One of the men smashed the front door with a metal object and attached a cable to the ATM that was already hooked to the vehicle, investigators said.</p><p>Officers found <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/aftermath" target="_blank" rel="noopener">extensive damage to the storefront</a>, with debris, shelving and merchandise scattered throughout the store and into the parking lot.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/entertainment/guy-fieris-missing-tequila-mystery-wild-new-details-emerge-after-million-dollar-heist" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>GUY FIERI’S MISSING TEQUILA MYSTERY: WILD NEW DETAILS EMERGE AFTER MILLION-DOLLAR HEIST</strong></a></p><p>Police said it took two attempts for the thieves to get the ATM out of the store.</p><p>The ATM was dislodged from the vehicle as the suspects fled and was later recovered by police.</p><p>The <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/auto/style/suv" target="_blank" rel="noopener">black SUV</a> had been stolen in Dallas, investigators added.</p><p>Police used license plate readers and <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/tech/technologies/cameras" target="_blank" rel="noopener">city cameras</a> throughout the city to try and locate it about a half-mile from the store.</p>
