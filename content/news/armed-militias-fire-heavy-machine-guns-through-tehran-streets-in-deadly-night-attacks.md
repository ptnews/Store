<p>Gunfire echoed through Tehran Tuesday as <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/armed-forces" target="_blank" rel="noopener">heavily armed militias</a> were deployed across the Iranian capital, transforming some districts into fortified zones under intense security.</p><p>Video footage showed bursts of automatic weapons after dark as government buildings, state media sites and major intersections were reportedly placed under guard, with armored pickups and masked fighters patrolling the streets in Toyotas.</p><p>The trucks were mounted with heavy machine guns and were moving in convoys with weapons firing into the darkness as armed men shouted commands.</p><p>In the video, large-caliber guns can be heard rattling as vehicles maneuver through urban streets.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/kurdish-fighters-try-exploit-iran-chaos-regional-threat-grows-amid-nationwide-protests" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>ARMED KURDISH FIGHTERS TRY TO BREACH IRAN BORDER AS REGIONAL THREAT GROWS AMID PROTESTS: REPORTS</strong></a></p><p>"There has been a deployment of dozens of Toyotas mounted with heavy machine guns (DShK) and other heavy weapons <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/conflicts/iran" target="_blank" rel="noopener">in Tehran</a>," Ali Safavi, a senior official with the National Council of Resistance of Iran (NCRI), told Fox News Digital.</p><p>"They are reportedly being used by elements linked to Lebanese Hezbollah and Iraqi Popular Mobilization Forces (PMF)," he said.</p><p>"Their commander speaks in Farsi, and these fighters are <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/world-regions/iraq" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Iraqi Hashd al-Sha’bi</a>, Popular Mobilization Force and Hezbollah fighters who have joined the IRGC. The IRGC are their commanders, and you can hear them shouting in Farsi."</p><p>According to Safavi, the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/conflicts/iran" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Iranian regime</a> has increasingly relied on foreign proxy forces to maintain control of the capital.</p><p>"The regime has brought in at least 5,000 foreign elements now from Iraq and Hezbollah to control Tehran," he explained.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/us-ambassador-warns-iran-emergency-un-meeting-trump-man-action-all-options-on-table" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>US AMBASSADOR WARNS IRAN AT EMERGENCY UN MEETING THAT TRUMP IS 'MAN OF ACTION,' 'ALL OPTIONS ARE ON THE TABLE'</strong></a></p><p>"They are guarding the government buildings and the state radio and TV and are using heavy machine guns, which are Russian-made and 50 caliber."</p><p>Safavi added that "at night, there are fierce clashes that are ongoing as well as running street battles between the protesters and the special unit forces."</p><p>The footage emerged as the <a 4,519, while the number of deaths still under investigation stands at 9,049," the agency said, adding that at least 5,811 people have been seriously injured and 26,314 arrested.</p><p>HRANA reports also described an overwhelming security presence, particularly with law enforcement, the IRGC, Basij units and plainclothes agents after nightfall, creating what the group called an atmosphere of ammunition and volleys of tear gas," Safavi said.</p><p>He alleged that IRGC units attacked a hospital in Gorgan, killing wounded patients, stationing snipers on rooftops and firing into surrounding <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/ali-khamenei" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Ali Khamenei</a> has repeatedly blamed foreign enemies for unrest while backing the IRGC’s "What is required is an organized nationwide resistance rooted in active, combat-ready forces inside Iran’s cities to defeat one of the most brutal and repressive apparatuses in the world today — the IRGC."</p>
