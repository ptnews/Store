<p>Cellphone video footage released Friday shows the moments that led to the fatal shooting of a Minneapolis woman by a <a class="c-link" href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/immigration" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">federal agent</a>, amid escalating tensions around the Trump administration’s illegal immigration crackdown in the city.</p><p>The video was released two days after a U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) agent fatally shot Renee Nicole Good as she allegedly drove toward officers.</p><p>The footage taken from an ICE agent’s vantage point shows Good’s Honda Pilot parked in the middle of a residential street, appearing to block traffic.</p><p>"That’s fine, dude," Good says to an agent. " I’m not mad."</p><p><a class="c-link" href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/ice-agents-report-unprecedented-3200-surge-car-attacks-last-year" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>ICE AGENTS REPORT ‘UNPRECEDENTED’ 3,200% SURGE IN CAR ATTACKS LAST YEAR</strong></a></p><p>The agent walks around the vehicle to check the license plate. Seconds later, another <a class="c-link" href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/immigration/enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">agent is seen walking</a> toward the driver’s side of Good’s vehicle and ordering Good to get out.</p><p>"Get out of the car. Get out of the f------ car," the agent is heard saying.</p><p>Good then backs up the vehicle, before moving it forward toward the agent wearing the body camera. He is heard saying "whoa" before shots ring out.</p><p><a class="c-link" href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/renee-nicole-good-part-ice-watch-group-dhs-sources-say" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>RENEE NICOLE GOOD PART OF 'ICE WATCH' GROUP, DHS SOURCES SAY</strong></a></p><p>Good’s vehicle then crashes into a parked car.</p><p>The circumstances that led to her death have ignited a firestorm of criticism of the Trump administration and the condemnation of the ICE agents' actions.</p><p>Federal officials have said that the <a class="c-link" href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/state-and-local/federal-relations" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">agents acted in self defense</a> and labeled the incident an act of domestic terrorism, while Democratic officials have rejected the self-defense assertion.</p><p><a class="c-link" href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/aoc-accuses-vance-believing-american-people-should-assassinated-street" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>AOC ACCUSES VANCE OF BELIEVING ‘AMERICAN PEOPLE SHOULD BE ASSASSINATED IN THE STREET’</strong></a></p><p>The <a class="c-link" href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/police-and-law-enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">agent who opened fire</a> was dragged and injured by a fleeing driver in a separate incident last year, officials said.</p><p>He was recovering from Wednesday's incident, officials said this week. Minneapolis Mayor Jacob Frey, however, downplayed the agent's injuries.</p><p>"The ICE agent walked away with a hip injury that he might as well as gotten from closing a refrigerator door with his hips...," he told reporters on Friday. "Give me a break. No, he was not ran over. He walked out of there with a hop in his step." </p><p>Authorities have said that Good had been following and harassing federal officers earlier that day.</p><p>She also worked as a <a class="c-link" href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/minneapolis-st-paul" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Minneapolis-based immigration activist serving</a> as a member of "ICE Watch," federal sources told Fox News.</p><p>Homeland Security sources told Fox News that the group aims to monitor, track, interfere and oppose ongoing federal immigration enforcement operations. The group is present in multiple <a class="c-link" href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/topic/sanctuary-cities" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">sanctuary cities</a> across the country.</p>
