<p>A surgeon accused of killing his ex-wife and her dentist husband <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/us-regions/midwest/ohio" target="_blank" rel="noopener">in Ohio</a> has pleaded not guilty after he was extradited to face murder charges.</p><p>Michael McKee was arrested on Jan. 10 after allegedly shooting and killing Spencer Tepe, 37, and <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/ohio-police-hunt-killer-after-dentist-wife-found-shot-death-children-safe-inside-home-timeline" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><u>Monique Tepe</u></a>, 39, in the couple's home in Columbus' Weinland Park neighborhood between 2 a.m. and 5 a.m. on Dec. 30. McKee is Monique's ex-husband.</p><p>McKee was arrested in <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/us-regions/midwest/illinois" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Rockford, Illinois</a> on Jan. 10 and was booked into the Winnebago County Jail in Illinois, before he was <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/surgeon-ex-husband-extradited-ohio-slaying-dentist-wife" target="_blank" rel="noopener">extradited to Columbus, Ohio</a>, where he's being held at the James A. Karnes Corrections Center. He faces upgraded premeditated aggravated murder charges in relation to the deaths of Spencer and Monique.</p><p>McKee appeared remotely at the arraignment hearing on Tuesday from jail and pleaded not guilty to four charges of premeditated aggravated murder with firearm specifications and aggravated burglary. Three aggravated murder charges include specifications alleging McKee of using or displaying an automatic firearm or silencer, and the fourth alleged that a firearm was displayed or brandished.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/ohio-dentist-slayings-suspect-indicted-more-aggravated-murder-burglary-charges" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>OHIO DENTIST SLAYINGS SUSPECT INDICTED ON MORE AGGRAVATED MURDER, BURGLARY CHARGES</strong></a></p><p>McKee is being represented by attorney Diane Menashe, who manages her own firm and has been a lawyer for 27 years. The car was then located in Rockford, Illinois, and police found evidence it belonged to McKee.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/police-and-law-enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Columbus Police Chief</a> Elaine Bryant earlier told Fox News Digital that several weapons were taken from McKee's property, adding that a <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/police-chief-touts-gun-link-ohio-dentist-murders-records-show-three-9mm-casings-found-killings" target="_blank" rel="noopener">preliminary link was established</a> that one of those firearms to the murders.</p><p>One of Spencer and Monique's friends found <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/homicide" target="_blank" rel="noopener">the couple dead</a> on Dec. 30 at their home, <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/frantic-911-calls-detail-moments-before-columbus-dentist-wife-found-dead-theres-body" target="_blank" rel="noopener">according to a 911 call</a>.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/police-arrest-ex-husband-ohio-woman-slain-alongside-dentist-husband-double-murder-records-show" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>POLICE ARREST EX-HUSBAND OF OHIO WOMAN SLAIN ALONGSIDE DENTIST HUSBAND IN DOUBLE MURDER, RECORDS SHOW</strong></a></p><p>"There's a body," the caller says.
