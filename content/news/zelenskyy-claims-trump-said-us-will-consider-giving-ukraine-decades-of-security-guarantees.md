<p>Ukrainian President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/volodymyr-zelenskyy" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Volodymyr Zelenskyy</a> indicated in a post on X that Ukraine would like to have 30, 40 or even 50 years of security guarantees from the U.S. and that President Donald Trump said the U.S. will consider it.</p><p>Zelenskyy met with Trump in Florida on Sunday, as his nation remains locked in a deadly, protracted war against Russia, and the U.S. administration aims to help broker peace.</p><p>In a Monday post on X, the president of the embattled Eastern European nation indicated that Trump had "confirmed strong security guarantees" during their meeting.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/trump-zelenskyy-say-ukraine-peace-deal-close-thorny-issues-remain-after-florida-talks" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>TRUMP, ZELENSKYY SAY UKRAINE PEACE DEAL CLOSE BUT ‘THORNY ISSUES’ REMAIN AFTER FLORIDA TALKS</strong></a></p><p>"He confirmed the details that had been developed up to this point by our negotiating teams regarding these security guarantees, and he confirmed that they would be put to a vote by the United States Congress. This is a very strong agreement," Zelenskyy noted.</p><p>During a joint press conference alongside Zelenskyy on Sunday, <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/donald-trump" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Trump</a> was asked whether he offered any promises or assurances of security for Ukraine.</p><p>"I did. We wanna work with Europe," Trump answered, adding that Europe will "take over a big part of it" but that the U.S. will assist.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/marjorie-taylor-greene-criticizes-trumps-meetings-zelenskyy-netanyahu-can-we-just-do-america" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>MARJORIE TAYLOR GREENE CRITICIZES TRUMP'S MEETINGS WITH ZELENSKYY, NETANYAHU: ‘CAN WE JUST DO AMERICA?'</strong></a></p><p>Zelenskyy, in another Monday post on X, indicated that Ukraine would like decades of security guarantees from the U.S.</p><p>"In the documents, the guarantees are set for 15 years, with the possibility of extension. I raised this issue with the President. I told him that our war has already been going on for more than a decade, and therefore, we would very much like the guarantees to last longer. We would like to consider the possibility of 30, 40, or 50 years. It would then become a historic decision by President Trump. The President said that the U.S. would consider it," the foreign leader noted in the post.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/trump-zelenskyy-meet-sunday-ukrainian-leader-vows-country-do-whatever-takes-end-war" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>TRUMP SAYS RUSSIA, UKRAINE IN ‘FINAL STAGES’ OF TALKS, PREDICTS WAR WILL ‘END’ SOON OR ‘GO ON FOR A LONG TIME’</strong></a></p><p>Fox News Digital reached out to the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/executive/white-house" target="_blank" rel="noopener">White House</a> on Monday for comment, but they did not immediately respond.</p>
