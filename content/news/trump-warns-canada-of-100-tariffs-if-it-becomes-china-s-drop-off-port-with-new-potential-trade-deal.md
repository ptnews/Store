<p>President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/donald-trump" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Donald Trump</a> threatened on Saturday that he would implement 100% tariffs on Canada if it strikes a deal to become a "drop off port" for China.</p><p>"If Governor Carney thinks he is going to <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/world-regions/canada" target="_blank" rel="noopener">make Canada a "drop off port"</a> for China to send goods and products into the United States, he is sorely mistaken. China will eat Canada alive, completely devour it, including the destruction of their businesses, social fabric, and general way of life," Trump wrote on <a href="https://truthsocial.com/@realDonaldTrump/posts/115950424403894234" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">Truth Social</a>.</p><p>"If Canada makes a deal with China, it will immediately be hit with a 100% tariff against all Canadian goods and products coming into the U.S.A.," the president added.</p><p>Trump referred to Canadian Prime Minister Mark Carney as a "governor," echoing comments he made while campaigning for a second term about annexing America’s northern neighbor. He previously used the same term when speaking about Carney’s predecessor, Justin Trudeau.</p><p><i>This is a developing story. Please check back for updates.</i></p>
