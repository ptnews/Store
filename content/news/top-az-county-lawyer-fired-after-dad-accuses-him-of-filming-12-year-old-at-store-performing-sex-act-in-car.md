Department officers then responded to the scene, and bodycam footage shows Correa repeatedly denying the allegations. </p><p>Correa, however, was swiftly fired the day after the incident, officials announced on Dec. 18. <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/us-regions/southwest/arizona" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Cochise County Attorney</a> Lori Zucco said the decision was made after
