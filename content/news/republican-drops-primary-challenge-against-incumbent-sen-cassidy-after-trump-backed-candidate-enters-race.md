<p>Louisiana state Rep. Julie Emerson announced on Thursday that she was nixing her U.S. Senate bid in light of President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/donald-trump" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Donald Trump</a>-backed U.S. Rep. Julia Letlow entering the GOP primary.</p><p>"With Congresswoman Letlow's entrance into the race, the path to victory that was visible a couple of months ago has diminished. I support President Trump and respect his decision to endorse Julia Letlow to defeat Bill Cassidy. Because of this, I'm choosing to end my campaign now," Emerson said in a <a href="https://x.com/JulieEmerson/status/2014438484035961169" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">statement</a>.</p><p>Incumbent Republican Sen. Bill Cassidy of <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/us-regions/southeast/louisiana" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Louisiana</a>, who has served in the U.S. Senate since 2015, is running for re-election.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/trump-endorsement-rocks-louisiana-senate-race-letlow-poised-jump" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>TRUMP ENDORSEMENT ROCKS LOUSIANA SENATE RACE AS LETLOW JUMPS IN</strong></a></p><p>After the House impeached Trump in 2021, Cassidy was one of the Senate Republicans who voted to convict during a vote that occurred after Trump had already departed from office — the Senate vote ultimately fell short of the threshold required to convict Trump.</p><p>The president pledged his endorsement to Letlow in a Truth Social post on Saturday.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/who-john-fleming-freedom-caucus-founding-member-challenging-gop-sen-bill-cassidy" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>WHO IS JOHN FLEMING, THE FREEDOM CAUCUS FOUNDING MEMBER CHALLENGING GOP SEN BILL CASSIDY?</strong></a></p><p>"Should she decide to enter this Race, Julia Letlow has my Complete and Total Endorsement. RUN, JULIA, RUN!!!" the president exclaimed in the <a href="https://truthsocial.com/@realDonaldTrump/posts/115913724519885202" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">post</a>.</p><p>Letlow launched a Senate bid days later.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/gop-louisiana-state-senator-says-hes-running-us-senate-because-incumbent-republican-sucks" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>GOP LOUISIANA STATE SENATOR SAYS HE'S RUNNING FOR US SENATE BECAUSE INCUMBENT REPUBLICAN ‘SUCKS’</strong></a></p><p>"Today, I am announcing my candidacy for the United States Senate to ensure the nation we leave our children is safer and stronger. Louisiana deserves a conservative Senator who will not waver. I am honored to have President Trump’s endorsement and trust. Let’s Geaux!" she declared in a Tuesday <a href="https://x.com/jbletlow/status/2013646066801955020" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">post</a> on X.</p><p>Louisiana State Treasurer John Fleming and state Sen. Blake Miguez are also running in the Republican <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/senate" target="_blank" rel="noopener">U.S. Senate</a> primary in the state.</p>
