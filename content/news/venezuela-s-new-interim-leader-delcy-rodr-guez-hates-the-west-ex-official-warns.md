<p>Nicolás Maduro’s interim replacement now <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/topic/venezuelan-political-crisis" target="_blank" rel="noopener">leading Venezuela</a> in the wake of his arrest represents a continuation of the same regime, and with a woman who "hates the West" at the helm, a former Venezuelan official has warned.</p><p>Delcy Rodríguez’s appointment, the source said, underscores how Venezuela’s leadership continues to rely on a tight circle of Maduro loyalists rather than new figures, leaving what the source described as "the axis of evil in a <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/foreign-policy/geopolitics" target="_blank" rel="noopener">geopolitical struggle</a> three hours from Miami."</p><p>Rodríguez, Venezuela’s former <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics" target="_blank" rel="noopener">vice president</a> and oil minister, was sworn in Monday after Maduro’s removal from office and his appearance in a New York courtroom on drug trafficking charges.</p><p>"The regime is still the same, and she hates the West," the official told Fox News Digital, speaking on the condition of anonymity.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/kamala-harris-blasts-trump-administrations-capture-venezuelas-maduro-unlawful-unwise" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>KAMALA HARRIS BLASTS TRUMP ADMINISTRATION’S CAPTURE OF VENEZUELA’S MADURO AS ‘UNLAWFUL AND UNWISE’</strong></a></p><p>"Delcy has evolved from a mediocre lawyer, and now she’s the president of one of the richest nations in Latin America," the source said.</p><p>"She has probably been in every post, because they rotate the same people because they don’t have anybody else that they trust.</p><p>‘Delcy is a very close and trustworthy person for Maduro and was also the same for Chávez."</p><p>President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/donald-trump" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Donald Trump</a> said Saturday that Rodríguez, 56, stood "willing to do what we think is necessary to make Venezuela great again."</p><p>Rodríguez, who has served as foreign minister, president of the Constituent Assembly, vice president, and oil minister, <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/trump-issues-direct-warning-venezuelas-new-leader-delcy-rodriguez-following-maduro-capture" target="_blank" rel="noopener">was sworn in by her brother</a>, Jorge Rodríguez, the head of the National Assembly.</p><p>Born in Caracas, Rodríguez is the daughter of Marxist guerrilla leader <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jorge_Antonio_Rodr%C3%ADguez#:~:text=Jorge%20Antonio%20Rodr%C3%ADguez%20(February%2016,the%20American%20executive%20William%20Niehous." target="_blank" rel="noopener">Jorge Antonio Rodríguez</a>, a co-founder of the Socialist League.</p><p>He was arrested in connection with the 1976 kidnapping of American businessman William F. Niehous and later died in police custody, according to the <a href="https://www.nytimes.com/2013/10/18/world/americas/william-f-niehous-survivor-of-abduction-in-venezuela-dies-at-82.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener">New York Times</a>.</p><p>"That ordeal is the foundation and the origin of Delcy’s hatred of the West and of democracy," the source said, before claiming, "her whole background was Marxist."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/iran-maduro-ties-suffer-major-blow-following-us-operation-capture-venezuelan-dictator" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>IRAN for three years until authorities located the group hiding in the Amazon jungle," the source said.</p><p>Rodríguez’s tenure as foreign minister was also marked by controversy, the source claimed, abroad, according to <a href="https://www.cnn.com/2017/02/08/world/venezuela-passports-investigation-characters#:~:text=Delcy%20Rodr%C3%ADguez&amp;text=Delcy%20Rodriguez%20has%20been%20the,the%20allegations%20were%20not%20true." target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">reports</a>.</p><p>At the 2016 United Nations General Assembly, Rodríguez said the allegations were untrue.</p><p>"There were some passports being sold in Iraq,
