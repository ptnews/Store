<p>While some Democrats advocate for the abolition of U.S.<a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/immigration/enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener"> Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE)</a>, Sen. John Fetterman, D-Pa., has pressed his party not to advocate for "extreme" ideas.</p><p>"Even Mayor Frey of Minneapolis doesn’t support abolishing ICE. The party must resist the destructive tendencies to push extreme positions," <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/john-fetterman" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Fetterman noted</a> in the post on X. </p><p>"Secure the border. Deport all the criminals. Stop targeting the hardworking migrants in our nation," <a href="https://x.com/SenFettermanPA/status/2011863439371542625" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">he added.</a></p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/democrats-dilemma-progressive-push-abolish-ice-sparks-fresh-divide-party" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>DEMOCRATS' DILEMMA: PROGRESSIVE PUSH TO ‘ABOLISH ICE’ SPARKS FRESH DIVIDE IN PARTY</strong></a></p><p>His post featured a screenshot of a headline by The New York Times that reads, "Abolish ICE? It’s a Slogan Some Democratic Critics of ICE Would Abolish."</p><p>Fetterman asserted in a July 2025 post on X that "ICE performs an important job for our country," describing "calls to abolish" the agency as "inappropriate and outrageous."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/democrat-john-fetterman-declares-support-ice-condemning-any-calls-abolition-outrageous" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>DEMOCRAT JOHN FETTERMAN DECLARES SUPPORT FOR ICE, CONDEMNING ANY CALLS FOR ABOLITION AS ‘OUTRAGEOUS’</strong></a></p><p>In another post later in July, Fetterman shared a screenshot of the headline of a Fox News Digital <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/ice-arrests-over-200-illegal-alien-child-sex-offenders-houston-area-past-6-months" target="_blank" rel="noopener">article titled,</a> "ICE arrests over 200 illegal alien child sex offenders in Houston area in past 6 months," expressing his support for the law enforcement activity.</p><p>"I don’t support or agree with all of ICE’s tactics or actions. I do fully support moves like these. This makes our nation more secure and all our children safer," he <a href="https://x.com/SenFettermanPA/status/1949880166362337746" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">noted in the post.</a></p><p>Rep. Shri Thanedar, D-Mich., has introduced legislation to abolish ICE.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/ice-agent-shoots-venezuelan-national-minneapolis-shovel-attack-ambush-dhs" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>ICE AGENT SHOOTS VENEZUELAN NATIONAL IN MINNEAPOLIS AFTER SHOVEL ATTACK DURING AMBUSH: DHS</strong></a></p><p>"Effective on the date that is 90 days after the date of enactment of this Act, U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement is abolished," part of the measure reads.</p>
