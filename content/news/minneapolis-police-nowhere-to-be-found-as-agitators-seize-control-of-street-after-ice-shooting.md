<p>MINNEAPOLIS – The <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/police-and-law-enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Minneapolis Police Department</a> was absent from the block where Wednesday’s ICE shooting unfolded, allowing agitators to seize control of the street and erect makeshift barricades that kept others out of the area.</p><p>According to the Department of Homeland Security (DHS), the shooting happened during an <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/surveillance-footage-shows-immigration-activist-blocking-road-before-fatal-ice-shooting" target="_blank" rel="noopener">ICE enforcement operation</a> in south Minneapolis on Wednesday. DHS said agents were trying to make arrests when Renee Good allegedly used her vehicle as a weapon, which the agency said prompted a federal agent to fire shots in self-defense.</p><p>Following the shooting, agitators took control of the street where the shooting took place and used makeshift barricades with chairs, trash cans, wood shipping pallets, bicycles and more.</p><p>Fox News Digital went to the area surrounding the makeshift barricades and observed agitators directing traffic. No police officers were observed nearby.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/media/jonathan-turley-slams-minneapolis-mayors-reckless-response-ice-shooting" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>JONATHAN TURLEY SLAMS MINNEAPOLIS MAYOR'S 'RECKLESS' RESPONSE TO ICE SHOOTING</strong></a></p><p>Minneapolis police took down the makeshift barricades early Friday morning.</p><p>"F--- ICE" was spray-painted on one of the wooden shipping pallets. Another nearby sign said "ICE are terrorists."</p><p>During a news conference after the shooting, Homeland Security Secretary Kristi Noem said Good, a 37-year-old U.S. citizen, was "<a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/noem-alleges-woman-killed-ice-shooting-stalking-impeding-agents-all-day" target="_blank" rel="noopener">stalking and impeding agents</a>" and was instructed to get out of her car just before the shooting, but did not comply.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/minnesota-schools-shut-down-teachers-union-demands-ice-leave-city" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>MINNESOTA SCHOOLS SHUT DOWN, TEACHERS UNION DEMANDS ICE LEAVE CITY</strong></a></p><p>Good was pronounced dead shortly after. On Wednesday, <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/minneapolis-mayor-demands-state-included-ice-involved-shooting-probe-slams-pam-bondis-doj" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Minneapolis Mayor Jacob Frey</a> publicly disagreed with DHS's assessment of the shooting as self-defense, calling it "bull----."</p><p>On Thursday, Minnesota Gov. <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/tim-walz" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><u>Tim Walz</u></a> authorized the Minnesota National Guard to be ready to support local and state law enforcement to keep protests peaceful.</p><p>"Minnesotans have met this moment. Thousands of people have peacefully made their voices heard. Minnesota: Thank you. We saw powerful peace," Walz said in a statement. "We have every reason to believe that peace will hold."</p><p>"We have soldiers in training and prepared to be deployed if necessary. I remind you, a warning order is a heads-up for folks. Minnesota will not allow our community to be used as a prop in a national political fight," he added.</p>
