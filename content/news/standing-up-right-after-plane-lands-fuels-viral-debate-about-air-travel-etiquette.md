the aisle as soon as the aircraft comes to a stop.</p><p>Diane Gottsman, a national etiquette expert based in Texas, told Fox News Digital the issue has become a source of frustration for travelers.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/travel/passenger-defends-controversial-gate-lice-behavior-airlines-crack-down-early-boarding-attempts" target="_blank"
