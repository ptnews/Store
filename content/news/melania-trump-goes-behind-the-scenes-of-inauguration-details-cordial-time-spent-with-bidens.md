<p>First lady <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/melania-trump" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Melania Trump</a> offered a behind-the-scenes look at President Donald Trump's second inauguration in an exclusive "Fox &amp; Friends" interview on Tuesday, detailing the events leading up to their historic White House return and their time spent with the Biden family that day.</p><p>"They were very nice and [we had a] very beautiful relationship that day, so it was a very special day that we [were] spending together, driving to the Capitol, and it was a very cordial relationship," she shared with co-host Ainsley Earhardt.</p><p>Mrs. Trump joined the show live from the White House to promote her <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/exclusive-first-look-melania-film" target="_blank" rel="noopener">upcoming movie, "MELANIA,"</a> ahead of its global launch on Friday.</p><p>The 104-minute film details her life leading up to the inauguration — from her home in Trump Tower in New York City to Mar-a-Lago in Palm Beach — plus behind-the-scenes access inside the White House.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/media/melania-trump-named-fox-nations-patriot-year-global-childrens-advocacy-work" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>MELANIA TRUMP NAMED FOX NATION’S ‘PATRIOT OF THE YEAR’ FOR GLOBAL CHILDREN’S ADVOCACY WORK</strong></a></p><p>Discussing the film, Mrs. Trump described scenes that offer a glimpse into her relationship with the president, including one that showed her helping him prepare his inauguration speech. </p><p>"I give him my advice, and I tell him what I think. Sometimes he listens, sometimes he doesn't, but I am here to support him. I think it's very important to have open communication," she said. </p><p>Earhardt detailed another scene in which Mrs. Trump is seen <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/executive/national-security" target="_blank" rel="noopener">planning security</a> for the inauguration alongside the U.S. Secret Service.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/melania-trump-rolls-out-ai-audiobook-memoir-spanish-amazing-journey" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>FIRST LADY MELANIA TRUMP ROLLS OUT AI AUDIOBOOK OF FIRST MEMOIR IN SPANISH: 'AMAZING JOURNEY'</strong></a></p><p>Just months before, the president had two close encounters with death: one when gunman Thomas Matthew Crooks opened fire at a rally in Butler, Pennsylvania, and another when would-be assassin Ryan Wesley Routh pointed a gun at Trump while he was golfing at his West Palm Beach club. </p><p>Earhardt questioned Mrs. Trump about safety concerns in her role as first lady, asking whether similar dangers are a part of her everyday thoughts.</p><p>"You think about it, and I have a great Secret Service team, and you need to be very vigilant," she replied. </p><p>"What do you do? Where you go? We are living in dangerous times, and preparation for [the] inauguration was part of it."</p>
