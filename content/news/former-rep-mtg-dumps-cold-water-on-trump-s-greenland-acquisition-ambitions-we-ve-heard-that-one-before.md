Is anyone even talking about how much it’s going to cost the American people to take over Greenland?" Greene asked in a Tuesday <a href="https://x.com/FmrRepMTG/status/2013656624733262204" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">post</a> on X.</p><p>"Saying it’s ‘for your safety’ is not sufficient.
