<p>The California Republican Party is aiming to get the U.S. <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/judiciary/supreme-court" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Supreme Court</a> to block the Golden State from using a new congressional map.</p><p>"If left uncorrected, this pernicious and unconstitutional use of race will irreparably harm Applicants and the public. Applicants therefore respectfully request an injunction pending appeal precluding California from conducting any congressional election under the map enacted through Proposition 50 ('Proposition 50 Map') and requiring it to use the prior map adopted in 2021 by the independent Citizens Redistricting Commission," the <a href="https://www.dhillonlaw.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/SCOTUS_Emergency_App_FINAL.pdf" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">emergency application </a>declares.</p><p>In November, <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/us-regions/west/california" target="_blank" rel="noopener">California voters</a> approved Proposition 50 to temporarily adopt the new congressional map.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/federal-court-clears-californias-new-house-map-boosting-democrats-2026-midterms" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>FEDERAL COURT CLEARS CALIFORNIA'S NEW HOUSE MAP BOOSTING DEMOCRATS AHEAD OF 2026 MIDTERMS</strong></a></p><p>Those redrawn districts would apply during this year's midterms.</p><p>If California elections are allowed to proceed according to the new map, Democrats would likely see an advantage during the 2026 contests as they seek to win the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/elections/house-of-representatives" target="_blank" rel="noopener">House majority.</a></p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/federal-judge-scorches-dems-pandering-latinos-california-map-fiery-dissent" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>FEDERAL JUDGE SCORCHES DEMS FOR PANDERING TO LATINOS WITH CALIFORNIA MAP IN FIERY DISSENT</strong></a></p><p>"California cannot create districts by race, and the state should not be allowed to lock in districts that break federal law," California Republican Party Chairwoman Corrin Rankin said, according to a <a href="https://x.com/CAGOP/status/2013704570464280830" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">post on X</a> by the California Republican Party.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/newsom-warns-pathetic-foreign-leaders-grow-backbone-bizarre-takedown-likening-trump-t-rex" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>NEWSOM WARNS ‘PATHETIC’ FOREIGN LEADERS TO GROW A BACKBONE IN BIZARRE TAKEDOWN LIKENING TRUMP TO A T.REX</strong></a></p><p>"Our emergency application asks the Supreme Court to put the brakes on Prop. 50 now, before the Democrats try to run out the clock and force candidates and voters to live with unconstitutional congressional districts," Rankin said. "Californians deserve fair districts and clean elections, not a backroom redraw that picks winners and losers based on race."</p>
