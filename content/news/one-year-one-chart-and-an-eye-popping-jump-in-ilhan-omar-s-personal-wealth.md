<p>Minnesota Rep. <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/ilhan-omar" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><u>Ilhan Omar's</u></a> latest federal financial disclosures show millions of dollars in newly reported assets tied to her husband’s businesses, representing a massive increase compared to previous years' listings.</p><p>The disclosures, filed as part of Congress’ annual financial reporting requirements, come as Omar has publicly denied being a millionaire, labeling such assertions "ridiculous."</p><p>Despite those denials, the sudden appearance of the assets at high valuations has raised questions about her husband’s <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/meet-longtime-biz-partner-ilhan-omars-husband-questions-swirl-skyrocketing-net-worth" target="_blank" rel="noopener">business holdings</a> and prompted President Donald Trump to call for a Department of Justice investigation into the couple’s shared wealth.</p><p>Omar’s office did not immediately respond to Fox News Digital’s request for comment.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/meet-longtime-biz-partner-ilhan-omars-husband-questions-swirl-skyrocketing-net-worth" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>MEET THE LONGTIME BIZ PARTNER OF ILHAN OMAR'S HUSBAND AS QUESTIONS SWIRL OVER HER SKYROCKETING NET WORTH</strong></a></p><p>Two of her husband’s business ventures, a Santa Rosa, California-based winery and a Washington, D.C.–based venture capital firm, account for the bulk of the reported assets.</p><p>Federal disclosure forms do not list exact amounts, instead reporting assets in broad ranges; the figures shown in the chart below reflect the midpoint of those ranges for comparison.</p><p>The winery, listed as eStCru LLC, saw its reported valuation jump from a range of $15,000 to $50,000 in 2023 to between $1 million and $5 million the following year. </p><p>Rose Lake Capital showed an even sharper increase, rising from a reported value of between $1 and $1,000 in 2023 to an asset range of $5 million to $25 million in 2024.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/trump-calls-investigation-rep-ilhan-omars-wealth-says-should-start-now" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>TRUMP CALLS FOR INVESTIGATION INTO ILHAN OMAR'S WELATH, SAYS IT SHOULD START ‘NOW’</u></strong></a> </p><p>At the same time, Omar’s disclosures list outstanding debts, including student loans, auto payments and credit card balances.</p><p>The increase has drawn growing scrutiny, including from Trump, who questioned Omar’s finances in a series of Truth Social posts. </p><p>On Monday, Trump said the Department of Justice would investigate her reported wealth, which he claimed totaled $44 million. "Time will tell all," Trump wrote.</p><p>Separately, Minnesota has been at the center of multiple <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/minnesota-fraud-exposed" target="_blank" rel="noopener">high-profile fraud</a> cases in recent years, underscoring broader concerns about financial oversight in the state.</p>
