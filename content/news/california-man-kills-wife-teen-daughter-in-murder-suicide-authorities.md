<p>A 52-year-old man killed his wife and teenage daughter before taking his own life in a shooting inside a <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/los-angeles" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Los Angeles County</a> home Thursday morning, authorities said.</p><p>Los Angeles County <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/police-and-law-enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener">sheriff’s deputies</a> responded around 8 a.m. to a report of an assault with a deadly weapon at a home in Lakewood, a city about 20 miles southeast of Los Angeles. A caller told dispatchers her father had fired a gun at her and was lying unconscious nearby, deputies said.</p><p>When deputies arrived, they met the caller, a 19-year-old woman, outside the home. She was not injured and was taken in for questioning.</p><p>Inside the house, deputies found a man lying on the floor in the den with a gunshot wound to his upper torso. He was identified as the father and shooter, Hector Lionel Alfaro, 52.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/mississippi-prosecutors-seek-death-penalty-against-man-accused-deadly-rampage-included-girl-pastor" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>MISSISSIPPI PROSECUTORS TO SEEK DEATH PENALTY AGAINST MAN ACCUSED OF DEADLY RAMPAGE THAT INCLUDED GIRL, PASTOR</strong></a></p><p>Deputies then discovered Alfaro’s 48-year-old wife, Roxana Rodriguez, in a bedroom suffering from <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/homicide" target="_blank" rel="noopener">gunshot wounds</a> to her torso. In a second bedroom, deputies found the couple’s 17-year-old daughter, Sienna Alfaro, who had also been shot. All three were pronounced dead at the scene.</p><p>"Through investigation, Homicide Investigators learned gunshots were heard within the residence by Alfaro’s 19-year-old daughter while she was asleep," the sheriff’s department said in a statement obtained by <a href="https://www.foxla.com/news/lakewood-triple-shooting-man-kills-wife-daughter-himself" target="_blank" rel="noopener">FOX11 Los Angeles</a>. "Alfaro then appeared near her armed with a handgun and shot at her several times; however, she was not struck by the gunfire. Alfaro then shot himself while standing near his 19-year-old daughter."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/crime" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Two firearms</a> were recovered from the home.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/six-killed-series-mississippi-shootings-individual-custody-reports" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>SIX KILLED IN SERIES OF MISSISSIPPI SHOOTINGS, INDIVIDUAL IN CUSTODY: REPORTS</strong></a></p><p>Authorities have not identified a motive. The investigation remains ongoing.</p><p>"Our hearts are broken as we share the tragic loss of a loving mother and her daughter, whose lives were taken by an act of unimaginable violence," wrote Andrew, the husband to the couple’s eldest daughter Desiree, in a GoFundMe post. "This devastating loss has left behind their surviving children, who are now facing the world without the people who meant everything to them."</p><p>Sienna’s soccer teammate, Hazel Ferman, remembered her friend as "a ray of sunshine" at a candlelight vigil on Saturday, according to <a href="https://abc7.com/post/lakewood-murder-suicide-victims-honored-candlelight-vigil-gofundme-launched-support-family/18422982/" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow">KABC-TV</a>.</p><p>"She's a very jolly person," Ferman said. "Her smile would bright up the room. Her energy was just out of this world, man like she would walk in, even though she's a shy girl, when she would smile is like everybody knew that we just connected."</p>
