<p>President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/donald-trump" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Donald Trump</a> praised cartoonist-turned-conservative influencer Scott Adams after learning of his death on Tuesday.</p><p>Adams, known for the white-collar office-humor "Dilbert" comic strip, <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/media/dilbert-creator-scott-adams-dies-age-68" target="_blank" rel="noopener">died at age 68</a> following a battle with cancer. His ex-wife, Shelly Miles, tearfully announced his passing during a livestream on the "Real Coffee with Scott Adams" YouTube channel.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/donald-trump" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Trump reacted</a> to Adams' death on Truth Social, writing, "Sadly, the Great Influencer, Scott Adams, has passed away. He was a fantastic guy, who liked and respected me when it wasn’t fashionable to do so. He bravely fought a long battle against a terrible disease. My condolences go out to his family, and all of his many friends and listeners. He will be truly missed. God bless you, Scott!"</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/entertainment/dilbert-creator-scott-adams-receiving-hospice-care-health-declining-rapidly-ex-wife-says" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>'DILBERT' CREATOR SCOTT ADAMS RECEIVING HOSPICE CARE, HEALTH DECLINING 'RAPIDLY,' EX-WIFE SAYS</u></strong></a></p><p>"’If you are reading this, things did not go well for me,’" Miles <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Rs_JrOIo3SE" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">read from Adams' final message</a> to fans, which he wrote on New Year's Day. "’I have a few things to say before I go. My body failed before my brain. I am of sound mind as I write this January 1st, 2026."</p><p>"’Next, many of my Christian friends have asked me to find Jesus before I go. I'm not a believer, but I have to admit the risk-reward calculation for doing so looks so attractive to me, so here I go,’" Adams wrote. <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/christianity" target="_blank" rel="noopener">"’I accept Jesus Christ</a> as my Lord and Savior and look forward to spending an eternity with him. The part about me not being a believer should be quite quickly resolved if I wake up in heaven. I won't need any more convincing than that. I hope I'm still qualified for entry.'"</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/trump-admin-responds-dilbert-creators-plea-help-save-my-life-expediting-cancer-treatment" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>TRUMP ADMIN RESPONDS TO 'DILBERT' CREATOR'S PLEA TO 'HELP SAVE MY LIFE' BY EXPEDITING CANCER TREATMENT</u></strong></a></p><p>Adams first announced his prostate cancer diagnosis on his show in May, saying he was in pain every day and had been using a walker for months.</p><p>"If you're wondering if I'll get better, the answer is no, it will only get worse," he said at the time. "There's only one direction this goes."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/media" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>CLICK HERE FOR MORE COVERAGE OF MEDIA AND CULTURE</u></strong></a></p><p>Adams stood out for his fervent backing of Trump. He was one of few celebrities to openly predict Trump would be elected president in 2016.</p>
