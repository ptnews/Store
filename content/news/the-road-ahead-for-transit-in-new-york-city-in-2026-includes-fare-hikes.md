<p>Newly inaugurated New York City Mayor <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/zohran-mamdani" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Zohran Mamdani</a>, a self-described democratic socialist, won election last year while campaigning on making Big Apple buses "fast and free" — but there's a problem.</p><p>The Metropolitan Transportation Authority, not the city, oversees the bus system.</p><p>And while Mamdani longs for a future of free <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/new-york-city" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Big Apple</a> trips, riders will instead be getting slapped with price hikes in a matter of days.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/media/mamdani-accidentally-honors-free-market-subway-station-ceremony-washington-post-argues" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>MAMDANI ‘ACCIDENTALLY HONORS’ FREE MARKET WITH SUBWAY STATION CEREMONY, WASHINGTON POST ARGUES</strong></a></p><p>The small increase in the base bus fare will go into effect on January 4. </p><p>"The base fare for subways, local buses, and Access-A-Ride will increase 10 cents, from $2.90 to $3," according to the <a href="https://www.mta.info/fares-tolls/2025-changes" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">MTA</a>. "The reduced fare will increase from $1.45 to $1.50." </p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/terrifying-video-shows-out-of-control-mta-bus-plowing-cars-bronx-injuring-8" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>TERRIFYING VIDEO SHOWS OUT-OF-CONTROL MTA BUS PLOWING INTO CARS IN THE BRONX, INJURING 8</strong></a></p><p>Another change is already in effect as of New Year's Day.</p><p>"As of January 1, 2026, you can no longer buy or refill a MetroCard," according to the <a href="https://www.mta.info/fares-tolls/subway-bus" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">MTA</a>. </p><p>At some point in 2026, the MetroCard will no longer be accepted, and <a href="https://www.foxbusiness.com/category/money" target="_blank" rel="noopener">coins</a> will no longer be accepted for bus fares, according to the MTA. </p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/new-york-city-about-test-mamdanis-progressive-economic-vision" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>NEW YORK CITY IS ABOUT TO TEST MAMDANI'S PROGRESSIVE ECONOMIC VISION</strong></a></p><p>"If you still have a valid MetroCard, you can transfer the value to an OMNY Card at a Customer Service Center. You can also spend down the remaining value before it expires by paying for subway and bus rides," the MTA has noted. "Once cash is no longer accepted on buses, you will still be able to use cash and coins to add value to an OMNY Card at vending machines in every subway station and at more than 2,700 retail locations."</p>
