"When an agent moved to detain the person who spit on him, the crowd tackled and attacked the agents while surrounding them."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/vice-president-jd-vance-visit-minneapolis-source-says-amid-unrest-over-ice-operations" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>VICE PRESIDENT JD VANCE TO VISIT MINNEAPOLIS, SOURCE SAYS, AMID UNREST OVER ICE OPERATIONS</strong></a></p><p>The department said that the agents were forced to use crowd control measures to disperse the agitators.</p><p>The post comes just ahead of Vice President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/jd-vance" target="_blank" rel="noopener">JD Vance</a>'s visit to Minneapolis.
