<p>Sen. Rick Scott, R-Fla., is predicting that the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/world-regions/cuba" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Cuban regime</a> will fall, possibly in 2026 or 2027.</p><p>Scott had said during an <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xe24P0OJf08" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">appearance</a> last year on "60 Minutes" that Venezuelan leader Nicolás Maduro's days were "numbered," predicting that "whether it's internal or external," something would "happen." </p><p>He also predicted that the removal of Maduro would "be the end of Cuba."</p><p>So far, the first part of the senator's prediction has been fulfilled, as the U.S. captured Venezuelan dictator Nicolás Maduro and extradited him to New York last week. </p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/opinion/sen-rick-scott-i-grew-up-public-housing-i-know-how-fix-obamacare" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>SEN RICK SCOTT: I GREW UP IN PUBLIC HOUSING AND I KNOW HOW TO FIX OBAMACARE</strong></a></p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/person/s/rick-scott" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Scott stood</a> by his prognostication during a Tuesday appearance on NewsNation, forecasting that the end of the "Díaz-Canel regime" will "happen."</p><p>"Everything takes longer than you think," Scott <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wOAc-9G9GBk" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">said, noting</a> that he thinks the Cuban regime will probably fall "maybe this year, maybe next year."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/cuba-identifies-32-military-personnel-killed-us-operation-maduro-regime-venezuela" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>CUBA IDENTIFIES 32 MILITARY PERSONNEL KILLED IN US OPERATION AGAINST MADURO REGIME IN VENEZUELA</strong></a></p><p>"So who knows when it's gonna happen.
