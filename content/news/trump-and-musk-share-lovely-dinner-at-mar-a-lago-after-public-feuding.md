<p>President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/donald-trump" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Donald Trump</a> and Elon Musk appear to have repaired their once-strained relationship, according to a post shared by the billionaire Tesla founder on X.</p><p>In a post shared Sunday, <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/elon-musk" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Musk sparked speculation that the pair’s bromance may be back on after months of tension.</p><p>After the 2024 campaign, Musk became one of the <a of dollars, according to Reuters.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/trump-lays-out-where-he-stands-elon-musk-after-big-beautiful-bill-blowup" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>TRUMP
