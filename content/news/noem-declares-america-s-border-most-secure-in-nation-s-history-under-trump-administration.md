<p>Department of Homeland Security (DHS) Secretary <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/kristi-noem" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Kristi Noem</a> declared Friday that America’s border is the "most secure" in history, pointing to an eighth straight month of zero parole releases.</p><p>In a post on X, Noem touted newly released Customs and Border Protection (CBP) numbers for December 2025, praising President Trump and frontline law enforcement for delivering historic results.</p><p>"Thanks to President Trump’s leadership and the dedication of DHS law enforcement, <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/immigration/border-security" target="_blank" rel="noopener">America’s borders</a> are safer than any time in our nation’s history," Noem wrote. "What President Trump and our CBP agents and officers have been able to do in a single year is nothing short of extraordinary."</p><p>CBP reported zero parole releases in December, compared to 7,041 released along the southwest border in December 2024 under the Biden administration.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/opinion/trump-has-made-border-secure-again-now-hard-part-begins" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>TRUMP HAS MADE THE BORDER SECURE AGAIN — BUT NOW THE HARD PART BEGINS</strong></a></p><p>"Once again, we have a <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/trump-administration-touts-secure-border-history-2-5-million-migrants-exit-us" target="_blank" rel="noopener">record low number of encounters</a> at the border and the 8th straight month of zero releases," Noem said. "Month after month, we are delivering results that were once thought impossible: the most secure border in history and unmatched enforcement successes."</p><p>According to CBP, total nationwide encounters from October through December fell to 91,603, the lowest ever recorded at the start of a fiscal year and 25% below the previous record low set in 2012.</p><p>December alone saw just 30,698 encounters nationwide, a 92% drop from the Biden-era peak of 370,883 and the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/new-data-reveals-border-crossings-reach-record-lows-amid-trump-admins-crackdown" target="_blank" rel="noopener">lowest December total on record</a>, according to the federal agency.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/opinion/new-high-tech-tool-trump-using-secure-our-border" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>THE NEW HIGH-TECH TOOL TRUMP IS USING TO SECURE OUR BORDER</strong></a></p><p>Along the southwest border, <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/border-crossings-plummet-historic-lows-trumps-enforcement-policies-yield-big-results" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Border Patrol recorded just 21,815 apprehensions</a> in the first quarter of fiscal year 2026, marking a 95% drop compared to the Biden administration’s first-quarter average, CBP noted.</p><p>Border Patrol agents made 6,478 apprehensions in December along the southwest border, marking a 96% decrease from the Biden administration’s monthly average and fewer than what agents encountered in just four days in December 2024. </p><p>Border patrol agents averaged 209 apprehensions per day during the month, compared to Biden-era levels that exceeded that number every 1.5 hours, CBP wrote.</p><p>"Our agents and officers have set a new standard for border security, achieving historic results that speak for themselves," CBP Commissioner Rodney Scott said in a statement.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/new-data-reveals-border-crossings-reach-record-lows-amid-trump-admins-crackdown" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>NEW DATA REVEALS BORDER CROSSINGS REACH RECORD LOWS AMID TRUMP ADMIN'S CRACKDOWN</strong></a></p><p>CBP also seized 39,030 pounds of illicit drugs nationwide in December. </p><p>"The men and women of CBP are demonstrating what’s possible through unwavering commitment and effective enforcement and will continue to ensure the safety and security of our nation’s borders every single day," Scott added.</p>
