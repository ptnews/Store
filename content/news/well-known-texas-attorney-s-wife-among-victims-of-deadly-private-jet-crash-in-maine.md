<p>Family and friends are mourning after six people were killed when a private business jet bound for France crashed during takeoff from Bangor International Airport in <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/us-regions/northeast/maine" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Maine</a> Sunday night, erupting into flames and leaving no survivors.</p><p>The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) said the jet flipped and caught fire <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/travel/general/airports" target="_blank" rel="noopener">during departure</a> at about 7:45 p.m. during Winter Storm Fern.</p><p>Right before takeoff, someone was heard on the flight radio saying, "Let there be light," the <a href="https://nypost.com/2026/01/26/us-news/first-victim-in-deadly-maine-plane-crash-idd-as-wife-of-prominent-texas-law-firm-founder/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">New York Post</a> reported.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/business-jet-crashes-maine-airport-leaving-all-passengers-presumed-dead-winter-storm-pummels-us" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>BUSINESS JET CRASHES AT MAINE AIRPORT LEAVING ALL PASSENGERS PRESUMED DEAD AS WINTER STORM PUMMELS US</u></strong></a></p><p>Multiple emergency crews rushed to the accident, and controllers were heard saying, "Aircraft upside down. We have a passenger aircraft upside down."</p><p>FAA officials have not yet released the cause of the crash, citing "unknown circumstances" on departure. The National Transportation Safety Board (NTSB) has been notified and will investigate.</p><p>The jet was registered to a partner of personal injury law firm Arnold and Itkin Trial Lawyers in Houston.</p><p>Sources told local news station <a href="https://abc13.com/post/6-people-private-plane-tied-houston-law-firm-presumed-dead-crash-maine-officials-say/18480329/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">ABC13</a> Tara Arnold, an attorney at the firm and wife of founding partner Kurt Arnold, was aboard the doomed flight.</p><p>The Louisiana native was a 46-year-old mother of two, according to the <a href="https://www.arnolditkin.com/meet-our-attorneys/tara-arnold/#~44c77984-baf0-4abd-80af-bdbf54e73cbe" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">firm's website.</a></p><p>Kurt Arnold, who has won more than $20 billion in verdicts and settlements for his clients, and his business partner, Jason Itkin, were reportedly not on the flight.</p><p>Arnold and Itkin Trial Lawyers did not immediately respond to Fox News Digital's request for comment.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/faa-warns-about-flying-central-south-america-eastern-pacific-citing-possible-military-activities" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>FAA WARNS ABOUT FLYING IN CENTRAL, SOUTH AMERICA AND EASTERN PACIFIC, CITING POSSIBLE ‘MILITARY ACTIVITIES’</u></strong></a></p><p>The pilot, Jacob Hosmer, 47, also perished in the crash, his father, Gary, <a href="https://www.click2houston.com/news/local/2026/01/26/private-jet-with-ties-to-houston-crashes-at-maine-airport/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">told KPRC2.</a></p><p>Fellow aviator Bob Howie shared a photo of Hosmer on Facebook, describing him as a "very good friend."</p><p>"I’m on the right, but, on the left, is my very good friend Jacob Hosmer who was pilot-in-command last night when his Challenger 650 crashed on takeoff from Bangor, Maine," Howie wrote in <a href="https://www.facebook.com/bob.howie.75/posts/10236371845734939?ref=embed_post" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">a post</a>. "I knew Jake’s co-pilot equally well and had flown with him too. God bless, you guys! Godspeed and fair winds, fellas. Loved sharing the skies with y’all. Big loss."</p><p>Hosmer's cousin, David Lopez Jr., also took to social media following the tragedy, calling him "a role model."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxbusiness.com/lifestyle/thousands-flights-canceled-delayed-nationwide-amid-winter-storm-chaos" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>THOUSANDS OF FLIGHTS CANCELED OR DELAYED NATIONWIDE AMID WINTER STORM CHAOS</u></strong></a></p><p>"It's surreal to think my cousin Jacob is no longer with us. He was the light of the room and his big smile was infectious," Lopez wrote <a href="https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=10166838779174832&amp;set=a.10150137358069832&amp;type=3&amp;ref=embed_post" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">on Facebook</a>. "I don't know that I ever told him, but he was a role model to me and will be forever missed. Please pray for the entire Hosmer family during these difficult times."</p><p>Hosmer, an experienced pilot, started working for Arnold and Itkin in May 2025, according to his <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/jacob-hosmer-61705135" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">LinkedIn account.</a></p><p>A third victim was identified as Shawna Collins, according to ABC13.</p><p>Collins’ daughter reportedly told the outlet her mother, an event planner, was <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/travel/regions/europe" target="_blank" rel="noopener">traveling to Europe</a> for a business trip.</p><p>The identities of the six people killed on the flight have not yet been confirmed by authorities.</p><p>The <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/travel/general/airports" target="_blank" rel="noopener">airport canceled or diverted flights</a> after the crash and remained closed on Monday, according to a statement posted on the Bangor Maine Police Department Facebook page.</p><p><i>Fox News Digital's Alex Nitzberg, Greg Wehner and The Associated Press contributed to this report.</i></p>
