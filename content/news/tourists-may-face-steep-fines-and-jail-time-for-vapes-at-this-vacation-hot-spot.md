rel="noopener">heading to Mexico</a> may want to skip packing their e-cigarettes, as the country is now actively enforcing a nationwide ban on vaping devices, according to Mexico’s Chamber of Deputies.</p><p>The new rules took effect Jan. 17 after a <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/regulation/health-care" target="_blank" rel="noopener">constitutional reform</a> aimed at protecting public health was enacted.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/travel/smoking-vaping-may-banned-one-states-most-popular-beaches-parks-heres-why" target="_blank" electronic cigarettes, vapes or similar devices, according to the chamber's website.</p><p>It also prohibits the making, selling and handling of toxic substances and chemical ingredients, along with
