Lee Bright to introduce the legislation as a way to extend full legal protections to unborn children.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/pro-life-pregnancy-centers-see-client-increase-after-supreme-court-decision-study" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>PRO-LIFE PREGNANCY CENTERS SEE CLIENT INCREASE AFTER SUPREME COURT DECISION: STUDY</strong></a></p><p>"These children deserve equal protection.
