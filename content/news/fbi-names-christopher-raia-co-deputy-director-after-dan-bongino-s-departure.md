<p>FBI veteran Christopher Raia has been named co-deputy director of the federal law enforcement agency, the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/tech/topics/fbi" target="_blank" rel="noopener">bureau</a> confirmed Friday to Fox News Digital.</p><p>Raia, who runs the bureau's New York City field office, will move to Washington, D.C., and begin his job on Monday serving as co-deputy director with Andrew Bailey.</p><p>Raia’s elevation comes after Dan Bongino announced he was <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/dan-bongino-leaves-fbi-deputy-director-role-after-less-than-year-returns-civilian-life" target="_blank" rel="noopener">leaving the position</a> and returning to "civilian life." His last day on the job was Jan. 3.</p><p>Bongino was a conservative commentator and podcaster before President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/donald-trump" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Donald Trump</a> nominated him for the position.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/dan-bongino-leaves-fbi-deputy-director-role-after-less-than-year-returns-civilian-life" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>DAN BONGINO OFFICIALLY LEAVES FBI DEPUTY DIRECTOR ROLE AFTER LESS THAN A YEAR, RETURNS TO ‘CIVILIAN LIFE’</strong></a></p><p>"It’s been an incredible year thanks to the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/house-of-representatives/leadership" target="_blank" rel="noopener">leadership and decisiveness</a> of President Trump," Bongino wrote on X Saturday. "It was the honor of a lifetime to work with Director [Kash] Patel, and to serve you, the American people. See you on the other side."</p><p>Bongino made the announcement he was leaving last month, thanking Trump, Patel and U.S. Attorney General <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/pam-bondi" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Pam Bondi</a> "for the opportunity to serve with purpose."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/bondi-patel-tap-missouri-ag-additional-fbi-co-deputy-director-alongside-bongino" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>BONDI, PATEL TAP MISSOURI AG AS ADDITIONAL FBI CO-DEPUTY DIRECTOR ALONGSIDE BONGINO</strong></a></p><p>Bongino and Bondi had previously clashed over the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/fbis-dan-bongino-ag-bondi-clash-over-handling-epstein-files" target="_blank" rel="noopener">release of the Epstein files</a>, and a source told Fox News over the summer he had considered resigning over the Justice Department’s handling of the situation.</p><p>Bongino didn’t give a reason for his resignation less than a year after he started as deputy director, but Trump said last month the 51-year-old "wants to go back to his show."</p>
