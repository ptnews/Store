<p><strong>FIRST ON FOX:</strong> The House Judiciary Committee Subcommittee on Crime and Federal Government Surveillance is holding a hearing centered around various <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/minnesota-fraud-exposed" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Minnesota fraud scandals</a> and will feature testimony from Nick Shirley, a YouTuber and freelance journalist who helped uncover an organized fraud network in the state.</p><p>The hearing, "When Public Frauds are Abused: Addressing Fraud and the Theft of Taxpayer Dollars," will be held Jan. 21, and will focus on several cases of fraud that took place in the Land of 10,000 Lakes.</p><p>"I pulled up earlier today a report from last July, and they're interviewing [Minnesota] residents," Rep. Andy Biggs, R-Ariz., who chairs the subcommittee, told Fox News Digital. "You can't keep anything on your porch or in your yard because it gets stolen, you get your windows broken out of your car.</p><p> "<a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/police-and-law-enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener">It seems lawless</a>," he added.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/gop-lawmaker-moves-award-congressional-medal-journalist-who-exposed-minnesota-fraud" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>GOP LAWMAKER MOVES TO AWARD CONGRESSIONAL MEDAL FOR JOURNALIST WHO EXPOSED MINNESOTA FRAUD</strong></a></p><p>In 2022, federal officials in Minnesota launched an independent investigation into Feeding Our Future, a nonprofit that prosecutors later described as a key driver of what grew into one of the largest COVID-relief fraud schemes on record.</p><p>Over the course of the investigation, authorities uncovered an estimated $250 million in fraudulent claims, leading to criminal charges against 78 individuals. Prosecutors have said the total scope of the alleged fraud connected to the operation may ultimately approach $9 billion.</p><p>The hearing will feature testimony from Shirley in addition to Jennifer Larson, CEO of the Holland Autism Center and Clinic, and former Minnesota police officer and former Minnesota fraud investigator Scott Dexter.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/doj-send-prosecutors-crack-down-minnesota-fraud" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>PAM BONDI DISPATCHES FEDERAL PROSECUTORS TO MINNESOTA FOLLOWING SOMALI FRAUD ALLEGATIONS</strong></a></p><p>Judiciary Committee Chairman Rep. <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/jim-jordan" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Jim Jordan</a>, R-Ohio, will be joining members of the subcommittee during the hearing. </p><p>Rep. Laurel Lee, R-Fla., will also be in attendance, and told Fox News Digital the hearing’s purpose is a matter of "transparency, accountability and safeguards that prevent this kind of abuse from happening again."</p><p>"The scale of fraud uncovered in Minnesota is staggering, and it represents an egregious abuse of federal taxpayer dollars by criminal actors," Lee told Fox News Digital.
