<p>A North Georgia landmark could soon be renamed after President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/donald-trump" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Donald Trump</a> – if one state lawmaker has his way.</p><p>Rep. David Clark, R-Buford, introduced a resolution Tuesday to rename Sawnee Mountain in Forsyth County, located a little under 50 miles northeast of Atlanta, as "Trump Mountain," <a href="https://www.wsbtv.com/news/local/forsyth-county/lawmaker-wants-dub-metro-atlanta-landmark-trump-mountain/TE4W7DDE5ZA7LFSIGCZBTJMM6A/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">according to WSB-TV</a>.</p><p>"President Trump is one of the most transformative presidents in our nation’s history," Clark wrote in a <a href="https://www.facebook.com/votedavidclark/posts/1302893901645025?ref=embed_post" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">Facebook post</a> detailing the move.</p><p>"That’s why today I introduced a resolution to rename Sawnee Mountain in Forsyth County to Trump Mountain to honor his <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics" target="_blank" rel="noopener">historic legacy</a> of Making America Great Again."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/sanders-backed-bill-jabs-trump-narcissism-ban-self-named-federal-buildings" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>SANDERS-BACKED BILL JABS TRUMP ‘NARCISSISM’ WITH BAN ON SELF-NAMED FEDERAL BUILDINGS</strong></a></p><p>The decision drew backlash from some Trump critics who blasted the move in the comments.</p><p>"This is satire right?" one commenter wrote.</p><p>Another chimed in by saying, "Transformative is not a compliment."</p><p><a CHARLIE KIRK AFTER ASSASSINATION ROCKS CONSERVATIVE MOVEMENT</strong></a></p><p>Others pointed to the mountain's historical significance to the Native American community, drawing parallels between the name stems from a celebrated Native American chief. </p><p>Some commenters were more supportive of Clark's proposal.</p><p><a
