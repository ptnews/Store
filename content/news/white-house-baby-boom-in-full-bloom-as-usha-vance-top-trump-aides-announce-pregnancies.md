<p>The <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/executive/white-house" target="_blank" rel="noopener">White House</a> is experiencing a baby boom. </p><p>At least three women with close ties to the White House are pregnant, including second lady <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/usha-vance" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Usha Vance</a>, White House press secretary <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/karoline-leavitt" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Karoline Leavitt</a> and <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/entertainment/genres/family" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Katie Miller</a>, who is married to White House deputy chief of staff Stephen Miller. </p><p>The Vances announced Tuesday that they are <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/education/parents" target="_blank" rel="noopener">expecting their fourth child</a> in July. The couple share three children together: Ewan, Vivek and Mirabel. </p><p>"Usha and the baby are doing well, and we are all looking forward to welcoming him in late July," the Vances wrote in a statement shared on social media.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/vice-president-jd-vance-declares-marriage-wife-usha-is-strong-its-ever-been" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>VICE PRESIDENT JD VANCE DECLARES MARRIAGE TO WIFE USHA 'IS AS STRONG AS IT'S EVER BEEN'</strong></a></p><p>"During this exciting and hectic time, we are particularly grateful for the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/military/military-families" target="_blank" rel="noopener">military doctors</a> who take excellent care of our family and for the staff members who do so much to ensure that we can serve the country while enjoying a wonderful life with our children," they said. </p><p>The Vances have been married since 2014, and met while they were students at Yale Law School. </p><p>Leavitt announced Dec. 26, 2025, that she and her husband Nick were <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/karoline-leavitt-expecting-baby-girl-may-2026-says-motherhood-closest-thing-heaven-earth" target="_blank" rel="noopener">expecting their second child</a>, who is due in May. Leavitt and her husband welcomed their first child, Niko, in July 2024.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/vance-unleashes-profanity-laced-two-word-message-critics-his-wife-usha" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>VANCE UNLEASHES PROFANITY-LACED TWO-WORD MESSAGE FOR CRITICS OF HIS WIFE USHA</strong></a></p><p>"My husband and I are thrilled to grow our family and can’t wait to watch our son become a big brother," Leavitt told Fox News Digital. "My heart is overflowing with gratitude to God for the blessing of motherhood, which I truly believe is the closest thing to Heaven on Earth."</p><p>Leavitt told Fox News Digital in December 2025 that she is "extremely grateful to President Trump and our amazing Chief of Staff Susie Wiles for their support, and for fostering a pro-family environment in the White House."</p><p>"Nearly all of my West Wing colleagues have babies and young children, so we all really support one another as we tackle <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/lifestyle/parenting" target="_blank" rel="noopener">raising our families</a> while working for the greatest president ever," Leavitt said.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/media/former-doge-adviser-katie-miller-launches-new-podcast-aimed-busy-moms" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>FORMER DOGE ADVISER KATIE MILLER LAUNCHES NEW PODCAST AIMED AT BUSY MOMS</strong></a></p><p>Leavitt is the first press secretary to be pregnant, and is remaining press secretary, according to a senior White House official. </p><p>Likewise, <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/media/katie-miller-tells-ruthless-about-threats-against-family-from-leftists-kids-unsafe-front-yard" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Katie Miller, a conservative</a> podcast host, and Stephen Miller shared a joint Instagram post Dec. 31, 2025, celebrating the new year and depicting Katie Miller holding her baby bump. The couple shares three children: Mackenzie, Jackson and Hudson.</p><p>The White House did not immediately respond to a request for comment from Fox News Digital. </p><p><i>Fox News’ Brooke Singman and Alexandra Koch contributed to this report. </i></p>
