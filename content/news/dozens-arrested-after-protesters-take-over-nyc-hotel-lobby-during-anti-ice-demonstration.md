<p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/police-and-law-enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Police made dozens of arrests</a> after an anti-ICE protest broke out inside a New York City hotel Tuesday evening, according to reports.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/new-york-city" target="_blank" rel="noopener">New York City Police Department</a> officers responded to the Hilton Garden Inn on Sixth Avenue in Tribeca at around 6 p.m. after protesters took over the lobby and obstructed pedestrian traffic.</p><p>Sunrise Movement, an activist group, posted on X earlier Tuesday claiming the hotel was housing ICE officials.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/socialist-groups-ramp-up-nationwide-anti-ice-protests-target-noem-nyc" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>SOCIALIST GROUPS CHANT 'KILLER KRISTI' WHILE ESCALATING NATIONWIDE ANTI-ICE PROTESTS</strong></a></p><p>Videos posted on social media show protesters chanting inside the hotel and holding up signs, while refusing to leave the lobby.</p><p>Protesters can be heard chanting, "No ICE, No KKK, No Fascist USA" and "<a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/immigration/immigrant-rights" target="_blank" rel="noopener">ICE out of New York</a>."</p><p>In a separate video, protesters chant, "<a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/kristi-noem" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Kristi Noem</a> will hang," blatantly calling out the Department of Homeland Security secretary.</p><p>Sam Raskin, a spokesperson for New York City Mayor Zohran Mamdani, released a statement Tuesday in response to the protests and the police response.</p><p>"Mayor Mamdani commends the protestors who exercised their right to protest against ICE today," he said.
