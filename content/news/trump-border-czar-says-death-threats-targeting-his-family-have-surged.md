<p>Trump administration "border czar" <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/tom-homan" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Tom Homan</a> told Fox News that death threats against him have surged and include threats toward his family as anti-ICE rhetoric intensifies.</p><p>"Death threats against me alone have tripled," Homan said Wednesday on "<a href="https://www.foxnews.com/shows/the-faulkner-focus" target="_blank" rel="noopener">The Faulkner Focus</a>."</p><p>"They don’t want to kill me. They want to kill my family. You know, tear my soul out so I suffer the rest of my life," he added.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/doj-surging-federal-prosecutors-minneapolis-fraud-immigration-cases-after-resignations-sources" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>DOJ SURGING FEDERAL PROSECUTORS TO MINNEAPOLIS FOR FRAUD, IMMIGRATION CASES AFTER RESIGNATIONS: SOURCES</strong></a></p><p>Homan blamed what he called "<a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics" target="_blank" rel="noopener">hateful rhetoric</a>" from Democratic leaders for fueling the rise in real-world threats. He said the danger is not limited to him but extends to law enforcement officers across the country.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/media/border-patrol-chief-urges-illinois-follow-louisiana-blueprint-threats-against-agents-surge" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>BORDER PATROL CHIEF URGES ILLINOIS TO FOLLOW LOUISIANA BLUEPRINT AS THREATS AGAINST AGENTS SURGE</strong></a></p><p>"I said back in March … if the hateful rhetoric doesn't decrease, there’s going to be bloodshed," Homan said.</p><p>"Unfortunately, I was right."</p><p>The Department of Homeland Security released figures on Jan. 8 showing ICE officers have faced more than a <a href="https://www.dhs.gov/news/2026/01/08/radical-rhetoric-sanctuary-politicians-leads-unprecedented-1300-increase-assaults" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">1,300% increase in reported assaults</a>, a 3,200% rise in vehicular attacks and an 8,000% spike in death threats.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/ice-agents-report-unprecedented-3200-surge-car-attacks-last-year" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>ICE AGENTS REPORT ‘UNPRECEDENTED’ 3,200% SURGE IN CAR ATTACKS LAST YEAR</strong></a></p><p>Immigration enforcement operations have continued nationwide, with activity increasing in cities such as Minneapolis. Anti-ICE rhetoric rose in Minnesota after the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/media/minneapolis-mayor-says-unconstitutional-ice-conduct-has-been-met-peaceful-protests" target="_blank" rel="noopener">fatal shooting of Renee Nicole Good</a>.</p><p>The shooting sparked national debate over whether the federal officer’s use of deadly force was justified. Minneapolis Mayor Jacob Frey is among the Democratic leaders calling out immigration officers. </p><p>On Wednesday, on "Fox &amp; Friends," he <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/media/minneapolis-mayor-frey-refuses-walk-back-get-f-out-message-ice" target="_blank" rel="noopener">doubled down on his infamous comments</a> telling ICE to "get the f---" out of the city in the hours after Good’s death.</p><p>"I stand by exactly what I said," Frey told "<a href="https://www.foxnews.com/shows/fox-and-friends" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Fox &amp; Friends</a>."</p><p>The mayor said he does not support abolishing ICE but accused the Trump administration’s immigration enforcement of fueling "chaos" in the city.</p><p>In June 2025, Homan said he had been living apart from his wife due to threats. Homan said on the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/media/border-czar-tom-homan-says-hes-been-living-apart-from-his-wife-due-death-threats-nyp" target="_blank" rel="noopener">"Pod Force One" podcast</a> that his wife is living elsewhere for safety, calling the threats "outrageous." </p>
