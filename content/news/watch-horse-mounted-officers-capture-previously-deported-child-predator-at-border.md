<p>Horse-mounted Texas Department of Public Safety officers captured a previously deported <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/immigration/illegal-immigrants" target="_blank" rel="noopener">illegal alien</a> child predator close to the border.</p><p>In a statement Tuesday, the state agency said officers from its Border Mounted Patrol Unit made "multiple" illegal immigrant apprehensions on New Year’s Day as part of an effort called Operation Lone Star.</p><p>The agency said the operation took place in Maverick County along the U.S. border with Mexico, just south of the town of Del Rio, <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/us-regions/southwest/texas" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Texas</a>.</p><p>Among those arrested by Texas DPS officers was Marcio Steven Izaguirre, a 41-year-old Honduran illegal alien and child predator, according to the agency. DPS shared body camera video of the arrest in which horse-riding troopers discovered Izaguirre hiding in thick desert brush.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/video-shows-23-illegal-immigrants-found-hidden-truck-cab-during-tense-traffic-stop-police" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>VIDEO SHOWS 23 ILLEGAL IMMIGRANTS FOUND HIDDEN IN TRUCK CAB DURING TENSE TRAFFIC STOP: POLICE</strong></a></p><p>The video shows two mounted troopers riding quickly through rocky desert terrain and eventually discovering Izaguirre. The officers shouted commands for Izaguirre to exit the brush and handcuffed him and led him away in front of their horses.</p><p>The agency said it was alerted to the illegal’s presence by a drawbridge camera set off on a private ranch around 10:30 a.m. New Year’s Day.</p><p>DPS said, through further investigation, troopers learned that Izaguirre was a previously deported felon with multiple deportations dating back to 2004. The agency said Izaguirre also has a lengthy criminal history, including convictions from 2008 for furnishing alcohol to a minor, contributing to the delinquency of a minor and child molestation out of Cobb County, <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/us-regions/southeast/georgia" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Georgia</a>.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/two-arrested-dozens-guns-including-cop-killer-model-found-spare-tire-southern-border-dps" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>TWO ARRESTED AFTER DOZENS OF GUNS, INCLUDING 'COP-KILLER' MODEL, FOUND IN SPARE TIRE AT SOUTHERN BORDER: DPS</strong></a></p><p>The agency said it has since referred Izaguirre to U.S. Border Patrol.</p><p>Lt. Chris Olivarez, a Texas DPS spokesperson, said in an X post Jan. 1 that mounted officers, along with Border Patrol and aided by K-9 "Bona," apprehended five illegal immigrants on New Year’s Day.</p><p>Olivarez said the operation prevented gotaways and was a way of "keeping Texas safe."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/texas-woman-tries-flee-mexico-across-rio-grande-infant-after-human-smuggling-bust-authorities-say" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>TEXAS WOMAN TRIES TO FLEE TO MEXICO ACROSS RIO GRANDE WITH INFANT AFTER HUMAN SMUGGLING BUST, AUTHORITIES SAY</strong></a></p><p>Texas makes up well over half of the U.S. border with Mexico, around 1,254 miles. It is the only southern border state to explicitly ban <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/topic/sanctuary-cities" target="_blank" rel="noopener">sanctuary jurisdictions</a>. Texas also requires state and local law enforcement to cooperate with federal immigration authorities.</p><p>Texas law also mandates that local sheriffs cooperate with federal immigration enforcement by seeking to enter federal 287(g) agreements under the federal Immigration and Nationality Act.</p>
