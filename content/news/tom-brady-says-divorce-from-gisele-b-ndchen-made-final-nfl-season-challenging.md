<p>NFL legend <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/tom-brady" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Tom Brady</a> said off-the-field issues contributed to some of his struggles on the field during his final season in the NFL.</p><p>Brady was going through a divorce with <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/gisele-bundchen" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Gisele Bündchen</a> during his final season, and the seven-time Super Bowl champion said the issues in his personal life made it a challenge.</p><p>"My last season was tough," Brady said during a recent appearance on FOX Sports’ "The Herd."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/sports" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>CLICK HERE FOR MORE SPORTS COVERAGE ON FOXNEWS.COM</strong></a></p><p>"I was going through — I had a lot of, you know, just a personal, family issue, and it was a challenge. It was very ... it just took a lot out of me in terms of, you know, my ability to continue to play."</p><p>Brady’s final season with <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/sports/nfl/tampa-bay-buccaneers" target="_blank" rel="noopener">the Buccaneers</a> was the lone season in his career he had a record below .500, going 8-9 yet still winning the NFC South. In 17 games that season, Brady completed 66.8% of his passes for 4,694 yards with 25 touchdowns and nine interceptions.</p><p>While Brady considered that a challenging year, his 4,694 yards were still impressive. For context, Los Angeles Rams quarterback <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/matthew-stafford" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Matthew Stafford</a> led the NFL with 4,707 yards this season.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/sports/fox-super-6-contest-chris-the-bear-fallicas-nfl-divisional-round-predictions" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>FOX SUPER 6 CONTEST: CHRIS 'THE BEAR' FALLICA'S NFL DIVISIONAL ROUND PREDICTIONS</u></strong></a></p><p>Brady added that, during that season, he knew it was time for him to attend his kids’ games.</p><p>"I had 23 years of (playing in the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/sports/nfl" target="_blank" rel="noopener">NFL</a>), so I didn’t feel like I was missing anything, retiring. I felt like, ‘OK, this time I always had a goal — 45.’ I was 45 years old. I wanted to spend time with my kids, I felt like, 'OK, now it’s time for me to be at all my kids' games.' They’ve been to enough of their dad’s games."</p><p>Brady and Bündchen were married for 13 years and had two kids together before getting divorced in 2022.</p><p>Bündchen married her jiujitsu instructor Joaquim Valente in 2025 after going public with their relationship in 2023.
