<p>Democrats secured victories in two special elections for the State House on Tuesday, as the state reels from controversy over federal immigration enforcement.</p><p>Democrats Shelley Buck and Meg Luger-Nikolai won special elections for Districts 47A and 64A. Their victories mean the GOP and Democrats are now tied for seats in the Minnesota <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics" target="_blank" rel="noopener">State House</a> at 67-67.</p><p>Luger-Nikolai won her race with 95% of the vote, while Buck ran uncontested.</p><p>The State House is currently operating under a power-sharing agreement between Republicans and Democrats. The House Speaker is still Republican Lisa DeMuth, but most committees will have co-chairs from both parties.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/mob-mentality-endangers-officers-amid-anti-ice-unrest-chaos-minneapolis-retired-cops-warn" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>'MOB MENTALITY' ENDANGERS OFFICERS AMID ANTI-ICE UNREST AND CHAOS IN MINNEAPOLIS, RETIRED COPS WARN</strong></a></p><p>The elections came just days after federal agents <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/media/trump-laments-shooting-deaths-good-pretti-insists-minnesota-personnel-shuffle-not-pullback" target="_blank" rel="noopener">shot and killed Alex Pretti</a> in Minneapolis on Saturday. Pretti is the second U.S. citizen to be killed by federal agents in Minneapolis this month.</p><p>President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/donald-trump" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Donald Trump</a>'s administration issued a report on the timeline of the shooting to Congress on Tuesday.</p><p>Border Patrol agents and Customs and Border Protection officers were active in Minneapolis Saturday morning as part of Operation Metro Surge.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/anti-ice-agitators-clash-federal-agents-minneapolis-hotel-agents-deploy-tear-gas-flashbangs" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>ANTI-ICE AGITATORS CLASH WITH FEDERAL AGENTS AT MINNEAPOLIS HOTEL, AS AGENTS DEPLOY TEAR GAS, FLASHBANGS</strong></a></p><p>Officials said Border Patrol agents tried to take Pretti into custody, but he "resisted," and a struggle ensued.</p><p>During the struggle, an unidentified Border Patrol agent yelled, "He's got a gun!" multiple times, according to the report.</p><p>About five seconds later, the two federal agents opened fire.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/media/tim-walz-accuses-trump-organized-brutality-immigration-crackdown-says-ice-tactics-un-american" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>TIM WALZ
