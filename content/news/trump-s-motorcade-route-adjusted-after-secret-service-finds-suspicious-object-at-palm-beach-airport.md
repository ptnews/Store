<p>A suspicious object discovered during a security sweep at Palm Beach International Airport ahead of President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/donald-trump" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Donald Trump</a>’s departure from Mar-a-Lago in Florida on Sunday prompted the U.S. Secret Service to adjust the presidential motorcade route, the White House said.</p><p>The discovery did not disrupt Trump’s travel schedule, officials said, as agents evaluated the item and made security adjustments out of an abundance of caution.</p><p>"During advance sweeps of PBI Airport, a suspicious object was discovered by USSS," White House press secretary <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/karoline-leavitt" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Karoline Leavitt</a> said. "A further investigation was warranted and the presidential motorcade route was adjusted accordingly."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/prosecutors-present-final-witness-tying-ryan-routh-trumps-golf-club-gun-purchase" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>PROSECUTORS PRESENT FINAL WITNESS TYING RYAN ROUTH TO TRUMP'S GOLF CLUB AND GUN PURCHASE</strong></a></p><p>The latest security concern follows a separate incident months earlier, when the U.S. Secret Service discovered a suspicious hunting stand positioned with a direct line of sight to where Trump exits Air Force One at Palm Beach International Airport.</p><p>That find was made in October, when agents located the elevated stand but did not find anyone in the surrounding area.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/tony-hawk-taiwan-flashlight-trump-assassination-attempt-suspects-bizarre-defense" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>TONY HAWK, TAIWAN AND A FLASHLIGHT: TRUMP ASSASSINATION ATTEMPT SUSPECT’S BIZARRE DEFENSE</u></strong></a></p><p>"Prior to the President’s return to West Palm Beach, USSS discovered what appeared to be an elevated hunting stand within sight line of the Air Force One landing zone," FBI Director <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/kash-patel" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Kash Patel</a> told Fox News Digital at the time.
