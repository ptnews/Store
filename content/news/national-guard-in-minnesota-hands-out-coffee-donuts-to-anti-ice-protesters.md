<p>The <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/military/national-guard" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">Minnesota National Guard</a> was seen handing out coffee and donuts to anti-ICE protesters in St. Paul on Sunday.</p><p>Video captured guardsmen in yellow vests offering donuts and coffee out the back of a vehicle. One National Guard member told WCCO-TV that this was a "demonstration of safety and security."</p><p>The Minnesota National Guard posted on X earlier this month that if troops were deployed to the Twin Cities, they would wear reflective vests "to help distinguish them from other agencies in similar uniforms."</p><p>The guardsmen in the video were seen one day after a Border Patrol agent shot and killed 37-year-old <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/us-regions/midwest/minnesota" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">Minneapolis</a> resident Alex J. Pretti after he allegedly confronted officers during a Department of Homeland Security operation in south Minneapolis.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/anti-ice-rapid-response-network-flagged-agents-before-deadly-minneapolis-killing-sparked-socialist" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>THE FAR-LEFT NETWORK THAT HELPED PUT ALEX PRETTI IN HARM'S WAY, THEN MADE HIM A MARTYR</strong></a></p><p>Federal officials initially <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/alex-pretti-37-identified-man-fatally-shot-border-patrol-agent-minneapolis" target="_blank" rel="noopener">claimed Pretti</a> approached agents with a handgun and resisted disarmament.</p><p>Bystander video and eyewitness accounts circulating online raised questions about that version of events and whether Pretti was threatening officers when he was shot.</p><p>Pretti’s death happened just weeks after an ICE officer fatally shot 37-year-old Renee Nicole Good while she allegedly blocked an operation in Minneapolis and drove toward the officer.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/tim-walz-compares-minnesota-ice-actions-holocaust-anne-frank-hiding-houses" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>TIM WALZ COMPARES MINNESOTA ICE ACTIONS TO HOLOCAUST AND ANNE FRANK: 'HIDING IN THEIR HOUSES'</strong></a></p><p>After the shooting of Good, Democratic officials, including Minnesota Gov. Tim Walz and Minneapolis Mayor Jacob Frey, encouraged anti-ICE protesters to assemble, <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/federal-prosecutors-open-investigation-walz-frey-over-alleged-impeding-law-enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">prompting a probe by federal prosecutors</a> for the alleged impeding of law enforcement efforts.</p><p>Walz on Sunday called for President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/donald-trump" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Donald Trump</a> to pull out "his 3,000 untrained agents" from Minnesota "before they kill another American in the street" following Pretti's death.</p><p>Trump confirmed to The Wall Street Journal on Sunday that his administration is "<a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/trump-confirms-federal-review-minneapolis-shooting-killed-nurse-reviewing-everything" target="_blank" rel="noopener">reviewing everything</a>" in the wake of the latest shooting.</p><p><i>Fox News Digital’s Emma Bussey and Alexandra Koch contributed to this report.</i></p>
