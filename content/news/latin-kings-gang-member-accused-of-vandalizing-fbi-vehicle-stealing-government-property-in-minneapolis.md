<p>A member of the violent Latin Kings gang was arrested after allegedly stealing government property from an FBI vehicle vandalized during unrest <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/minneapolis-st-paul" target="_blank" rel="noopener">in Minneapolis</a> Wednesday night, federal authorities said.</p><p>Fox News confirmed that Raul Gutierrez, 33, was arrested Thursday in a joint operation involving the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/justice-department" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Department of Justice</a> (DOJ) and the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives (ATF).</p><p>The FBI said multiple government vehicles were vandalized and broken into Wednesday night in Minneapolis while agents were responding to a reported assault on a federal officer, adding that federal property was stolen from inside the vehicles.</p><p>"One individual who allegedly stole federal government property out of an FBI vehicle in Minneapolis last night has been arrested," FBI Director <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/kash-patel" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Kash Patel</a> wrote on X, adding that the suspect was a member of the Latin Kings gang with a violent criminal history. "FBI personnel are continuing to pursue other subjects involved. There will be more arrests."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/protesters-clash-federal-officers-after-another-ice-shooting-minneapolis" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>PROTESTERS CLASH WITH FEDERAL OFFICERS AFTER ANOTHER ICE SHOOTING IN MINNEAPOLIS</strong></a></p><p>Patel added that "any individual who attacks law enforcement or <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/terror/vandalism" target="_blank" rel="noopener">vandalizes federal property</a> paid for by hardworking taxpayers will be found and arrested."</p><p>Attorney General <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/pam-bondi" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Pam Bondi</a> said the suspect allegedly stole FBI body armor and weaponry, and has a history of violent crimes.</p><p>"This criminal is a perfect example of what our brave <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/police-and-law-enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener">federal law enforcement agents</a> are up against every day as Minnesota leadership ENCOURAGES lawbreaking," she wrote in a post on X.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/dhs-arrests-armed-man-extra-ammunition-assaulting-federal-officer-late-night-minneapolis-riot" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>DHS ARRESTS ARMED MAN WITH EXTRA AMMUNITION FOR ASSAULTING FEDERAL OFFICER AT LATE-NIGHT MINNEAPOLIS RIOT</strong></a></p><p>White House border czar Tom Homan said Thursday on the "The Ingraham Angle" that the individual who stole a firearm from the FBI "is now wearing a set of handcuffs in custody," adding that additional arrests may be on the way amid ongoing clashes between agitators and federal law enforcement in Minneapolis.</p><p>"Others are coming," he said. "They're gonna be held accountable."</p><p>The alleged theft came as protests erupted in Minneapolis Wednesday following an Immigration and Customs Enforcement operation, and after the Department of Homeland Security said an <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/ice-agent-shoots-venezuelan-national-minneapolis-shovel-attack-ambush-dhs" target="_blank" rel="noopener">ICE agent shot an illegal immigrant</a> from Venezuela in the leg after an alleged shovel attack during an ambush.</p><p>Fox News Digital has reached out to the Department of Justice.</p>
