<p>A masked 18-year-old man carjacked a woman at gunpoint in <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/us-regions/southeast/florida" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Orange County, Florida</a>, telling her he needed the vehicle to go see his grandmother, authorities said.</p><p>Deputies arrested Kenneth Shears late Saturday after a brief chase that ended when he crashed the stolen car into another vehicle parked on a front lawn, according to the Orange County Sheriff’s Office. Investigators said Shears has never had a driver’s license.</p><p>The victim spoke out about the ordeal to <a href="https://www.fox35orlando.com/news/deputies-sorry-carjacking-suspect-stole-suv-go-see-grandmother-crashed-during-chase" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow">FOX35 Orlando</a> on condition of anonymity, saying she was sitting in her car when Shears approached with a gun pointed at her. At first, she thought it was a joke.</p><p>"I am sorry to be doing this, but I need to see my grandmother," Shears told the victim, per the arrest report.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/armed-carjackers-wild-corvette-rampage-turns-downtown-war-zone-sergeant-wounded-deadly-shootout" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>ARMED CARJACKER’S WILD CORVETTE RAMPAGE TURNS DOWNTOWN INTO WAR ZONE AS SERGEANT WOUNDED IN DEADLY SHOOTOUT</strong></a></p><p>She said she pleaded with him for several minutes and offered to give him a ride. Shears promised to return the car, authorities said.</p><p>"He looked like something was wrong," she told the outlet. "If he was robbing me, he could’ve <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/robbery-theft" target="_blank" rel="noopener">taken all my jewelry</a>. I had on jewelry. He could’ve taken my money. I had money on me and credit cards. He could’ve taken my bag. It had my laptop in it. He didn’t take anything."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/florida-officer-shot-face-during-service-call-tied-mental-health-dispute-suspect-killed" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>FLORIDA OFFICER SHOT IN FACE DURING SERVICE CALL TIED TO MENTAL HEALTH DISPUTE; SUSPECT KILLED</u></strong></a></p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/police-and-law-enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Deputies</a> said Shears led them on a short chase before crashing into a parked car, totaling both vehicles.</p><p>Shears faces <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/crime" target="_blank" rel="noopener">multiple charges,</a> including carjacking with a firearm, and is being held at the Orange County Jail.</p>
