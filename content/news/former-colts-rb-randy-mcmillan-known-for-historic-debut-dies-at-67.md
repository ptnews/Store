McMillan quickly captured the hearts of Colts fans with a historic performance in his NFL debut.</p><p>In his first pro game against the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/sports/nfl/new-england-patriots" target="_blank" rel="noopener">New England Patriots,</a> McMillan ran for 146 yards, scoring two touchdowns to help lift Baltimore to a 29-28 victory in September 1981.
