<p>Recently departed Washington Gov. <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/jay-inslee" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Jay Inslee</a> caught blowback Wednesday for demanding that Congress insert a provision in the upcoming spending bill to <a href="https://x.com/JayInslee/status/2016308583408009321" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow">ban ICE agents</a> from congregating near polls on Election Day.</p><p>Critics ratioed Inslee online, saying he admitted Democrats’ long-held claim — to the contrary — that they do not want illegal immigrants voting or that illegal immigrants are not voting.</p><p>Inslee, a 2020 Democratic presidential candidate, <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/voting" target="_blank" rel="noopener">called the ban a "must have"</a> and said it is the "last chance to do this."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/dems-blasted-trying-deport-ice-from-swing-county-referencing-blood-money-rent" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>DEMS BLASTED FOR TRYING TO ‘DEPORT’ ICE FROM SWING COUNTY, REFERENCING ‘BLOOD MONEY’ RENT</strong></a></p><p>"In the midst of ICE horror, the Senate needs to prohibit Trump from using ICE as a <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/state-and-local/elections-disputes" target="_blank" rel="noopener">voter suppression</a> tool," Inslee said.</p><p>"Senate Dems need to put into the approp[riation]s bill a prohibition on ICE agents being near polling places."</p><p>Federalist editor Sean Davis remarked that Inslee should "finish his thought," suggesting it would expose the truth about Democrats’ fervent opposition to Trump-era immigration enforcement and deportations.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/media/swalwell-promises-elected-governor-former-ice-agents-would-un-hirable-california" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>SWALWELL PROMISES IF ELECTED GOVERNOR, FORMER ICE AGENTS WOULD BE 'UN-HIRABLE' IN CALIFORNIA</strong></a></p><p>"Wait.
