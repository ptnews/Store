<p><strong>FIRST ON FOX:</strong> The Trump administration is ending <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/somali-immigrant-community" target="_blank" rel="noopener">temporary protection status (TPS) for Somalia</a>, affecting several thousand Somalis currently living in the U.S. and several hundred currently living in Minnesota under the protection.</p><p>Somali migrants with TPS will be required to leave the country by March 17. </p><p>"Temporary means temporary," DHS Secretary <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/kristi-noem" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Kristi Noem</a> told Fox News Digital in a statement. "Country conditions in Somalia have improved to the point that it no longer meets the law’s requirement for Temporary Protected Status." </p><p>"Further, allowing Somali nationals to remain temporarily in the United States is contrary to our national interests," Noem added. "We are putting Americans first."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/ilhan-omar-kicked-out-ice-facility-after-dhs-requires-weeks-advance-notice" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>ILHAN OMAR KICKED OUT OF ICE FACILITY AFTER DHS REQUIRES WEEK'S ADVANCE NOTICE</strong></a></p><p>Sources at USCIS told Fox News Digital that there are 2,471 Somali nationals currently in the U.S. under TPS, with 1,383 in the country with pending TPS applications. </p><p>The move comes as DHS continues ICE operations in Minneapolis, Minnesota, after a widespread roughly $9 billion fraud scheme involving members of the Somali community plagued the state. </p><p>Fox News Digital learned that an estimated 600 Somali nationals who are protected by TPS live in Minnesota.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/media/kristi-noem-tells-cnns-jake-tapper-he-cant-change-facts-about-minnesota-ice-shooting" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>KRISTI NOEM TELLS CNN'S JAKE TAPPER THAT HE CAN'T 'CHANGE THE FACTS' ABOUT MINNESOTA ICE SHOOTING</strong></a></p><p>Somalia was granted temporary protection status in 1991 as a result of an ongoing civil war in the African nation. President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/joe-biden" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Joe Biden</a> most recently extended the protection in September 2024.</p><p>The decision to end TPS for Somalia also comes a day after the Twin Cities, Minneapolis and St. Paul, <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/minnesota-sues-trump-admin-over-sweeping-immigration-raids-twin-cities" target="_blank" rel="noopener">sued the Trump administration</a> after a historic federal immigration enforcement operation was launched by ICE and DHS.</p><p>"We allege that the obvious targeting of Minnesota for our diversity, for our democracy and our differences of opinion with the federal government is a violation of the Constitution and of federal law," <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/us-regions/midwest/minnesota" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Minnesota Attorney General</a> Keith Ellison told reporters at a press conference on Monday.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/bessent-blames-walz-treasury-probes-whether-minnesota-fraud-funds-reached-terror-group-al-shabab" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>BESSENT BLAMES WALZ AS TREASURY PROBES WHETHER MINNESOTA FRAUD FUNDS REACHED TERROR GROUP AL-SHABAB</strong></a></p><p>"DHS agents have sown chaos and terror across the metropolitan area," Ellison added.</p><p>Last week, during ICE operations in south Minneapolis, <a immigration officer as she drove her vehicle toward agents, prompting agitators to riot across the city in the following days.</p><p>Noem labeled the woman who was killed, Renee Nicole Good, as a "domestic terrorist," asserting that Good "weaponized her vehicle" after blocking ICE agents on the road.</p><p><a Streets were blocked off with rioters directing traffic and <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/police-and-law-enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener">little to no local police presence</a> was seen in the area.</p><p>Despite the unrest, DHS operations continued in Minneapolis, prompting <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/anti-ice-agitators-threaten-agents-chaotic-minnesota-protests-f-ing-die" target="_blank" rel="noopener">clashes between agitators and immigration agents</a>.</p>
