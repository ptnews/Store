<p>A body was found Tuesday evening as authorities in Bexar County, Texas, grapple with a series of <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/topic/missing-persons" target="_blank" rel="noopener">disappearances involving teen girls</a> who all went missing within a week.</p><p>The body was found during the search for 19-year-old Camila Olmos, though police said it is too early to determine whether the remains belong to her.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/us-regions/southwest/texas" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><u>Bexar County</u></a> Sheriff Javier Salazar said during a news conference that investigators had "just recently found a body in a field" around 4:40 to 4:45 p.m., adding that the medical examiner will determine both the identity of the body and the cause and manner of death.</p><p>The body was found by a joint team of sheriff’s deputies and FBI agents in an area of tall grass near a landscaping business, a few hundred yards from Olmos’ home, according to Salazar.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/missing-camila-mendoz-olmos-dashcam-captures-last-sighting-texas-teen-who-vanished-christmas-eve" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>MISSING CAMILA MENDOZ OLMOS: DASHCAM CAPTURES LAST SIGHTING OF TEXAS TEEN WHO VANISHED CHRISTMAS EVE</u></strong></a></p><p>A firearm was recovered near the body, which authorities said had been an item of interest during the search.</p><p>Salazar said investigators do not currently suspect murder and noted there were indicators consistent with possible self-harm, though he stressed it is too early to draw conclusions while the scene is still being processed.</p><p>Olmos was last seen <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/texas-19-year-old-camila-mendoza-olmos-vanishes-outside-her-home-christmas-eve" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><u>leaving her home</u></a> in far northwest Bexar County around 7 a.m. on Dec. 24, authorities said. Her case came as two other girls were reported missing in the area. Fourteen-year-old Sofia Gabriela Peters-Cobos has since been found safe, while 17-year-old Angelique Johnson remains missing, according to police.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/police-and-law-enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><u>Law enforcement</u></a> has said there is no confirmed connection between the cases.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/missing-camila-mendoz-olmos-dashcam-captures-last-sighting-texas-teen-who-vanished-christmas-eve" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>MISSING CAMILA MENDOZ OLMOS: DASHCAM CAPTURES LAST SIGHTING OF TEXAS TEEN WHO VANISHED CHRISTMAS EVE</strong></a></p><p>Texas Department of Public Safety said the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/missing-19-year-old-camila-mendoza-olmos-believed-imminent-danger-texas-sheriff-says" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><u>Clear Alert for Olmos</u></a> has been discontinued, though authorities urged anyone with information related to the cases or the whereabouts of Angelique Johnson to contact the Bexar County Sheriff’s Office.</p><p>The Bexar County Sheriff's Office and the San Antonio Police Department did not respond immediately to Fox News Digital's requests for comment.<br><br><i>Fox News Digital's Greg Norman and Michael Ruiz contributed to this report.</i></p>
