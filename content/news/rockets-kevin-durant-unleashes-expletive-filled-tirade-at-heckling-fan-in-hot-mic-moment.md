<p>Videos circulating across social media appeared to show <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/kevin-durant" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Kevin Durant</a> locked in a heated exchange with a fan seated courtside during Friday’s Rockets-Pistons game.</p><p>Durant grew irritated and eventually directed a profanity-laced rant at the fan, who wore Durant’s No. 7 <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/sports/nba/brooklyn-nets" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Brooklyn Nets</a> jersey. Durant joined the Nets in 2020 before being traded to the Phoenix Suns in 2023.</p><p>"Who the f--- you talking to, you fat m-----f–ker?" Durant appeared to say at the beginning of the first clip. "You ain’t gonna do s---! … Watch your f------ mouth."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/sports" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>CLICK HERE FOR MORE SPORTS COVERAGE ON FOXNEWS.COM</strong></a></p><p>A staff member intervened, but Durant continued to briefly jaw at the fan before eventually walking away.
