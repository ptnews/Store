<p>A Florida man has gone viral for <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/education/patriotism" target="_blank" rel="noopener">carrying an American flag</a> during long-distance runs, promoting unity and healthy living as he prepares to run a half-marathon in every state in 2026.</p><p>"I love this country, and I’m trying to spread as much love as possible," Teag "Pure Joy" McCoy told "Fox &amp; Friends" Tuesday.</p><p>McCoy said the idea began during a quiet moment in the bed of his truck, when he prayed for <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/faith-values" target="_blank" rel="noopener">a way to make a difference</a> in the world and the country.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/opinion/amid-division-noise-simple-truth-america-still-has-heart" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>AMID DIVISION AND NOISE, A SIMPLE TRUTH: AMERICA STILL HAS A HEART</strong></a></p><p>"It was a real windy night and I saw that flag waving. I picked her up the next day and I <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/sports/running" target="_blank" rel="noopener">ran for 22 miles</a> and the rest is history."</p><p>Since then, McCoy said about 90% of the reactions he receives "are extremely joyous."</p><p>"I get a lot of people saying, 'I love you' and 'God bless you.' We tell them we love them," he said.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/health/5-fitness-trends-went-viral-2025-experts-weigh-hype-vs-results" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>5 FITNESS TRENDS THAT WENT VIRAL IN 2025 — EXPERTS WEIGH HYPE VS RESULTS</strong></a></p><p>When asked if he had a message to the country heading into the new year, McCoy invited Americans to join him.</p><p><i>"</i>Get ready, I'm coming to your town," he said. "<a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/health/wellness" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Prioritize your health</a>, grab that flag and become patriotic again, baby. I love y'all."</p>
