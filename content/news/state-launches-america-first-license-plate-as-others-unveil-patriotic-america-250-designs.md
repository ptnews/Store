Steve Gooch, a Republican, and signed by Georgia Gov. <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/brian-kemp" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Brian Kemp</a> in May.</p><p>The plate features an American flag background, with the words "America First" shown at the bottom.</p><p><a the forefront," said Gooch at the time.</p><p>"It provides Georgians with an opportunity to express their national pride and underscores the importance of emphasizing domestic concerns like economic stability, national security, strong borders and the protection of the rights of our citizens," he added.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/newsletters?cmpid=fnfirstnl" target="_blank"
