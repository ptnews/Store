<p>Former President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/bill-clinton" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Bill Clinton</a> appears to have defied a congressional subpoena to appear before the House Oversight Committee on Tuesday morning.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/bill-clinton-scheduled-appear-before-oversight-committee-contempt-congress-threat-looms" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Clinton was compelled</a> to sit for a sworn closed-door deposition in the House's bipartisan probe into Jeffrey Epstein, but Fox News Digital did not see him before or after the scheduled 10 a.m. grilling.</p><p>House Oversight Committee Chairman James Comer, R-Ky., had threatened to begin contempt of Congress proceedings against Clinton if he did not appear Tuesday.</p><p>Comer said Tuesday morning, "We will move next week in the House Oversight Committee … to hold Bill Clinton in contempt of Congress."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/top-gop-chair-issues-stark-warning-clintons-defy-depositions-epstein-investigation" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>TOP GOP CHAIR ISSUES STARK WARNING TO CLINTONS IF THEY DEFY DEPOSITIONS IN EPSTEIN INVESTIGATION</strong></a></p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/doj-says-lawyers-working-clock-prep-epstein-files-release-missed-deadline" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>DOJ SAYS LAWYERS WORKING 'AROUND THE CLOCK' TO PREP EPSTEIN FILES FOR RELEASE AFTER MISSED DEADLINE</u></strong></a></p><p>Other lawmakers seen going into the committee room include Reps. Lauren Boebert, R-Colo., and Scott Perry, R-Pa.</p><p>Hillary Clinton had also been subpoenaed to appear on Wednesday, but likely will not show up.</p><p>The Clintons’ attorney sent Comer a letter confirming they’re challenging the legality of the subpoenas issued against them.</p><p>"[T]he Subpoenas issued to President and Secretary Clinton are invalid and legally unenforceable. Mindful of these defects, we trust you will engage in good faith to de-escalate this dispute," reads the letter, obtained by Fox News Digital.</p>
