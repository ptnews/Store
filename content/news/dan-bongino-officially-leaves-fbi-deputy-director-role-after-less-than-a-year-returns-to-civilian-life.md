<p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/dan-bongino" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Dan Bongino</a> returned to private life on Sunday after serving as deputy director of the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) for less than a year.</p><p>Bongino said on X that Saturday was his last day on the job before he would return to "civilian life."</p><p>"It’s been an incredible year thanks to the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/house-of-representatives/leadership" target="_blank" rel="noopener">leadership and decisiveness</a> of President Trump. It was the honor of a lifetime to work with Director Patel, and to serve you, the American people. See you on the other side," he wrote.</p><p>The <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/tech/topics/fbi" target="_blank" rel="noopener">former FBI deputy director</a> announced in mid-December that he would be <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/dan-bongino-resign-from-fbi-deputy-director-role-january" target="_blank" rel="noopener">leaving his role</a> at the bureau at the start of the new year.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/bondi-patel-tap-missouri-ag-additional-fbi-co-deputy-director-alongside-bongino" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>BONDI, PATEL TAP MISSOURI AG AS ADDITIONAL FBI CO-DEPUTY DIRECTOR ALONGSIDE BONGINO</strong></a></p><p>President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/donald-trump" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Donald Trump</a> previously praised Bongino, who assumed office in March, for his work at the FBI.</p><p>"Dan did a great job. I think he wants to go back to his show," Trump told reporters.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/media/fbi-director-top-doj-official-respond-failing-ny-times-article-claiming-disdain-each-other" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>FBI DIRECTOR, TOP DOJ OFFICIAL RESPOND TO 'FAILING' NY TIMES ARTICLE CLAIMING 'DISDAIN' FOR EACH OTHER</strong></a></p><p>Bongino spoke publicly about the personal toll of the job during a May appearance on "Fox &amp; Friends," saying he had sacrificed a lot to take the role.</p><p>"I gave up everything for this," he said, citing the long hours both he and FBI Director <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/kash-patel" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Kash Patel</a> work.</p><p>"I stare at these four walls all day in D.C., by myself, divorced from my wife — not divorced, but I mean separated — and it’s hard. I mean, we love each other, and it’s hard to be apart," he added.</p><p>Bongino’s departure leaves Andrew Bailey, who was <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/new-fbi-deputy-director-andrew-bailey-begins-work-alongside-bongino" target="_blank" rel="noopener">appointed co-deputy director</a> in September 2025, as the bureau’s other deputy director.</p>
