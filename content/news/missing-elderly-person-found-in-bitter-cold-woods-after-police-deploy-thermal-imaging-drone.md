<p>Authorities in Pennsylvania revealed Monday that officers located a missing <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/health/geriatric-health" target="_blank" rel="noopener">elderly person</a> in a wooded area amid bitter cold temperatures, thanks to the use of a drone equipped with thermal imaging.</p><p>The <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/police-and-law-enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener">West Chester Police</a> Department (WCPD) said an officer, cross-trained as a drone pilot, stepped in after the department was contacted by a neighboring agency to assist in a search and rescue effort on Sunday.</p><p>The pilot used a drone equipped with thermal imaging and a spotlight to help guide officers on the ground to the person's location.</p><p>Photos released by authorities show the thermal imaging outline of the missing person as well as crews working on the scene.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/video-captures-daring-helicopter-rescue-base-jumper-dangling-canyon-wall" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>VIDEO CAPTURES DARING HELICOPTER RESCUE OF BASE JUMPER DANGLING FROM CANYON WALL</strong></a></p><p>"Last evening, West Chester Police was requested by a neighboring agency to assist with the search of an elderly person who was lost," the agency said in a post on social media. </p><p>"A WCPD officer who is cross trained as a <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/tech/technologies/drones" target="_blank" rel="noopener">drone pilot</a> responded to the scene. Our officer used the drone, which is equipped with thermal imaging and a spotlight. With the drone, we located the elderly person in a wooded area, and our officer was able to direct officers on the ground to the person’s location."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/hikers-wilderness-adventure-ends-tragedy-popular-park-destination" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>HIKER’S WILDERNESS ADVENTURE ENDS IN TRAGEDY AT POPULAR PARK DESTINATION</u></strong></a></p><p>Authorities said medical care was "delivered swiftly."</p><p>WCPD said technology, such as drones, played a key role in the safe recovery of the missing elderly person.</p><p>"We are proud to say that this is an example of us <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/tech/topics/smart-and-safe-tech" target="_blank" rel="noopener">leveraging technology with training</a> to deliver high quality service to our community," police said. "It’s another way we are helping to build safer and stronger communities."<br><br>Fox News Digital reached out to West Chester Police Department for more information.</p>
