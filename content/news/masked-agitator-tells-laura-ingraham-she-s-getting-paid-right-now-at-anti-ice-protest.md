<p>A tense exchange during <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/minneapolis-st-paul" target="_blank" rel="noopener">anti-ICE protests in Minneapolis</a> took an unexpected turn when a demonstrator said that she was being paid to be there. Fox News host Laura Ingraham was on the ground in Minneapolis as demonstrations erupted nationwide after a woman was killed during a confrontation with federal agents.</p><p>"Do you have a job?" Ingraham asked a masked protester outside the Whipple Federal Building.</p><p>"I’m [getting] <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/us-protests" target="_blank" rel="noopener">paid right now</a>," replied the woman, though it was unclear if she was serious.</p><p>Before the exchange, the woman had been walking through the crowd shouting "shame" and profanity. When Ingraham asked why her face was covered, the woman said it was because her "nose is cold."</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/clinton-amplifies-left-wing-murder-claims-minneapolis-ice-shooting-sharp-trump-rebuke" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>CLINTON AMPLIFIES LEFT-WING ‘MURDER’ CLAIMS IN MINNEAPOLIS ICE SHOOTING WITH SHARP TRUMP REBUKE</strong></a></p><p>Hundreds of <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/minneapolis-protesters-confront-federal-agents-day-after-deadly-shooting" target="_blank" rel="noopener">protesters gathered in Minneapolis</a> and in other cities after a woman was shot and killed during a confrontation with federal agents. The Department of Homeland Security said Renee Nicole Good was shot and killed after she attempted to <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/noem-alleges-woman-killed-ice-shooting-stalking-impeding-agents-all-day" target="_blank" rel="noopener">"weaponize her vehicle"</a> to hit law enforcement agents.</p><p>Minnesota officials have disputed that account, claiming the woman did not pose a threat and calling for an investigation.</p><p>Ingraham later said that while some demonstrators appeared peaceful, others became hostile toward the media trying to ask basic questions.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/ice-agents-actions-minneapolis-shooting-were-absolutely-reasonable-expert" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>ICE AGENT’S ACTIONS IN MINNEAPOLIS SHOOTING WERE ‘ABSOLUTELY REASONABLE’: EXPERT</strong></a></p><p>"If you actually go into the mix and try to chat with people, you end up getting escorted out. And they kind of nicely, but aggressively escort you out across their border," she said.</p><p>Ingraham added that when she and her crew later left the area, protesters held up their middle fingers as they drove away.</p><p>The Minneapolis Public School District announced it was <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/minnesota-schools-shut-down-teachers-union-demands-ice-leave-city" target="_blank" rel="noopener">canceling classes due to</a> "safety concerns" in the city. Several businesses also shut their doors, citing safety concerns or expressing solidarity with demonstrators.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/entertainment/john-mulaney-postpones-minneapolis-tour-dates-fatal-ice-shooting-renee-nicole-good" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>JOHN MULANEY POSTPONES MINNEAPOLIS SHOWS AFTER DEADLY ICE SHOOTING SPARKS UNREST IN THE CITY</strong></a></p><p>Minneapolis Mayor Jacob Frey has been accused of <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/media/jonathan-turley-slams-minneapolis-mayors-reckless-response-ice-shooting" target="_blank" rel="noopener">inflaming tensions following the</a> shooting. He blamed ICE for the woman’s death and told the agency to "<a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/mayor-jacob-frey-tells-ice-get-f-out-minneapolis-rejects-dhs-self-defense-statement" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">get the f--- out</a>" of the city.</p><p>"They’re sowing chaos on our streets — and in this case, quite literally killing people," Frey said on Wednesday.</p><p>The demonstrations come five and a half years after protests and <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/george-floyd-protests-aftermath" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow">riots broke out in Minneapolis</a> following George Floyd’s death in May 2020.</p><p>Minnesota Gov. Tim Walz said that his state’s <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/gov-walz-authorizes-national-guard-staging-following-fatal-ice-shooting-minneapolis" target="_blank" rel="noopener">National Guard is prepared</a> to deploy if necessary.</p>
