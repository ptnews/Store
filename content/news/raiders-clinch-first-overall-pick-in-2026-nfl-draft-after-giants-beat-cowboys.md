New York needed a loss and a Raiders victory to regain the first pick, but the Giants beat the Dallas Cowboys, 34-17, giving the Raiders the selection.</p><p>It is widely assumed that Indiana quarterback Fernando Mendoza will be a Raider. Las Vegas acquired veteran quarterback Geno Smith from the Seattle Seahawks during the offseason, but amid a dreadful season for the Raiders, Smith was a disappointment.
