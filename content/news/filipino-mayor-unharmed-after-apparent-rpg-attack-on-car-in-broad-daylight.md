<p>A <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/elections/gubernatorial" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Filipino mayor</a> escaped unharmed after an apparent rocket-propelled grenade (RPG) attack on his convoy in broad daylight Sunday in the southern province of Maguindanao del Sur, authorities said. </p><p>The Philippine News Agency (PNA), the country’s state-run news outlet, <a href="https://www.pna.gov.ph/articles/1267658" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener"><u>reported that</u></a> Mayor Akmad Ampatuan was inside a bulletproof vehicle when the attack occurred at around 6:30 a.m.</p><p>Video of the incident shows two men stepping out of a white van, one holding what appears to be an RPG and another carrying a gun.</p><p>One of the men then <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/terror/counter-terrorism" target="_blank" rel="noopener">lowers the launcher</a> and aims it down the street.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/us/gunfire-arson-vandalism-tracking-political-violence-america" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>GUNFIRE, ARSON AND VANDALISM: TRACKING POLITICAL VIOLENCE IN AMERICA</strong></a></p><p>He fires the weapon before jumping back into the vehicle, moments before a black SUV turns onto the road and is <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/terror/bombings" target="_blank" rel="noopener">struck by the blast</a>.</p><p>PNA reported that the mayor’s backup vehicle, a pickup, was also hit by gunfire during the attack.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/us/crime/police-and-law-enforcement" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Police and military forces</a> later killed three suspected attackers in a pursuit.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/mexican-mayor-who-took-hard-line-against-drug-gangs-shot-killed-day-dead-event" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>MEXICAN MAYOR WHO TOOK HARD LINE AGAINST DRUG GANGS SHOT AND KILLED AT DAY OF THE DEAD EVENT</strong></a></p><p>The motive for the attack was not immediately known.</p><p>"The mayor is safe," Anwar Kuit Emblawa, the mayor’s executive assistant, told the PNA.</p><p>Two members of Ampatuan’s <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/tech/topics/security" target="_blank" rel="noopener">security detail</a> were injured, though their wounds were not life-threatening.</p><p>The outlet said the mayor has survived two previous assassination attempts in the past five years.</p>
