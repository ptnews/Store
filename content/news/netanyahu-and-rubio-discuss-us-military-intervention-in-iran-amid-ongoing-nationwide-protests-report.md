<p>Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and U.S. Secretary of State <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/marco-rubio" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Marco Rubio</a> discussed the possibility of U.S. intervention in Iran, according to a report.</p><p>The two leaders spoke by phone Saturday as Israel is on "high alert," preparing for the possibility of U.S. military intervention in Iran, according to Reuters, citing multiple Israeli sources.</p><p>The report comes as <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/world-protests" target="_blank" rel="noopener">nationwide anti-regime demonstrations</a> across Iran hit the two-week mark.</p><p>On Saturday, the Iranian regime <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/iran-flips-kill-switch-hide-alleged-crimes-death-toll-rises-amid-protests" target="_blank" rel="noopener">triggered an internet "kill switch"</a> in an apparent effort to conceal alleged abuses by security forces and as protests against it surged nationwide, according to a cybersecurity expert. The blackout reduced internet access to a fraction of normal levels.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/media/keane-warns-iranian-regime-take-trump-dead-serious-protest-killing-threat-amid-ongoing-demonstrations" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>KEANE WARNS IRANIAN REGIME TO TAKE TRUMP 'DEAD SERIOUS' ON PROTEST KILLING THREAT AMID ONGOING DEMONSTRATIONS</strong></a></p><p>On Sunday, Iran’s parliament speaker warned that the U.S. military and Israel would be "<a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/protests-spread-across-iran-regime-threatens-us-forces-legitimate-targets-after-trump-warning" target="_blank" rel="noopener">legitimate targets</a>" if America strikes the Islamic Republic.</p><p>Parliament Speaker Mohammad Bagher Qalibaf issued the threat as lawmakers rushed the dais in the Iranian parliament, shouting, "Death to America!" according to The Associated Press.</p><p>President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/donald-trump" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Donald Trump</a> offered support for the protesters on Saturday, writing on Truth Social that "Iran is looking at FREEDOM, perhaps like never before. The USA stands ready to help!!!"</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/world/iranian-military-leader-threatens-preemptive-attack-after-trump-comments" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>IRANIAN MILITARY LEADER THREATENS PREEMPTIVE ATTACK AFTER TRUMP COMMENTS</strong></a></p><p>At a news conference Friday, Trump said Iran was facing mounting pressure as unrest spreads across the country.</p><p>"Iran’s in big trouble," he said. "It looks to me that the people are taking over certain cities that nobody thought were really possible just a few weeks ago. We’re watching the situation very carefully."</p><p>The president said the U.S. would respond forcefully if the regime resorts to mass violence. </p><p>"We’ll be hitting them very hard where it hurts. And that doesn’t mean boots on the ground, but it means hitting them very, very hard where it hurts," he said.</p><p>Fox News Digital reached out to the <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/politics/foreign-policy/state-department" target="_blank" rel="noopener">State Department</a> and White House for comment.</p><p><i>Fox News Digital's Emma Bussey, Brie Stimson and The Associated Press contributed to this report.</i></p>
