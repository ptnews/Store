<p>President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/donald-trump" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Donald Trump</a> signaled a hardline stance toward Cuba on Sunday, vowing to halt all oil and financial support and urging the island nation to "make a deal" before it is "too late."</p><p>"THERE WILL BE NO MORE OIL OR MONEY GOING TO CUBA - ZERO! I strongly suggest they make a deal, BEFORE IT IS TOO LATE," Trump wrote in a Truth Social post.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxbusiness.com/politics/we-built-venezuelas-oil-industry-trump-vows-us-energy-return-after-maduro-capture" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>‘WE BUILT VENEZUELA’S OIL INDUSTRY:’ TRUMP VOWS US ENERGY RETURN AFTER MADURO CAPTURE</u></strong></a></p><p>Venezuela has long been <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/world/world-regions/cuba" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Cuba’s</a> largest oil supplier, though Trump has successfully secured oil shipments to the U.S. on the heels of former Venezuelan President <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/person/nicolas-maduro" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Nicolas Maduro</a>'s capture by U.S. forces.</p><p>"Cuba lived, for many years, on large amounts of OIL and MONEY from Venezuela," Trump added.</p><p>The warning comes as the Trump administration intensifies its efforts to isolate regional allies of <a href="https://www.foxnews.com/category/topic/venezuelan-political-crisis" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Venezuela</a> following Maduro’s capture.</p><p>Venezuela’s massive oil reserves, <a href="https://www.foxbusiness.com/politics/us-now-control-venezuelas-oil-reserves-largest-world-chart" target="_blank" rel="noopener">the largest in the world</a>, have historically underpinned its influence across the region, particularly through subsidized shipments to allies like Cuba.</p><p><a href="https://www.foxnews.com/politics/venezuela-still-owes-us-energy-companies-billions-trump-calls-new-investment" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong><u>VENEZUELA STILL OWES US ENERGY COMPANIES BILLIONS AS TRUMP CALLS FOR NEW INVESTMENT</u></strong></a></p><p>That leverage is now at the center of Trump’s push to reshape regional energy flows following the collapse of Venezuela’s former government.</p><p>On Friday, Trump hosted a lineup of U.S. and international oil executives representing key sectors of Venezuela’s energy supply chain, including producers, refiners and traders.</p><p>Trump pledged to bring U.S. companies back to the forefront of Venezuelan oil production and exports, according to people familiar with the discussions.</p>
